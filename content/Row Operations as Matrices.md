# Coming Up With Row Operations as Matrices
1. Simply apply the row operation to identity matrix
# Examples in $\mathbb{R}^{3}$
### $\lambda R_{2} \to R_{2}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
1 & 0  & 0\\
0 & \lambda & 0\\
0 & 0 & 1\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
### $R_{2} + \lambda R_{1} \to R_{2}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
1 & 0  & 0\\
\lambda & 1 & 0\\
0 & 0 & 1\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
### $R_{3} \leftrightarrow R_{1}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
0 & 0  & 1\\
0 & 1 & 0\\
1 & 0 & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
