A text editor and IDE software
# Commands
`C` denotes `CTRL`
`M` denotes `ALT`
### `C-f`
Move forwards one word
### `C-b`
Move backwards one word
### `C-n`
Move down one line
### `C-p`
Move up one line
### `C-v`
Move Down one page
### `M-v`
Move up one page
### `C-a`
Move to beginning of line
### `C-e`
Move to end of line
### `M-a`
Move to end of line. [[Iterative Process]]
### `M-e`
Move to end of line. [[Iterative Process]]
