# Example
- With point $(2,4,-1)$
- With normal vector $n = (2,3,4)$
$n = \frac{(2,3,4)}{(A,B,C)}$
$P_{o}= \frac{(2,4,-1)}{(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})}$
Then we get:
- $2 * (x-2) + 3(y-4) + 4(z+1) = 0$
- $\implies 2x - 4 + 3y-12 + 4z+4 = 0$
- $\implies 2x + 3y+ 4z -12 = 0$
We also have intercepts at $(6,0,0), (0,4,0), (0,0,3)$
