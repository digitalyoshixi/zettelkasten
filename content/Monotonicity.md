An expression is in a monotonic [[Context]] if:
- Substituting a subexpression with a [[Binary Strength|Weaker]] expression weakens the whole expression
- Substituting a subexpression with a [[Binary Strength|Stronger]] expression strengthens the whole expression
In contrast to [[Anti Monotonicity]]
