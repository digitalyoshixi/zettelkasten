These are pins that have a wide groove cut around the middle.
![[Spool Pin-20250620134753189.webp|255]]
- Catches on the lip of the cylinder when raising. Might fool lockpicker into thinking the pin is set when it isn't
- A lock can't contain entirely spool pins. Locks with spools must contain atleast one [[Driver Pin]]
- Spool pins may cause [[Counter Rotation]] when pushed upwards
