When two nodes use different [[Point-to-point Communication|Duplex]] standards (full one one end, half on other).
- Causes many re-transmissions
- Can happen in `up/up` [[Cisco IOS Interface Status]]
- Avoided by [[Ethernet Autonegotiation]] for both parties
