A [[Linear Operator]] that changes the spin of a [[Qubit]].
# Definition
The quantum operation $U \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ is
$$U = \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }} \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
1 & 1\\
1 & -1\\
\end{array}\right]$$
Or, $u \ket{0} = \alpha$.
# Properties
- The quantum operation $U \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ is Norm preserving, and thereby [[Preserving Inner Products and Vector Isomorphism|Inner Product Preserving]]
- Therefore, the quantum operation is a [[Unitary Operator|Unitary]] operation $U U^{*} = I$
