# Definition
- Suppose [[Random Variable|RV]] $X,Y$
- Suppose we define two transformations $Z, W$
For one function:
$$\left[\begin{array}{cc}X\\Y\end{array}\right] \implies Z = h(X,Y)$$
For two functions merging into one function:
$$\left[\begin{array}{cc}X\\Y\end{array}\right] \implies \left[\begin{array}{cc}Z\\W\end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cc}h_{1}(X,Y)\\h_{2}(X,Y)\end{array}\right] = h(X,Y)$$
