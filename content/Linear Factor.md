These are factors of a [[Polynomial]] that have a [[Degree]] of $1$.
# Example
$f(x) = (x)^{2}(x-2)$
Note that all factors in the polynomial are linear
# Non-Linear Factor
A non-linear factor is one that cannot be reduced into linear factors.
$f(x)= (2x^{2}+1)(x)$ has non-linear factor $2x^2+1$
