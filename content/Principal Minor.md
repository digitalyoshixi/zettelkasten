# Definition
The derivatives of a given $n \times n$ submatrix of matrix $M$.
# Example
$$M = \left[\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 2 & 3\\
4 & 5 & 6\\
7 & 8 & 9\\
\end{array}\right]$$
Has principal minors:
$$
\det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
1
\end{array}\right]
$$
$$
\det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
1 & 2\\
4 & 5\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
$$
\det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
1 & 2 & 3\\
4 & 5 & 6\\
7 & 8 & 9\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
