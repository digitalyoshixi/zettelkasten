For a given function $f : \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}^{k}$
The derivative $Df(x_{0})$ is the matrix:
$$
Df = \left[ \frac{\partial f_{i}}{\partial x_{j}} \right]
= \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
\frac{\partial f_{1}}{\partial x_{1}} & \dots & \frac{\partial f_{1}}{\partial x_{n}}\\ 
\vdots & \dots & \vdots\\
\frac{\partial f_{k}}{\partial x_{1}} & \dots & \frac{\partial f_{k}}{\partial x_{n}}
\end{array}\right]
$$
