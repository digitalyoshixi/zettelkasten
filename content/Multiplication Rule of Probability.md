# Rule
$$
P(A_{1} \cap  \dots \cap A_{n}) = P(A_{1}) \times P(A_{2} | A_{1}) \times P(A_{3} | A_{2} , A_{1})  \times \dots P(A_{n} | A_{n-1},\dots,A_{1})
$$
Works with any order
### Mutual Events Rule
For mutual events $A_{1},\dots,A_{n}$
Then, $P(A_{1} \cap \dots \cap A_{n}) = P(A_{1}) \times \dots \times P(A_{n})$
