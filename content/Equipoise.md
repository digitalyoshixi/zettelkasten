Equality of distributions of results.
# Theoretical Equipoise
You are in theoretical equipoise if evidence of two treatments are exactly balanced in results.
Example:
- Two drugs, no reason at all to prefer one or the other
Often overwhelmingly fragile, as the first results of a drug dictate the favoring of one or another.
