Suppose that $M = [m_{ij}]$ is a $n \times n$ matrix.
The quadratic form $Q_{M}(h) = h^TMh$
in coordinates:
$$Q_{M}(h_{1}, \dots,h_{n}) = [h_{1} \dots h_{2}] \left[\begin{array}{cc}  
m_{11} & \dots & m_{1n}\\
\vdots & \dots & \vdots\\
m_{n1} & \dots & m_{nn}\\ 
\end{array}\right]
\left[\begin{array}{cc} 
h_{1} \\
\vdots \\
h_{n} \\
\end{array}\right]
= \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{m} m_{ij}h_{i}h_{j}
$$
