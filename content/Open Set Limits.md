# Definition
Suppose $f : D \subset \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}^{k}$ where $D$ is an [[Open Set]]
Suppose $x_{0} \in D$
Then, we say $\lim_{ x \to x_{0} }f(x) = b$ if:
- For every [[Neighbourhood]] $N$ of $b$, there is a [[Neighbourhood]] $U$ of $x_{0}$ s.t $x \in U \implies f(x) \in N$
