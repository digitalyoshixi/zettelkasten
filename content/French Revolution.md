A event in 1788 created during the [[The Enlightenment]].
# Cause
- Upper class royalty living lavish lifestyle
- Lower class working hellish conditions
- French in a poorer state after the [[Seven Years War]]
- Monarchy starts to tax the poor for arbitrary conditions
	- [[Labour Tax]]
- Collection of natural disasters make farm conditions poor
# Events
- Poor start to riot
- [[Estates of Realm]] hold a meeting, unfair reprsentation of the poor
- Poor create a [[National Assembly]] - their own government, locked down by the upper class
- Financial minister that wanted to change things was dismissed
- Revolution creates a national guard, many french guard converts to revolutionary side
- July 14 1789, raiding military hospital to get weaponry
- Revolution raid the Bastille to get weaponry
- National assembly creates [[Declaration of Rights of Man and of the Citizen]]
- Women demanded audience with king in Versaille, king moved to Paris
