A [[First Order Predicate Formula|Predicate Formula]] of interest. 
# Definition
A [[First Order Predicate Formula|Predicate Formula]] is in PNF Form $\Longleftrightarrow$ it has form:
- $Q_{1} x_{1} Q_{2}x_{2} \dots Q_{k}x_{k} E$
Where:
- $k \geq 0$
- Each [[Quantifier]] $Q_{i}$ is either $\exists, \forall$
- Each $x_{i}$ is a variable
- $E$ is quantifier [[Free Predicate Variable]]
