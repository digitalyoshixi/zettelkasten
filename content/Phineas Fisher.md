# Metholodogy
1. Reconnaissance (look at public facing networks, social networks, etc)
2. Gain a foothold (find technology, find [[Zero-Day]])
3. Perform internal reconnaissance (passive network listening, slow network scanning)
4. Gain influence of the network (find credentials, gain access)
5. Total compromise (Install malware)
