- For [[Independent and Identically Distributed Random Variables|IID]] [[Random Variable|RV]] $X_{1},\dots,X_{n}$
- With [[Cumulative Distribution Function|CDF]] $F(x)$
- With [[Portable Document Format|PDF]] $f(x)$
Then,
- The distribution of maximum $X_{(n)}$ is $F_{(n)} (x) = [F(x)]^{n}$
- The [[Probability Density Function|PDF]] $f_{(n)}(x) = n [F(x)]^{n-1} f(x)$
