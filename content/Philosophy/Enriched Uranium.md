# Thresholds
### 3-6% Enriched
Used for civilian power
### 20% Enriched
Used for research reactors and [[Medical Isotope]]
### 60% Enriched
Able to create crude nuclear weapons
Most of the work required to enrich to 90% has been done.
### 90% Enriched
Weapons-grade uranium
