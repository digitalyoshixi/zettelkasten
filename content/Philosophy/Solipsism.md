Your mind is the only thing that exists.
The external world is a by-product of the images created in your mind.
# Radical View
Nobody really believes in this and there is not good reason to favour this
# Views
### Metaphysical Solipsism
The view that only one's own mind exist
### Epistemic Solipsism
The view that one can know things only about ones own mind
