An act to modernize legislative provisions as regards to protection of personal information.

Requires existing organizations using AI to amend their existing privacy program.
- Make sure that privacy policies can be understood by people providing consent - people under 15 cannot consent

Law can be enforced and organizations can be brought to court.
# Privacy Requirement
- A new framework for giving consent
	- Tell people how data being used
	- Tell people how data is collected
	- Retention limits for data
	- Designated compliance officer
