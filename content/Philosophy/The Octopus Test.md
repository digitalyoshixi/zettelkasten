# Thought Experiment
- Imagine two humans communicate another through cables across two islands
- Suppose hyper-intelligent octopus eavesdrops on communication.
- The octopus cannot see, but can pick up on patterns in communcations - never seen tree, but knows patterns of discourse around tree
- Person invents something new - lets say catapult, the octopus cannot represent catapult in their mind.
