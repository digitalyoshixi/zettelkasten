A notion of fairness that is decided upon by law.
Requires:
- Benefit be available to everyone
- Decisions be decided by [[Merit]]
# Formal Definition
$F(\cdot|c,e)$ is the cumulative distribution of utility at fixed effort $e$ and circumstance $c$ where:
- $e$ is the effort one puts into life
- $c$ is the circumstances beyond ones control
