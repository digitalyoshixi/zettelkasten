Believing that:
- The external world does not exist

# Self-Affirming Past
To prove the past, you must assume that a past exists. This is self-reinforcing and is absurd.
# Phenomena
- You do not directly observe phenomena
- All notions of causation are scientific theories that cannot be validated
- Our senses that we use to observe the world cannot be trusted
