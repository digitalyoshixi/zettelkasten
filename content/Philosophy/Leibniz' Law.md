If $x = y$ then, $x$ and $y$ have exactly the same properties.
The contrapositive is:
- $x$ and $y$ dont have the same properties $\implies x \neq y$
It is easy to accept Leibniz's law but difficult to accept its converse which is [[Identity of Indiscernables]].
