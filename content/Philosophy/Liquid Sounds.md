These are sounds that depend on the length of the tongue like:
- `[l]`
- `[r]`
# Types
### Lateral Approximant (`[l]`)
These are sounds that occur when the sides of the tongue are lowered
### Retroflex Approximant (`[r]`)
These are sounds that happen when the tongue is curled back behind the alveolar ridge
