Who is responsible when something goes wrong.
# Reduction Of Liability
- If you have a caution sign, then the person who trips has the liability
- If there is no sign, then the business is liable
# Personal Liability
You as the business owner has liability over business actions. If your business can't pay it off, then you must pay it off
# Unlimited Liability
The business has unlimited liability. The limits are established by the court.
### Example
- Your business causes a flood in the person's house
- The customer to repair damages, requires you to pay several thousand for damages
# Joint/Several Liability
Each partner is responsible for the debts and liabilities of the other partner.
You are responsible for your partner's actions.
# Limited Liability
Only liable to a certain degree
