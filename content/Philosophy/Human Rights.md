These are the given permissions to be given to all humans.
A human can choose to absolve themselves of certain rights, so long as it does not break another human right:
- A human can revoke their right to life
- A human cannot revoke their freedom to sell themselves as a slave
# Rights
### Fundamental Freedoms
- Freedom of [[Conscience]] and [[Religion]]
- Freedom of thought, belief, opinion and expression, including freedom of the press and other media of communication
- Freedom of peaceful assembly
- Freedom of association
### Democratic Rights
- Right to vote in election of members in house of commons or legislative assembly
### Mobility Rights
- Right for every citizen to be able to enter, remain or leave [[Canada]]
- Right to take residence in any province
- Right to pursue gaining of livelihood in any province
### Life, Liberty, Security of person
- Right to [[Life]]
- Right to [[Liberty]]
- Right to personal security
### Search or Seizure
- Those who act on behalf of converment must carry duties in a fair and resonably way
### Detention or Imprisonment
- Everyone has right to not be arbitrarily detained or improsoned
### Arrest or Detention
- Everyone has right to:
	- Be informed of reason for arrest
	- Retain or instruct counsel to be informed of that right
	- Have validity of detention determined by [[Habeas Corpus]] and be released if detention is not lawful
