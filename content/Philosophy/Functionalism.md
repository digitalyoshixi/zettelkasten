*Phenomenal properties are identical to functional properties*
A [[School of Thought]] in [[Philosophy of Mind]].
What makes something a [[Mental State]] depends its function rather than its biological constitution.
The mind adopts [[Substrate Independence]] meaning that a mind could just be a [[Software Brain]] that requires the correct hardware to support it.

# Modern Day
Functionalism is now the most prominent view
