If $A$ causes $B$, then:
- $A$ occurs before $B$ 
- $A$ and $B$ are wholly distinct
# Examples
- You skipping readings caused you to fail the exam
# Causality Haziness
We don't know why one action can cause another.
This is a widespread debate in [[Physics]], [[Mathematics]] and [[Philosophy]].
