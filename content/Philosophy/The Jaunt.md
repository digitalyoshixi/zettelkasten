*Longer than you think!*
A teleportation method written by [[Stephen King]] wherein consciousness operates on a different time-scale than the body.
# Effects
If a person goes through the jaunt while conscious, they will experience an eternity within a empty void with no inputs to their brain.
The mind will auto-cannibalize itself and start destroying itself when left with nothing to do.
# Reflections
- If one is trapped in the jaunt forever, they may create a [[Boltzmann Brain]], making their own universe
- Longer than you think could signify that there are only finite amount of time you can have to think about everything, so then once you think of everything, there is nothing left to do
- The fact that ricky and foggia are able to return and speak english, and also remember the previous conversations before jaunting implies that they retain their sanity after jaunting. The switch back to reality might be the problem. Too much sensory input is given back to you at once after a eternity of nothingness, that the mind caves in upon itself.
