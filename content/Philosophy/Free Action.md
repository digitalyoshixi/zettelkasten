The ability to act on one's [[Free Will]].
- A paralyzed person does not have free action
# Philosophers
- [[Strawson]] believes an action is free if the action wasn't [[Determinism|Causally Determined]]
- [[Ayer]] believes an action is free if it wasnt performed under [[Constraint]]
### Free Action 1 ([[Hard Determinism]] Version)
To have been able to have chosen to do otherwise, an agent's action must have not been determined
### Free Action 2 ([[Compatibilism]] Version)
To have been able to have chosen to do otherwise, an agent's action must have been without [[Constraint|Constraints]]
