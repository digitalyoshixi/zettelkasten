The degree/[[Probability]] in which you believe in a [[Proposition]].

The credence you have that X will happen + the credence you have that X wont happen must sum up to 1.

Additionally, these weights must be [[Justification|Justified]] somehow.
Credence should also change when given new information.
# Degrees
- 0 credence is certainty of falsehood
	- [[Contradiction]] propositions
	- [[Absurdism]] propositons
- 1 credence is certainty of truth
	- [[Tautology]] propositions
