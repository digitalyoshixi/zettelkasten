A board that allows or disallows research.
Comprised of scientists and community members.
Requires researchers to bundle a package of research goals which will be approved or disapproved.
# Considerations
1. Is the study scientifically sound? Will produce meaningful results?
2. Is research ethically sound? Will be conducted that meets ethical requirements. Considers:
	1. [[Risk Benefit Assessment]]
	2. [[Ethical Recruitment and Consent]]
	3. Vulnerable populations (children, homeless, prison, etc)
	4. [[Privacy]] and data handling
	5. Compensation
	6. Dissemination and transparency
