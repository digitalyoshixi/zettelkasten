A [[Assimilation]] process wherein a stop becomes more pronounced due to the vowels surrounding it.
Caused by the tongue *tapping* against the [[Alveolar Ridge]].
Specific for [[English]] wherein `/t/` or `/d/` when between [[Vowel|Vowels]] can become a tap `[ɾ]`.
