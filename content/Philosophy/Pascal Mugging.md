A argument against [[Utilitarianism]] where a robber promises a very unlikely high reward for giving them a wallet.
![[Drawing 2025-01-28 11.38.52.excalidraw]]
The potential of the reward is very low, but the reward value outweighs not going for the reward.

It is a twist on [[Pascal's Wager]] that does not involve infinite value
