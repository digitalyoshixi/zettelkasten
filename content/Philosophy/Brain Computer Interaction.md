This is a subset of [[Human Computer Interaction|HCI]] that uses brains electrical signals like:
- [[Electroencephalography|EEG]]
- [[Electrooculography|EOG]]
- [[Electromyography|EMG]]
Allows interactions of:
- Brain-to-interface
- Brain-to-[[Prostetics]]
- Brain-to-text & Image
- Brain-to-brain
