A modified version of [[Formal Equality of Opportunity]] that takes into account willingness of people to want opportunities.
Created by [[John Rawls]]

Fairness of events are given to:
- Those that it applies to (Are able to take advantage of it. Wealthy course $\implies$ [[Sample Space]] is rich people)
- Those that have [[Merit]]
# Formal Definition
$F(\cdot|c,e) = F(\cdot|c', e)$ is the cumulative distribution of utility at fixed effort $e$ and circumstance $c$. Where:
- $e$ is the effort one puts into life
- $c$ is the circumstances beyond ones control
In other words, cumulative utility depends only on $e$
