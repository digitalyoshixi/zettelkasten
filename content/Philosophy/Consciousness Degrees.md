# Ungradability of [[Phenomenal Character]]
It is unreasonable for us to grade one phenomenal character above another.
### Objective
```standardform
P1: Subjectivity is gradable
P2: Subjectivity is a dimension of phenomenal character
-- Conj --
C: So, phenomenal character must be gradable
```
##### Reponse
It is not always true that a sub-set of a higher-dimensional thing implies that the higher-dimensional property is gradable.
- Color & brightness. Brightness is gradable, color is not. Brightness is a dimension of color
- Personality & Enthusiasm. Enthusiasm is gradable, personality is not. Enthusiasm is a dimension of color
