An argument in which the premises are [[Contradiction|Contradictory]].
# Example
1. If you know you have hands then you aren't a [[Brain in a Vat]]
2. You don't know that you aren't a [[Brain in a Vat]]
3. You know that you have hands
Premise 2 and 3 are contradictory.
