Two distinct stars that appear to be different, but turn out to be the same. A reply to [[Knowledge Argument for Qualia|Marys Room]] stating that new knowledge could just be old knowledge under a new guise.
# Hesperus (Morning Star)
The last star visible when morning comes
# Phosphorus (Evening Star)
The first star visible when evening comes
