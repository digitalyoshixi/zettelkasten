A book that stipulates over half the jobs in america are bullshit jobs:
1. Flunkies: those who make their supervisors feel important (receptionists, administrative assistance, door attendants, store greeters)
2. Goons: Those who harm or deceive on behalf of their employers (lobbyists, corporate lawyers, telemarketers, public relations)
3. Duct tapers: Those who fix temporary problems that could be fixed permanently (Programmers repairing shoddy code, airline desk staff that calm passengers with lost luggage)

5. Taskmasters: Those who create extra work for those who dont need it (Middle management, leadership professionals)
