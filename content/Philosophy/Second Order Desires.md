`A wants to want to X`
Desires about desires invoked by self-reflection.
They are higher-order than [[First Order Desires]].
A human may want X, without ever requiring to experience X.
- Want to know how it feels to be addicted, but not actually want to be addicted
- Wants to want [[Discipline]], so that they can be a better person
