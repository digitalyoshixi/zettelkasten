# Argument
- [[Gopher Snake]] often mimic [[Rattlesnake]], giving us a reason to doubt them when they act as a rattlesnake
- AI systems mimic a human in instances where they are evaluated, thus we have a reason to doubt them when they mimic our behaviors
# Formal Definition
Suppose observable feature $F_{1}$ provide good evidence for feature $F_{2}$.
If you learn that a system is designed to $mimic(F_{1})$, then this evidential reason is undercut.
