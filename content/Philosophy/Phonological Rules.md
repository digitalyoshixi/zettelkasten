# [[Nasalization]] rule
![[Phonological Rules-20250604184659186.webp]]
Any vowel that comes before a [[Nasal Sounds|Nasal Symbol]] must have a tilde.
# Aspiration Rule for `/p/`
`/p/` is [[Aspiration|Aspirated]] at the beginning of a stressed syllable immediately before a vowel.
![[Phonological Rules-20250604190026448.webp]]
# Voiceless Stop Aspiration Rule
Voiceless stops before a vowel become aspirated when the syllable is stressed.
