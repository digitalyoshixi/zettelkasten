# Thought Experiment
- Patient has cereberal paulsy, and stops responding after a tumor grows
- Treatment of tumor can return them to a baseline condition, but has some risks of infection
- Should we do the treatment?
# Ideas
- Tumor removal is not guaranteed to remove the tumor, and so it can possibly regrow
- Justice must be able to be willing to give the same treatment to an able-bodied person aswell
