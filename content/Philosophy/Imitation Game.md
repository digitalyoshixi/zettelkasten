A game used during [[Turing Test]] with a [[Turing Von Neumann Architecture]] computer
# Game
1. We have the first game that has:
	1. One man, one woman, one interrogator
	2. Interrogator must determine who is the man and who is the woman
	3. The man tries to lie and pretend he is a woman
	4. The woman tries to be truthful
2. We have the second game that has:
	1. One AI, one woman, one interrogator
	2. Interrogator must determine who is the AI and who is the woman
	3. The AI tries to lie and pretend he is a woman
	4. The woman tries to be truthful
3. If the AI performs just as well as the man, then the AI has passed the turing test
