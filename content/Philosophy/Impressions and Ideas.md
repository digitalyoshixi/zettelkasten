Every [[Idea]] we know is given first by an [[Impression]].
*Everything you think about must be seen first, you cannot think about things never seen*.

# Example
- You must have seen the color red to have the idea of red in your mind ([[Simple Idea]])
- Anything not previously seen can be constructed from segments of things already seen ([[Complex Idea]])
