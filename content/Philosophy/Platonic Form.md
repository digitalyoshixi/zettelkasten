A theory in [[Metaphysics]] coined by [[Plato]] to suggest that the only real things (and the only things that can provide [[Knowledge]]) are idealized representations of what we see exist.
# Form
A form is a blueprint of what should be.
- A school form is the ideal school
# Capturing Form
Every individual has a different belief about what a concept is. These are just different views of the same form.
The real form is created by the collection of all these views.
