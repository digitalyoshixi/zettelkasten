A thought experiment involving [[Identity]]. Specifically, the debate between whether [[Continuity of Form]] or [[Continuity of Matter]] defines an entity.
# Thought Experiment
1. You have Theseus' ship
2. You replace one plank at a time until the ship no longer has any of its original wood
3. At what point does this ship no longer Theseus' ship
