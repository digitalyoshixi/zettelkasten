A counterargument to [[Pascal's Wager]] that says it is illogical to assume that the one god is the god that values [[Piety]] and renounces [[Philosophy/Vice]].
There could be a god that:
- Values [[Philosophy/Vice]]
- Values [[Vegetables]]
It could be that whatever wagers for god is something arbitrary like drinking beer. This is unlikely, but not impossible.
