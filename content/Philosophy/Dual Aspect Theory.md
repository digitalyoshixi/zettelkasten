The belief that mental experiences are separate from [[Physics|Physical]] experiences but that the [[Brain]] can provide them both. They are both aspects of the [[Brain]].
The brain is the host for:
- [[Physics|Physical]] Chemical and electrical signals
- [[Phenomenology|Phenomenal]] Mental processes
# Example
Biting into a chocolate bar produces:
- Chemical and electrical changes in your brain
- Mental experience of the chocolate flavour
# Contrast
- [[Physicalism]]
- [[Dualism]]
