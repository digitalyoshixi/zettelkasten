The law that allows patients to request assistance to end their own life.
# Tracks
- [[MAID Track 1]]
- [[MAID Track 2]]
# Requirements
- Eligible for health services funded by government of [[Canada]]
- 18 or over and capable of making medical decisions
- Have a [[Grievous And Irremediable Condition]]
- Made a voluntary request for MAID
- Given informed consent to MAID
- Informed of other treatment means
# Responses
### Pro
- Not dying will cause more harm
- MAID prevents suffering
- MAID promotes autonomy
### Anti
- Death will cause a lot of harm
- It is wrong to cause harm, and killing is causing harm
- The current legal requirements for MAID are too strict:
	- The terminal illness is arbitrary ([[Terminal Illness Maid Thought Experiment]])
	- Requires self administration. Excludes those with physical disabilities
