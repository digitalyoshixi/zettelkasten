A eternal [[Causality|Causal]] state where one event's change requires that another event to change aswell.
# Definition
- Differences in A entail differences in B
- No change in A properties without a corresponding change in B properties
![[Supervenience-20250204170846626.webp]]
# Examples
- Your GPA supervenes on your grades for individual classes
- Biological properties supervenes on microphysical properties
- Color and shape facts supervene on microphysicical facts
# Difference from Dependence
- Kids depend on parents, Kids do not supervene on parents
