# Contrast
- Belief is [[Binary]]. You either believe or don't believe that P
- Credence is a gradient for your belief in P

# Relation
We want to reduce one down to the other. Philosophers are not sure which can be reduced to the other.
- Believing = Having credence more than 0.5
- Having credence is having the belief about the probability of P
