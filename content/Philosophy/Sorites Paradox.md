A paradox that involves the ambiguity of terms.
In contrast to [[The Ship of Theseus]]
# Paradox
1. You have a heap of sand
2. You remove grains from the heap
3. At what point does the heap cease to be a heap?
# Square Version
1. There are 1000 squares that gradually shift from red to yellow
2. Square 1 is red
3. Square 1000 is yellow
4. Square n+1 is indistinguishable from square n
5. Thus, following [[Induction]], we can say square 1000 is yellow
