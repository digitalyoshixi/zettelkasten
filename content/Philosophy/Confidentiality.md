The requirement or duty to withhold information to others.
- Therapists must keep client sessions confidential
- Doctors must keep checkups confidential

It must be held confidential unless you have the [[Duty to Warn]]. i.e:
- You are required by law to disclose the information
- You are permitted by law to disclose the information
# Example Reporting
- You can report if there is danger to a child
