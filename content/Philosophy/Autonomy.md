The value of self-governance or self-control.
It is the ability for a person to be in charge of their own life.
A doctor receive [[Informed Consent]] for every procedure, or else they are performing assault.
In contrast to [[Paternalism]]
# Tension
In [[Bioethics]], autonomy often requires letting patients make decisions for themselves, which is often a bad idea.
If there is a lack of autonomy, then it is advised to make decisions that allow the patient to live their life with their personal meaning.
