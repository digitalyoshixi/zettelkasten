An [[Ontological]] argument proving the existance of god.
1. God is the greatest being, and no other being greater than god can ever exist
2. Suppose that god does not exist
3. Then, a greater being than god can exist
4. Contradiction, no being can ever be greater than god
5. Thus, god exists

You may also conclude that god is not the greatest being
