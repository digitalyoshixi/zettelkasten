This is the property of two or more entities to be indistinguishable based on their observable outputs.
- Internally, the systems could have different [[Statistical Model|Models]]
# Formal Definition
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Observational_equivalence
Two terms $M,N$ are observationally equivalent $\Longleftrightarrow$ in all contexts, $C[\dots]$, $C[M] =C[N]$ and $C[M]$. $C[N]$ are all valid terms.
