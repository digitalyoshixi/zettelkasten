The different ways that consciousness comes in. Or in other words, the range of variations of consciousness.
# Uni-dimensional
There is only one form of [[Consciousness]] and all living things have the same consciousness.
# Multidimensional
There are multiple forms of [[Consciousness]].
- [[Subjectivity]] is low-dimensional or possibly zero-dimensional.
- [[Phenomenal Character]] is high-dimensional. Possibly infinite
