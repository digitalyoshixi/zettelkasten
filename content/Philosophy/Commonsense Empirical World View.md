The belief that truth presupposes and leads to evolutionary intuitions.
It establishes a link between truth and intuition by saying that intuition is a result of truth.

We only have intuitions if:
1. The intuition is true
2. Truth is linked with [[Evolution|Evolutionary Advantage]]
# Examples
- The truth of a tree existing leads me to see a tree in front of me.
