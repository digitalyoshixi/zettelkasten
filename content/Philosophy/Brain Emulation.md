The practice of creating a [[Brain Image]] and emulating all of the [[Neuron]] and [[Synapse]] via computers.
Has been replicated for a [[Fruit Fly]].
# Process
1. Take brain
2. Slice brain into slices
3. Image each slice
4. Using image classifiers to label slices for neurons, akin, denticle, etc
5. Create a virtual body for the copy, and embed the copied brain into the body
