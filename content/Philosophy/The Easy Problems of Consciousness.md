These are problems about [[Consciousness]] that can be answered with traditional [[Neuroscience]]. They are easy because they only concern functions and abilities.
Problems like:
- What allows our ability to discriminate, categorize, react to environment
- What allows information to be integrated into our cognitive system
- The reportability of mental states
- The ability of a system to access its own internal states
- the focus of attention
- The deliberate control of behavior
- The difference between wakefulness and sleep
