Conditions that prevent decisions. They impact how free a decision is.
If a decisions has conditions that prevent all other decisions, then it prevents [[Free Will]].

Not directly related to [[Causality]].
# Examples
- There is more constraint that determines your stuttering
- There is less constraint that determines you ordering pizza instead of pasta
Theres a sense that you could have done otherwise, but not every action is equally as free.
