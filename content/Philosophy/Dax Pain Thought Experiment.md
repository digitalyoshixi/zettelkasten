# Though Experiment
- Dax Cowart blew up in his car, and was sent to the hospital
- Dax is alive in excrutiating pain
- Doctor's kept Dax alive, believing that the Dax will appreciate his life after recovery
- Dax recovers and is blind, gets a law degree and a wife
- Dax still vouches for [[Patient Rights]] - the ability to die
# Responses
### Pro Doctor (Pro Survive)
- The belief is that doctor's have the obligation to keep patients alive
- Doctor's assess your best interest
### Pro [[Patient Rights]]
- Patient has a right to life, they can also decline and give up this right
