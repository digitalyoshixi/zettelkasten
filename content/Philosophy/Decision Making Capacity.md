The ability for a person to be able to make decisions in the right kind of way.
- Capacity is assumed for adults.
- [[Mature Minors]] are also able to have capacity.
# Criteria
1. Communicate a choice
2. Show understanding
3. Appreciate the situation
4. Reason about treatment options
