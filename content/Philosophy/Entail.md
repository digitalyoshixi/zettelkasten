$$\models, \vdash$$
A [[Truth Assignment]] that allows a [[Propositional Formula|Formula]] to be true
$\tau \models \mathcal{F}$ if $\tau(\mathcal{F}) = 1$
# Philosophy
It is a stronger form of [[Conditional Statement|Implies]] that fits [[Semantics|Semantic]] arguments better
$P \models Q$ means if $P$ is true, then $Q$ is true
