# Three Consonant Rule
![[Phonotactics-20250609175748623.webp]]
If a [[Onset]] in english contains 3 [[Consonant]]:
- The first segment is always [[Voiceless Consonant]] [[Alveolar Sounds|Alveolar]] [[Fricative]] (`[s]`)
- The second sound is always [[Voiceless Consonant]] [[Tabulate Corals]] (`[p]`, `[t]`, `[k]`)
- The third sound is a [[Liquid Sounds]] or [[Glide]] (`[l]`, `[r]`, `[j]`, `[w]`)
