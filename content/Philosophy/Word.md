The smallest free form of language. It is a word that can mean something by itself.
# Types
- [[Morpheme|Mono-Morphic]]
- [[Complex Word|Poly-Morphic]]
# Structure
1. Words contain a **root** [[Morpheme]] ([[Noun]], [[Verb]], [[Adverb]], [[Adjective]]) that cannot be analyzed into smaller parts
2. Words contain one or more [[Affix|Affixes]]
