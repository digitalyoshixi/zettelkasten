Occurs in people trying to find a solution:
- The effects of [[Late-stage Capitalism]]

They end up finding a dangerous solution to their problem, that takes them down the alt-right or alt-left pipeline.

# Alt Right Radicalization
### Entering the pipeline
##### Soft Sell
- Content creators advertise themselves as moderate or left-leaning
- They advertise a lifestyle, but do not say that it is affiliated with any politics
##### Infiltration
- A thing changes in the fandom that brings political discourse
	- A black protagonist
##### Creator Radicalization
1. A content-creator makes some racist joke
2. The vocal radical minority reacts very positively
3. The content-creator makes more racist jokes
4. The hurt leave
5. The only audience they have left, and that will buy things are the radicals
##### Slippery Slope
- Personal connection > political affiliation
	- Laughing at a deeply racist joke is personal
	- Not laughing is political
- Personal brings people together
- Political drives them apart
##### Affirmative Words
- "You are valuable because you are white"
### Echo Chamber
##### Political-Apolitical
- If the community makes a consensus on an issue, then having a personal opinion on the issue is **apolitical**, as it wont affect the communities opinion
- If the community doesn't have an opinion, and you have one, then you are considered **political**, and then shunned and kicked out
- Nazi communities have a majority of nazis, thus they can thrive
##### Caricatured Enemy
- The left are made fun of, and given caricatures

##### Jargon

##### Validated Fear
- People in the communities reiterate the fear

##### Toxic Recursion
- **People go to these communities to be upset**

	- They go back to these communities for the release
### The Flawed Argument
- Alt-right leaders often rely on bad arguments that are:
	- Repeated like mantras
	- Charismatic
	- Long winded
- They are flawed because reiterating these ideas are difficult
	- Telling a friend why, requires you to have them watch the videos
- Repetition dulls the shock that the argument may have
### Lack of Mission
The alt-right are decentralized.
When they try to formalize missions, like in Charlottesville, it all goes to shit.
Most of the work that is done is through online forums.
As a result you have a lot of people angry that have to sit an boil in their anger.
You have a lot of lone wolves that start acting out, they start shooting schools and bombing places.

- The more guilty you are, the harder it is to quit
- What hes done to the left could be done to him aswell, and if he leaves, he loses the power of his community
