A [[School of Thought]] in the [[Philosophy of Mind]] that attempts to solve the [[The Hard Problem of Consciousness]] by stating that there is a one-way causal relationship that physical states can cause mental states.
# Implications
- Implies that whatever my behavior is, is not caused by [[Consciousness]]
