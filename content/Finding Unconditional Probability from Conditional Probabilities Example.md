# Example
- With $2\%$ chance of people from sample space having disease [[Hepatitis]] ($HB$)
- With a test that provides:
	- $P(+|HB) = 0.96$
	- $P(+|HB') = 0.02$
- Then, we find the $P(+)$
### Soln
- $P(+) = P(+|HB) * P(HB) + P(+|HB') * P(HB')$
- $\implies P(+) = 0.96 * 0.02 + 0.02 * 0.98$
- $\implies P(+) = 0.0388$
