# Corrolary
Every [[Linear Operator]] $T$ in $\mathbb{C}$ is [[Upper Triangular Matrices|Triangularizable]].
# Proof
As $\mathbb{C}$ is always linear factorizable by [[Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic|FTOA]].
by [[Characterization of Triangularizability and Diagonalizability in Terms of the Minimal Polynomial]], $T$ is [[Upper Triangular Matrices|Triangularizable]].
