A [[Math/Set]] $A$ describing a data structure defined recursively with a finite collection of base and inductive cases.
- Base case [[Axiom|Axioms]] that have no premise, universally true $a \in A$
- Inductive cases have premises and are true when their premises are true ($a_{0} \in A \wedge \dots \wedge a_{n} \in A \implies a$) (Like a [[Horn Clause]])
