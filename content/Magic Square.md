A 2x2 matrix in which the sum of all columns and rows equals to the same number.
![[Magic Square-20250203172130057.webp|378]]
- Where the only entries in the matrix are in $\{1,2,\dots,n^{2}\}$
- Where the magic constant that all rows and columns sum to is equal to $M = \frac{n(n^{2}+1)}{2}$
