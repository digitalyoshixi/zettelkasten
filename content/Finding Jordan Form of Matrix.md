# Process
1. Find the [[Eigenvector|Eigenvalues]] of the matrix
2. Note down the [[Algebraic Multiplicity]] for each eigenvalue, this is how big your jordan box will be
3. For each eigenvalue, find the basis vectors for the kernels. This will allow you to find [[Geometric Multiplicity]]. This is the structure of 1's in your jordan box.
