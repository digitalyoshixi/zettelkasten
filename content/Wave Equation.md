# Equation
- For a given string stretched along $x$-axis, the height at position $x$ at time $t$ is given by $u(x,y)$
- If it is disturbed, then it will vibrate according to the wave equation:
$$
c^{2} \frac{\partial^{2} u }{\partial x^{2}} = \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial t^{2}}
$$
With:
- Constant $c$ as the rate of vibrations, usually $c = 1$
# Solutions to Wave Equation
A solution will satisfy $u(x,t) = \sin(x)\sin(ct)$
