# Techniques
### Theory Before Practice

### [[Verbal Checking]]
- "Right???"
- "This is self-explaining isn't it?"
"Right???" is the signal and reminder of the main goal in debugging a program.
Whenever a sentence ends with "Right??" you know that the point made before is important and worth assuming as a [[Fact]]
