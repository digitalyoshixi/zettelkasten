A modified [[Delay Flip-Flop|D Flip-Flop]] that has a [[Exclusive Or|XOR Gate]].
Used to store one bit of information. The $T$ switch flips the currently stored bit.
![[Toggle Flip-Flop-20250507024219599.webp]]
- $T = 0 \to Q = Q$
- $T = 1 \to Q = \overline Q$
