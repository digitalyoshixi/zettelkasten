This is a [[Circuit]] that has a [[Multiplexer|MUX]] to switch between two states:
- Act as a regular [[Counter]]
- Act as a [[Register]] to load the value
It has a load input, counter input, clock and clear to reset the [[Circuit State]] to 0.
