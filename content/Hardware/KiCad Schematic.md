# Schematic Editor
### Red Lines

### [[Operational Amplifier|Op-Amp Symbol]]
![[KiCad Schematic-20250604020230274.webp]]

![[KiCad Schematic-20250604020344408.webp]]
This opens up the symbol menu for all power symbols including:
- [[Voltage at the Drain|VDD]]
- [[Voltage at the Source|VSS]]
- 3V3
