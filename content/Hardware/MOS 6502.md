A small chip that handles 8-bit [[Binary|Words]].
- 8-bit data bus
- 16-bit address bus
- General purpose registers: `X`, `Y`, `A`
	- `X` and `Y` used as [[Index Register]]
	- `A` directly connected to ALU, allowed for arithmetic operations
- 8-bit [[Arithmetic Logic Unit|ALU]]
- 8-bit [[rsp|Stack Pointer]]
- 13 address modes
