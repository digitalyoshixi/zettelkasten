# Setup
- Given $X$ as [[Random Variable|RV]]
- Given [[Probability Distribution]] $P(X \in B) , \forall B \subset \mathbb{R}$
- Given [[Functions of Random Variables|Transformation]] $Y = h(X)$
- $P(Y \in A) = P(X \in h^{-1}[A])$
