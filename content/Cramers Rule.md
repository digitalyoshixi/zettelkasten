A formula to find the solution to $A \overrightarrow{x}= \overrightarrow{b}$
# Theorem
If $A$ is [[Isomorphism|Isomorphic]].
Then, $x_{i} = \frac{\det(A_{i})}{\det(A)}$ where:
- $x_{i}$ is the $i-th$ column of $x$
- $A_{i}$ is the matrix formed by replacing the $i-th$ column of $A$ with $\overrightarrow{b}$
