The little endian breaks their egg on the smaller side. The big endian breaks their egg on the larger side.

It works similarly to an egg. But the fluids are memory instead.


### Little endian

For little endian orders, we store the smallest value in the smallest address. If a value has a super small byte, it would be stored in addresses close to 0x00000001.

  

### Big endian

Inversely to little endian. Big endian stores the values smallest, the most farthest away from 0x00000000 address
