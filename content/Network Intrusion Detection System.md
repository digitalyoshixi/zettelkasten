A [[Intrusion Detection System|IDS]] that detects new traffic on a network for flagging as an intrusion attempt.
# Features
### Traffic Analysis
Finds patterns in common [[Network Messages|Packets]].
Detects unusual signatures, behaviors
### Alerting and Notifications
Can generate alerts or notifications to notify [[Incident Response|IR]]
### Passivity
Cannot actively stop a breach.
[[Network Intrusion Prevention System|NIPS]] can do that.
