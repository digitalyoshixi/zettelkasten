The elementary result of an [[Experiment]].
i.e, outcomes cannot be broken down into simpler results
Denoted by lowercase letters:
- $q$
- $r$
- $s_{1}, \dots, s_{n}$
# Examples
- Rolling a dice, and getting 5, you cannot have a simpler result
