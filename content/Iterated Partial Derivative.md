# Definition
- With $f : \mathbb{R}^{2} \to \mathbb{R}$ being [[Differentiability|Differentiable]]
- With partial derivatives $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} , \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$
- The iterated derivatives include: $\frac{\partial ^{2}f}{\partial y \partial x}, \frac{\partial ^{2}f}{\partial x \partial y}$
