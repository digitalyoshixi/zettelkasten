# Definition
- With $X, Y$ as [[Independent Random Variable]]
- With $X \sim \mathcal{X}_{(m)}^{2}$
- With $Y \sim \mathcal{X}_{(n)}^{2}$
- Then, $\frac{\frac{X}{m}}{\frac{Y}{n}} \sim F$ distribution with degrees of freedom $(m,n)$
# Properties
- $W \sim F(m,n) \implies \frac{1}{W} \sim F(n,m)$
