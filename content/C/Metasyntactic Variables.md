also known as placehold variables.
variables that have abstract meanings. usually some permutation of a 3 letter word examples include:
- foo
- bar
- goo
- baz
- boo
- moo

Etymologically speaking, these words are meaningless in context.
Origin of these words: https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc3092
