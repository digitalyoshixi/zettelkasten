The framework and process for developing designs for users.
Users are not part of the design team, but the designer plays an active part in interacting with the users for:
- Gathering requirements
- Gathering feedback
# Principles
- Early focus on users and tasks
- Empirical measurement 
- Iterative design

### Steps
1. Gather requirements from users
	1. Interview users
	2. Interview focus groups
	3. Survey/Questionaires
	4. Observation (Direct or Indirect)
