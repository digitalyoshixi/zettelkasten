These are people who are able to make medical decisions for you in cases where you are unable to.
# Priorities
1. Appointed SDM (You explicitly write who your SDMs are)
2. Spouse/Partner
3. Child or parent
4. Siblings
5. Other relatives
6. [[Public Guardian]]
# Issues
- The guardian may seek to benefit from the patients condition
- The guardian may not be the best choice
- May be too many guardians and too difficult to seek approval
