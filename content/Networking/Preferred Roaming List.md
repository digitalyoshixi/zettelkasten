If your device cannot send a signal to its cell provider, then it can use the tower of another cell provider at an additional cost.
This alternative cell provider is determined by a priority list called the PRL, which includes:
- Other carrier networks
- Frequency other carrier networks operate at
Helps in optimizing data transfer speeds.
# Baseband / Over the Air Updates
The PRL is updated frequently through Baseband updates that are sent over [[Cellular Data]] or [[WI-FI|WIFI]]. 
This updates the locations of [[Cell Tower|Cell Towers]]
