Metal poles that can transmit and receive signals.
Fixed directional antennas can increase [[WI-FI|WIFI]] signal strength.
Ensure that the antenna points directly towards another antenna to ensure strong signal transmission.
Government has regulations with the type of antenna used, so ensure you have a permit.
Antenna must:
- Not face any power source
- Be protected against lightning
