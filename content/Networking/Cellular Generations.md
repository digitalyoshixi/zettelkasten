# Cellular Generations
### 1G
- Entirely analog
- Cannot carry data
### 2G
- Allows digital signal
- Can encrypt messages
- Can use more frequencies
##### 2.5G
Implementations include:
- GPRS
- EDGE
### 3G 
- 10 Mbps Download
Implementations Include:
- EV-DO
### 4G
- 1 Gbps Download
- 100 Mbps Upload
Implementations Include:
- [[Long Term Evolution|LTE]]
### 5G
- 2.5 Gbps Download
- 1.25 Gbps Upload
