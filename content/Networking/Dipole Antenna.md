Increasing the size of these antenna's increases the gain, thereby increasing the coverage range of the [[Access Point|WAP]].
Increasing the power given to these antennas will also increase gain.
# Regulation
Some antenna sizes are illegal as they would cover too much space.
You must get a license to use larger antennae or use special equipment to cover that extra ground.
