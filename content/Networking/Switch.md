Common point of connection to create a [[Local Area Network|LAN]].
Unlike [[Hub|Hubs]], Switches use an [[Application Specific Integrated Circuit]] to route traffic only to the target device.
# Forwarding
1. Receive packet
2. Read destination MAC address
3. Forward to destination using its [[Application Specific Integrated Circuit|ASIC]]

Most consumer switches have 4-8 ports.
Enterprise switches have 32 ports.
