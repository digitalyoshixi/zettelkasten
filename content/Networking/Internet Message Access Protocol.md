Protocol for handling incoming e-mail. IMAP4 improves on [[Post Office Protocol 3|POP3]] by allowing for mail-server connections, diaries, calendars and folders.
Commonly used in corporate environments on mobile devices.
Conventionally seen on port `tcp/143`.
Secure port is TCP `tcp/993`
[[Apple]] does not endorse IMAP4
