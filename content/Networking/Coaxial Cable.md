# RG Ratings
The resistance ratings of cables measured in Ohms $\Omega$
### RG-59
- 75-ohm resistance
- Thinner cable
- Good for short distances
- Used for CCTV and analog video
### RG-6
- 75-ohm resistance
- Good for long distances
- Used for cable TV and modems
### RG-6QS
- Quad shield resistance
