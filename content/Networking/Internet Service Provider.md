Organization with several servers responsible for routing packets across great distances to establish [[The Internet]]
# Wireless ISP
A ISP that replaces cable with either:
- Mesh [[WI-FI|IEE 802.11]]
- [[Cellular Generations|5G]] hotspot
- Proprietary wireless technology
Can reach bandwidth of up to 1 Gbps.
