# Ethernet Flavours
### 10BASE-T
![[Ethernet Flavours-20240731180145901.webp|428]]
- Data is transferred at 10Mbps
- Pins 4,5,7 and 8 not used
- Wires are [[Point-to-point Communication|Simplex]]
### 100BASE-T (Fast Ethernet)
![[Ethernet Flavours-20240731180145901.webp|428]]
- Data is transferred at 100Mbps
- Pins 4,5,7 and 8 not used
- Wires are [[Point-to-point Communication|Simplex]]
### 1000BASE-T (Gigabit Ethernet)
![[Ethernet Flavours-20240731180014133.webp|406]]
- Data transferred at 1000Mbs or 1Gbs
- All pins are used
- Wire pairs would be [[Point-to-point Communication|Full Duplex]]
### 10GBASE-T 
Data transferred at 10Gbs
