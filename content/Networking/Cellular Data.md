A technology to create [[Wireless Wide Area Network|WWANs]] through radio frequencies by cell towers.
# Data Roaming
![[Cellular Data-20240724042256629.webp|414]]
In order to connect to sites, your phone signals will jump from cellular tower to cellular tower.
Every jump costs a bit of money, but these are relatively inexpensive within country.
International roaming will be very costly
