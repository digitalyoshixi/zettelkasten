Note that [[Linear Maps Determined by Their Actions on A Basis]] so, calculate:
$I(v_{1}) = v_{1} = 1 *v_{1} + 0 *v_{2}$
$I(v_{2}) = v_{2} = 0 *v_{1} + 1 *v_{2}$
This gives a matrix:
$$
[T]_{\alpha}^{\alpha} = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{array}\right]
$$
Note that $I(v_{1})$ occupies the first **column** of the matrix
Note that $I(v_{2})$ occupies the second **column** of the matrix
