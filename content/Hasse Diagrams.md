You can represent a [[Order|Finite Ordered Set]]/[[Partial Order|Porder]] as a [[Programming/Graph|Graph]] in $\mathbb{R}^{2}$ with:
- $P$ as the finite ordered set
- Nodes as the elements of $P$, placement of point reflects its 'magnitude' of the overall set
- Edges as relations between elements of $P$
