# Classifications
### Acessibility
- Internal : Attackers from within the organization taking advantage of familiarity with sytems and networks ([[Insider Threats]])
- External: Attackers from outside the organization that often use [[Social Engineering]] to access resources ([[Organized Crime]])
### Funding
- Well-Resourced : Threat actors that have access to substantial resources ([[Nation State]], [[Advanced Persistent Threat|APT]])
- Limited resources : Small-scale cybercriminals that operate with limited resources ([[Hacktivist]], [[Unskilled Attacker|Script Kiddie]])
### Sophistication
- High sophistication: Actors posses technical skills of various attack vectors ([[Nation State]], [[Advanced Persistent Threat|APT]], [[Organized Crime]])
- Less sophisticated: Attackers with little skill ([[Unskilled Attacker|Script Kiddie]])
