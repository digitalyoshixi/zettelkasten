# Querty Mapping Convention
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc} \\
1 & 2 & 3 & C\\
4 & 5 & 6 & D\\
7 & 8 & 9 & E\\
A & 0 & B & F\\
\end{array}\right]
\to
\left[\begin{array}{cc} 
1 & 2 & 3 & 4\\
Q & W & E & R\\
A & S & D & F\\
Z & X & C & V\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
