A region $D \subset \mathbb{R}^{2}$ where every horizontal or vertical line intersects $D$ in a line segment
# Simple Region
A region $R \subset \mathbb{R}^{2}$ is simple if it is x-simple and y-simple.
# Intuition
There is a pair of curves bounding x and a pair of curves bounding y
