A series $\sum a_{n}$ is the sum of terms in a [[Sequence]]
where $a_{n}$ is the general term of  for each $n \in \mathbb{N}$
# Formal Sum
$a_{1} + a_{2} + \dots = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}a_{n}$
# Partial Sum
$S_{n} = a_{1} + \dots + a_{n}$
Is the n-th partial sum of $\sum a_{n}$
