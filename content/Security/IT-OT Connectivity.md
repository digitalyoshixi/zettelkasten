The exposure of [[Operational Technology|OT]] to the internet via [[Internet of Things|IoT]] devices.
- Increases efficiency, visibility, integration
- Exposes more vulnerabilities

Originally, most [[Operational Technology|OT]] systems were [[Flat Network]]
# Contrast
### IT
- More focused on [[Confidentiality Integrity Availability Triad|CIA Triad]]
- Impact is data loss or downtime
- CVE exploitable age is average 250 days
### OT
- More focused on [[High Availability|Availability]] and safety
- Impact is physical harm and downtime
- CVE exploitable age usually 6-10 years
- Legacy systems
