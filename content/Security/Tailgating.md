Following an authorized employee into an unauthorized area.
Piggybacking is when the employee allows you to tailgate
Can be performed if the social engineer creates a situation where they appear to have access, but would require assistance to enter an area like:
- Social engineer is disabled and cant move up stairs
- Social engineer is pregnant and cant go long distances
- Social engineer is holding heavy objects so they cant open doors
