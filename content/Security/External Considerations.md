External entities that impact an organizaton.
# List
- Regulatory bodies : Enact laws and regulations we must follow
- Legal : Enforces contracts, [[Intellectual Property|IP]], liability and litigation
- Industry : Pressure to keep up with current market (Example is [[Dietary Supplement Health and Education Act]])
- Local/Region : Consider demands and regulations of current region ([[California Consumer Privacy Act|CCPA]] only applies for california)
- National: Consider demands for the nation ([[Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act|HIPPA]] is for the entire [[USA]])
- Global: Consider global standards ([[Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard|PCI DSS]] is globally applied)
