A [[Circuit Characterization]] technique that involves using [[Shunt Resistor|Shunt Resistors]] and [[Current Probes]] to measure the current at different points of a circuit to visualize the [[Circuit Trace]].
![[Circuit Characterization from Current Usage-20250619022218333.webp|459]]
# Ideas
- Look for common patterns like repeating currents (indicates loops)
- Look for dips or rises (pulling more or less power)
