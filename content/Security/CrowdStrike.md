A cybersecurity firm that specializes in [[Endpoint Detection and Response|EDR]] software.
# 2024 Crowdstrike Bug
A bad piece of code was pushed to crowdstrike's EDR.
Since the EDR operated at [[Protection Ring|Kernel Level]], every software crash would also lead to a OS crash.
The dependence on security outsourcing to crowdstrike EDR led many airports, banks and hotels to be inoperable until a technician went and disabled the software from [[Windows Safe Mode]]
