For the discrete logarithm 
$$Y=g^x\mod p$$
The possible values of $g$ include all prime numbers < p that result in $g^x \mod p$ being cyclic.
You can find all g values on this site: https://asecuritysite.com/elgamal/pickg
Commonly, Diffie Hellman exchanges use the smallest possible g value.
