When a product stops being created. But it may still be supported.
EOL products will eventually become EOSL, so it is a good indicator to start switching products.
# End Of Service Life (EOSL)
When a service for a product stops being supported.
There will be no further patches or updates.
Apple products usually have support for 3-5 years.
Certain organizations may provide premium-cost support for these EOSL products.
