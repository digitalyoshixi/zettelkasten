A fundamental framework for security.
![[Confidentiality Integrity Availability Triad-20250709024420770.webp|268]]
# Confidentiality
Information should be hidden from unauthorized individuals or systems.
- [[Cryptography|Encryption]]
- [[Access Control]]
- [[Multifactor Authentication|MFA]]
# Integrity
Messages should not be modified without detection. They should remain the same.
- Message [[Hashing]]
- [[Digital Signature]]
- [[Digital Certificate]]
- [[Non-Repudiation]]
# Availability
Systems and networks must be up and running
- Redundancy
- [[Fault Tolerance]]
- Patching to ensure stability
