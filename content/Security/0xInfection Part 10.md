### Part 10: Registers


EAX can be subdivided to hold different lengths.

EAX = 32bit

AX = 16bit

AL = 8bit

AH = 8bit-15bit

ESI, EDI, EBP and ESP can also be referenced by their 16bit equivalent SI, DI, BP and SP
