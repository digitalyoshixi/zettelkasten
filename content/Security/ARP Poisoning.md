A [[Man-In-The-Middle|MITM]] setup attack that has a third party device [[ARP Spoofing|ARP Spoof]] response to change the ARP cache of a target device.
![[ARP Poisoning-20240821183403012.webp]]
The attacker will then do the same attack to the router, thereby allowing the attacker device to sit between the traffic of the two devices.
