The entry point for all emails. Acts like a filter.
Can:
- Block SPAM with [[Sender Policy Framework|SPF]], [[Domain Keys Identification Mail|DKIM]] and [[Domain-based Message Authentication, Reporting and Conformance|DMARC]]
- Use [[Data Loss Prevention|DLP]] to prevent [[Personally Identifiable Information|PII]] from being sent out
