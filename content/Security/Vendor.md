A organization that provides services, and shares sensitive information between their clients.
# Risks
Relying on vendors can lead to [[Supply Chain Vulnerability|Supply Chain Attacks]]
- Data breaches
- Additional security vulnerabilities (e.g. Backdoors)
- [[Security Compliance]] challenges (when vendor does not comply)
- Operational disruption (Downtime caused by vendor)
