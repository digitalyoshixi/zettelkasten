The process of collection and centralization of all system logs for future analysis.
Could be collected from:
- [[Firewall]]
- [[Foreground Processes|Application]]
- [[Endpoint]]
- [[Operating System|OS]]
- [[Intrusion Detection System|IDS]]/[[Intrusion Prevention System|IPS]]
- [[Networking|Network]]
- [[Domain Name Server|DNS]]
- [[Web Server]]
Sent to [[Security Information and Event Management|SIEM]] systems.
