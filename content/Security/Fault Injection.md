- [[One Time Programmable Memory|OTP]] Overwritting

- [[Secure Boot]] bypass

- [[Encrypted Flash]] bypass
- Setting up [[Joint Test Action Group|JTAG]]/[[Serial Wire Debug|SWD]] support
