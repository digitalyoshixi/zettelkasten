Malware designed to grant an attacker remote access to your system so they can use your device's resources along with other infected devices to:
- Mine [[Cryptocurrency]]
- Launch [[Distributed Denial of Service|DDoS]] attacks
- Send [[Spam]]
Devices part of a botnet are called zombies. In fixing a botnet, you target these zombie programs.
