This is a number representing a key used for:
- Decrypting data sent to you that was previously encrypted with your [[Public Key]].
- Digitally signing messages
Never shared, and often kept within [[Hardware Security Module|HSM]]
Used to generate [[Public Key]]
- Saved within files like [[Personal Information Exchange Standard Syntax|P12]] or [[Personal Information Exchange|PFX]]
