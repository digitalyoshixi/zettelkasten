A blunt and common [[Fault Injection|Glitching]] circuit.
Involves shorting a [[Metal–Oxide–Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor|MOSFET]] to ground very quickly, dropping the voltage of the pin it is connected to, then bringing it back up quickly after.
