A management interface for:
- Company devices
- [[Bring Your Own Device|BYOD]] devices 
that gives the company access to all configurations of these devices.

A MDM server may be responsible for:
- Infrastructure changes
- Installing a company app on devices
- Pushing remote updates or policy changes
- Employing [[Mobile Application Management|MAM]]
- Using [[Profile Security Requirement]] [[eXtensive Markup Language|XML]]
- Automatic backups
- Blocking [[Android Rooting|Rooted]] or [[Sideloading|Sideloaded]] devices
- Paritioning storage for a personal drive and a company drive (so that company cant have access to your personal files)
