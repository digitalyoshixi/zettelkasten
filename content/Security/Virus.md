A malicious executable that must embed itself in legitimate software to run.
Its goals are to:
- Make copies of itself within other programs or within the [[Partitioning|Boot Sector]]
- Delete or modify data on your system
Viruses only replicate to other drives, and cannot spread via network.
Viruses cannot be controlled remotely.
A virus cannot run if its host is killed.
# Boot Sector Occupation
Most viruses are found in the boot sector, so that they load every time you start your computer.
