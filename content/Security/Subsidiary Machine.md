# Definition
- For set of machines $\mu_{1}, \dots, \mu_{n}$ where $\mu_{i} = (ID_{i}, c_{i}, \overline \mu_{i})$
- $\mu_{i}$ is a caller of $\mu_{j}$ if its a [[Subroutine Machine]] of $\mu_{j}$ or another subsidiary of $\mu_{j}$
