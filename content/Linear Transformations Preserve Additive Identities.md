If $T : V \to W$ is a linear transformation, then $T(0v) = 0w$
# Proof
In vector space we have $\hat{0} = \hat{0} \overrightarrow{z}$ for all $\overrightarrow{z}$
Suppose $T : V \to W$ is a [[Linear Transform]]
$T(\hat{0}\overrightarrow{v}) = T(\hat{0} \boxdot \overrightarrow{v})$
