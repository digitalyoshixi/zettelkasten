# Complement
Let $M = (Q, \Sigma, \delta, s, F)$ be a [[Deterministic Finite Automaton|DFSA]]
A complement FSA is $\overline M = (\overline Q, \Sigma, \overline \delta, \overline s, \overline F$ s.t $\mathcal{L}(\overline M) = \mathcal{L}(M)$
# Intuition
Make all accepting states non-accepting states and make all non-accepting states accepting states.
