Given $f_{X,Y}$ as the [[Joint Probability Density Function|Joint PDF]], the the [[Probability Density Function|Marginal PDFs]] are given by:
- $f_{X}(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_{X,Y}(x,y)dy$
- $f_{Y}(y) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_{X,Y}(x,y)dx$
