# Rights
Everybody has 4 rights:
1. The [[Human Rights]] (right to life, etc)
2. Dependency-rights (only for children, right to food, shelter, etc)
3. Near-adulthood rights (right to religion, etc)
4. Rights-in-trust (rights granted to children when they reach adulthood. right to reproduce, etc)
# Right to an Open Future
All children have these rights-in-trust that will be protected now to be exercised later.
