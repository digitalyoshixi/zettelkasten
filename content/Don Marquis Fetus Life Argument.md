Killing is wrong because it deprives the person of any positive things they would have experienced if they kept living
# Argument
```standardform
P1: It is wrong to kill an average individual
P2: Killing is wrong because it deprives us of a future of value
P3: Therefore, it is prima facie wrong to kill anything with a valuable future
P4: Fetuses have a valuable future
C: Therefore, it is wrong to kill fetuses
```
# Counter Argument
- We can say that the sperm and the egg also have future value, and the millions of sperms and eggs being killed is immoral
