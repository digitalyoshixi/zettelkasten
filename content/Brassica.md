A family of field mustard.
[[Selective Breeding]] allowed brassica to develop into a wide array of vegetables:
- Stem : [[Kohlrabi]]
- Leaves : [[Kale]]
- Seeds : [[Mustard]]
- Stem + Flowers : [[Broccoli]]
- Flowers : [[Caulifower]]
- Terminal buds : [[Cabbage]]
- Lateral buds : [[Brussel Sprout]]
