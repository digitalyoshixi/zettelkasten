# Definition
We say [[Matrix]] $A$ is unitarily upper triangular if there exists a [[Unitary Operator|Unitary Matrix]] $P$ such that
$$A = P^{-1}BP$$

A [[Schur's Decomposition]] tells us when $V$ is a [[Complex Inner Product Space]], that there exists an orthonormal basis $\beta$ such that $T$ is [[Upper Triangular Matrices|Upper Triangular]].
