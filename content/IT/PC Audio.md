# Analog Sound to Digital Sound
Computers record sound waves in an electronic format known as sampling. Sampling is capturing the quality of a sound wave at a set rate. This sampling rate is measured in kilohertz(KHz), and the higher this rate is, the higher the sound quality.
# Sound Capture
### Monoaural
Capturing a single track of unique audio
### Stereo
Ability to capture several tracks of audio
