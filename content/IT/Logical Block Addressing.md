![[Logical Block Addressing (LBA)-20240703005548132.webp]]
A method for the HDD or SDD to relay information about sectors or blocks to the [[Central Processing Unit|CPU]] and [[IT/Operating System|OS]]. It assigns each block a LBA number (which is also called a block), allowing for easy addressing of memory.
