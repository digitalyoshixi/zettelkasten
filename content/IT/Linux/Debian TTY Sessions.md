Remember, all linux is is just a terminal.
So, you can have several terminals at once right?

You can sign into several users at the same time.
You can host several X-servers at the same time.

The way to switch between TTY sessions is by doing:
`CTRL+ALT+{F1-F6}`
You can spawn up to 6 different TTY sessions, hence the F1- F6.
