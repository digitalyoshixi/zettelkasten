## Color scheme I like

Ok so tips on creating your linux terminal schemes.

![](file:///C:/Users/Digit/AppData/Local/Temp/lu17828v2iv72.tmp/lu17828v2ivsk_tmp_971b0cf29bf2fc1f.png)

you have the first 8 colors be default colors. These are used in 90% of all programs including the terminal.

You should generally have all these be their regular colors, different shades doesn’t matter, just make sure 1 is reddish, 2 is greenish, 3 is yellowish, so on so forth.

And then 8 and above, you will have colors that are less commonly used. Make these still similar to what they should be, but you can make then very bright very dark and shit.

Color 14 is used for neofetch
