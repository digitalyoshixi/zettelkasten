# Deciphering Processor Numbers
`Intel Core i7 7500 U`
- `Intel Core` - 'Core' brand
- `i7` - Chip family
- `7___` - Generation of `i7`
- `_500` - [[Stock Keeping Unit]]
- `U` - alpha suffix for ultra-low power
`Intel Core i7 12700K`
- `Intel Core` - 'Core' brand
- `i7` - Chip family
- `12___` - Generation of `i7`
- `__700` - SKU numbers
- `K` - alpha suffix for overclockable
