A combination of a [[Personal Digital Assistant]] and a [[Telephone]].
![[Smartphone-20240724023117753.webp|225]]
Commonly includes:
- Multitouch interface
- [[Application Program Interface]] to communicate with OS services
- Use of cellular data to allow for networking
- Synchronization and backup tools
- [[Micro SD]] for storage
Smartphones cannot be upgraded and require specialized repairs by the licensed technicians.
# Phone OS
- [[iOS]]
- [[Android]]
# Technologies
### Display
IPhones use [[Liquid Crystal Display|In-Plane Switching]] or [[Organic Light-Emitting Diode|OLED]]
Others use [[Liquid Crystal Display|Twisted Nematic]]
### Camera
Most cameras now use:
- [[High Dynamic Range]]
- Light compensation
- AI image adjustment
