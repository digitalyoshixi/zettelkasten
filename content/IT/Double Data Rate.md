This is SDRAM, but with double the throughput. It activates twice per clock cycle.
Requires motherboard have a specific [[Memory Chip Controller|MCC]] to work.
May also support [[Multi-Channel Architecture]]

# Backwards Compatibility
SDRAM is backwards compatible with modules of different types
