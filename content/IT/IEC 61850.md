A standard for networking between [[Intelligent Electronic Device]]. Part of [[International Electrotechnical Commission|IEC]]
# Concepts
- [[Generic Object-Oriented Substation Event|GOOSE]]
- [[Manufacturing Message Specification]]
- [[Sampled Value]]
- [[Sampled Measure Value]]
# Securing
- [[IEC 62351]]
- [[Time Synchronization]]
- [[Parallel Redundancy Protocol]] or [[High-Availability Seamless Redundancy]]
- Firewall with [[Generic Object-Oriented Substation Event|GOOSE]] and [[Sampled Measure Value|SMV]] [[Deep Packet Inspection]]
