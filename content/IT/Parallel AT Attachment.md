Ribbon cables that plug directly into the system's motherboard.
These are also called IDE Cables.
![[Parallel AT Attachment-20240702205941108.webp]]
They use a standard [[Molex Power Connector]].
Sends data with [[Parallel Communication]]
- Can have 40 or 80 wires
- 40 connector pins
- 2 devices on one PATA cable at once
- Devices on a PATA cable must be designated with a jumper
# Problems
- The ribbon cables of the PARA impede airflow
- Difficult to insert properly
- Can't [[Device Hot Swapping|hot swap]] PATA drives. you need to shut down the PC to replace drives
- Limited only to 18"
- The same [[Parallel Communication]] problems
