Every piece of PC hardware plugs into the motherboard.  
# How Motherboards Work
![[Motherboard-20240525182847015.webp|402]]
Motherboards are 4+ layers of [[Printed Circuit Board|Printed Circuit Boards]] coated with epoxy for strength.
The motherboard comes with a [[IO Shield Plate]] that protects its built-in [[Computer Ports]]

# Motherboard Compatability
1. Determine if the motherboard is [[Advanced Micro Devices]] or [[Intel]] based
2. Determine [[Motherboard Form Factor]]
3. How tough is the motherboard to configure? consult the motherboard manual
# Motherboard Installation

1. Attach all wire leads to the appropriate standoffs on the motherboard.
   ![[Motherboard-20240625184243800.webp|126]] 
   Devices that use wire leads include:
	- Soft power button
	- [[Reset button]]
	- [[Speaker]]
	- Hard drive activity light
	- Power light
	- [[Universal Serial Bus|USB]]
	- [[Thunderbolt]]
2. Attach [[Central Processing Unit|CPU]] and [[PC Fan|PC Fans]] to motherboard
