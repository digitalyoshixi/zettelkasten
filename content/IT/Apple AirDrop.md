File sharing solution based off [[Bluetooth]] and [[WI-FI]]. Files that are airdropped are sent to all other apple devices nearby.
# Troubleshooting Airdrop
1. See if device is iOS 7 or later
2. Ensure other devices are discoverable. Toggle in Finder > Go | Airdrop
3. Make sure each system's firewall has "Block All Incoming Connections" disabled
