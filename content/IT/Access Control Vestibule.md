A method to prevent [[Tailgating]] by creating 2 doors that require authentication to enter.
The outer door must be closed before the inner door can be opened.
Mantraps are often monitored by a security guard who keeps an [[Entry Control Roster]].
These rooms tend to have a:
- [[Badge Reader]]
- Security Guard
