![[Disc Mirroring-20240702231955238.webp]]
A method to backup data in [[Redundant Array of Independent Disks|RAID]] by copying all contents of one drive into another.
The drive with the original data is the primary drive, while the one with the backup is the mirror drive.
[[Disk Duplexing]] is another form of RAID 1 that is even more redundant
# RAID 1 Failure
Will still work aslong as one drive is operational
