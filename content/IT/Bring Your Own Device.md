The permission for employees to bring their own devices to use inside company networks and premises.
If personal devices are outlawed, then the company will provide company devices.

| Pros                                                 | Cons                                                              |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Control over company devices                         | Cost saving. Does not need to provide devices                     |
| Users cannot keep corporate data on personal devices | Can provide employee benefits relating to company wide data plans |
|                                                      |                                                                   |
# Ownership Dilemmas
### Ownership
1. The employee is storing business data on the device
2. Should the organization have some control over the device?
### Responsibility
1. The employee is using the device for business operations
2. Should this device become a business asset, and should the business pay for this device?
### Privacy
If an business has some control over the employee's device, how much control should they have?
- Remote access
- Ability to view other files
