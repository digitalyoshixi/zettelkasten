# Exam Details
- 90 minute exams
- 90 questions (82-83 usually)
- 8 performance based questions. Example: https://demosim.comptia.io/
- To stay certified, every 3 years you must retake the exams, perform sufficient education [[CompTIA Continuing Education]] or pass a higher level certification
- 1101 and 1102 exams retire in October 2025
- 1101 Passing score of 675 on a scale of 100-900
- 1102 Passing score of 700 on a scale of 100-900
