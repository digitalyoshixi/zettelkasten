A [[Power Surge]] is capable of destroying computer components, so every PC must use a surge suppressor device that absorbs extra voltage from a surge.
![[Surge Suppressors-20240625202830144.webp]]
A surge suppressor absorbs extra voltage from a surge to protect the PC. The [[Power Supply Unit|PSU]] has a built-in surge suppression for smaller surges, but you require dedicated 330-V, 2000-3500 joule surge suppressor for real safety.
# Ultimate Surge
Unplug your devices during storms as if the power grid fails, then your electronics are dead
# Power Conditioning
Great surge suppressors will also block [[Electromagnetic Interference|EMI]] and [[Radio Frequency Interference|RFI]].
