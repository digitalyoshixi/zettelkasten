A video requires both audio and visual data.
When given an audio and video track, to turn it into a video file, they must go through a codec, then a wrapper.
# Codec
A compression algorithm that compresses the audio and video files,
- [[MPEG-2]]
- [[AV1]]
### Wrappers
After video and audio tracks are compressed through the Codec, a wrapper wraps the audio and video tracks into a video file format.
- [[AVI]]
- [[Fraunhoffer MPEG-1 Layer 4|MP4]]
- [[MOV]]
