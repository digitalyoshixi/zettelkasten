Connects device adapters is [[Mass Storage Device|Mass Storage Devices]] to the [[Motherboard]].
They send data with [[Serial Communication]] allowing for less wires in the cable, resulting in more space and better airflow in the PC.
- Point-to-point connection.
- SATA is ~30x faster than [[Parallel AT Attachment|PATA]] 
- 1m long
- Data cables are 7 pins wide
- Power cables are 15 pins wide
# SATA Expansion Cards / Host Bus Adapter
