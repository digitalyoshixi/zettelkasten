# Jack Ports
![[Phone Connector-20240704205637120.webp|270]]
### Main Speaker Out (Green)
Where you plug your speaker or your headphones
### Microphone  (Pink)
Where you plug voice input devices
### Line in (Blue)
Connects to external devices like DVD/MP3 players so that you can use them to play audio through your computer speakers
### Line out
Allows you to play sounds out of your PC through a connected DVD/MP3 player
### Rear out
For rear speakers in a surround sound system
### Analog/digital out
Special digital connection for external digital devices or digital speaker systems
