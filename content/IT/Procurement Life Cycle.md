The process involved with buying assets that a project needs.
# Phases
1. Identify needs. Write a report when finished
2. Evaluate suppliers. Could be a simple search or a drawn out interview process
3. Negotiate terms and finalize purchase order. Both sides should be willing to make concessions 
4. Receive [[Invoice]] and process payment
5. Delivery and audit. When the product arrives, see if operations are better than its previous state
6. Maintain records for future audits. Continue to track effectiveness verses [[Return on Investment|ROI]]
