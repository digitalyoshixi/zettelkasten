# Deciphering Processor Numbers
`AMD Ryzen 7 5800X`
- `AMD Ryzen` - Brand
- `7` - Market power. Higher is more powerful
- `5___` - Generation
- `_8__` - Performance level
- `__00` - Model number
- `X` - [[AMD Suffix]] for high power performance
