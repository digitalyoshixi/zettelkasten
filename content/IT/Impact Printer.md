A method of printing documents by using a ink ribbon and a small matrix of 9-24 pins pressed against a paper to create images.
![[Impact Printer-20240725002338018.webp]]
They are used for:
- [[Point Of Sale]] in receipts
# Process
1. [[Raster]] image is made from input
2. Paper is put into the tractor feed
3. Print head touches ink ribbon
4. Print head pushes down to leave a mark on paper
# Components
### Tractor Feed
![[Impact Printer-20240725005526940.webp]]
Special paper with holes a the side are fed into clips that will use those holes to drag the paper into the printer.
The holes can be removed after print.
### Print Head
![[Pasted image 20240724210150.png|370]]
The matrix of pins used to print.
Draft quality: 9-pin 
Near letter quality (NLQ): 24-pin
### Ribbon
![[Impact Printer-20240725010555285.webp]]
A circular fabric ribbon coated in ink.
These ribbons often come in a proprietary model.
