Independent fans from the [[Power Supply Unit|PSU]] and [[Heat Sink]] ones. You should always have 2-3 of these in your system.
![[PC Fans-20240520201207809.webp|259]]
These fans are usually 80mm or 120mm in side length.
# Installation
The power cable for the fan(or heatsink if it has a fan) is  a 4-wire standout on the motherboard.
![[PC Fans-20240520201656516.webp|213]]
This allows you to configure your fan in the OS, but you rarely have to as most fans are self-maintaining.
