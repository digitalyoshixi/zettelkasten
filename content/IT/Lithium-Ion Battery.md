A re-chargeable battery commonly found in tablets and [[Laptop|Laptops]]. 
- These batteries avoid the [[Battery Memory Effect]].
- Total charge diminishes over time
- They tend to have higher energy density and longer life spans.
- Lithium batteries are prone to [[Battery Swelling]]
