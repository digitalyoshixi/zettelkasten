# ESD Strap
To prevent ESD, you must ensure that both you and the computer have the same electrical potential. This is called grounding yourself.
You can acomplish this by a electrostatic discharge (ESD) strap.
![[Hardware Troubleshooting-20240514013536749.webp|244]]
Put the alligator clip on a metal plate of the computer and tie the strap around your wrist.
ESD straps can also protect against power surges.
