Expansion slots are sockets to place expansion cards such as:
- Video card
- Sound card
- Capture card
- [[Network Interface Card|NIC]]
![[Expansion Bus-20240526011947930.webp|358]]
Expansion cards are usually connected directly to the [[Central Processing Unit|CPU]] or [[Chipset]] depending on the hardware
# Types of Expansion Slot
- [[Peripheral Component Interconnect|PCI]]
- [[Peripheral Component Interconnect eXtended|PCI-X]]
- [[Accelerated Graphics Port|AGP]]
- [[Peripheral Component Interconnect Express|PCIe]]
