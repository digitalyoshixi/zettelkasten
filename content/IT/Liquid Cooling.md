A alternative to [[Heat Sink]].
Water runs through the top of the CPU and absorbs all the heat, then cools off through the cooler portion, repeating the process. 
![[Liquid Cooling-20240520201525821.webp|322]]
It includes:
- A hollow metal block to sit above the CPU
- Pumps to move liquid around
- Cooler to cool the liquid
- Hosing
