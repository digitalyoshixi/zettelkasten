# Temperature, Humidity, Ventilation
Be aware of normal temperatures, humidity and ventilation levels to detect anomalies.
A computer works best at:
- Room temperature (22$^oC$)
- Dry air (<40% humidity range)
- Clean rooms
### Ventilation Maintenance
- Ensure air ducts clear of obstructions
- Ensure duct tampers are not too hot or cold
- Ensure equipment is located in area with good ventilation
- Use compressed air to clean fans
- Use vacuum to clean fans
- Replace air filters
- Wear N95 Face mask during maintenance
- Enclose system with dust shield if it is very dusty
