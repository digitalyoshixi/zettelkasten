A bus that supports both [[Serial AT Attachment|SATA]] and [[Peripheral Component Interconnect Express|PCIe]] storage devices.
Replaces the SATA link and transport layers with the PCIe version. The bus can carry up to 2000 MBps.
![[Serial AT Attachment Express-20240702214454962.webp]]
SATAe is forwards compatable with SATA. You can plug in SATA into SATAe. But, you need a SATAe compatable motherboard to use SATAe.
