[[Disk Striping]] with one parity disk. Can be done with a minimum of 3 disks
![[RAID 5-20240817222351808.webp]]
RAID 5 is the best [[Redundant Array of Independent Disks|RAID]] version in balancing storage and redundancy. 
The risk is that if you remove a healthy disk, all data would be lost.
# RAID 5 Failure
Can have all but one drive operational, and still work fine. You should still replace the bad drive ASAP.
