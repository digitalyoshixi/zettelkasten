Using a heated printhead to create an image on paper.
Requires thermal paper to use.
These printers tend to be very quiet
# Thermal Printer Types
### Direct Thermal Printer
Uses heating element to burn dots into the surface of the paper
### Thermal Wax Transfer Paper
Thermal paper passes through the heat element.
Used in [[Point Of Sale]] for reciepts.
