In a [[Disk Mirroring|RAID 1]] or [[RAID 5]], [[RAID 6]] array, you can resync to restore data from a mirror.
Usually performed when a bad drive is removed.
# Rebuild
A rebuild is a special type of resync for [[RAID 5]] or [[RAID 6]] that rebuilds from parity disks
