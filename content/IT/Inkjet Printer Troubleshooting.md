# Maintenance
Inkjet printers require little physical maintenance.
Software exists to automatically maintain the printer.
Manual maintenance occurs where:
- Initial setup of inkjet printers require calibration.
- Periodic cleaning of printer head with water
# Color Layers Aren't Aligned
- Recalibrate the printer
# No Ink coming out
- Ink in the print head dried up due to infrequent use of printer
- Overheated printer
- Multiple pages are being fed at the same time as a result of humidity
- Excessive ink bin overflowed and broke printer
# Paper Jam
1. Remove paper tray
2. Vigorously remove paper. If the printer has an option to release tension, then press it
# Vertical Lines on Output Page
- Clean Printheads
- Replace ink cartridge
# Grinding Noise
- Issue in carrier mechanism
