Swelling in [[Lithium-Ion Battery|Li-Ion]] and [[Lithium-Ion Polymer Battery|LiPo]] batteries is caused by:
- Overcharging
- Fast discharging
- Blunt damage to battery
- High or Low temperatures
# Avoiding Swelling
1. Keep battery temperature within 32$^oF$ and 95$^oF$
   ![[Battery Swelling-20240723213624983.webp]]
2. Keep battery charged around 70-80%
3. Never let battery drain to 0%
# Damage
![[Battery Swelling-20240723213340273.webp]]
Rupturing lithium batteries will cause toxic chemicals and combustions.
