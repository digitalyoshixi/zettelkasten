A [[Mass Storage Device]] that is very affordable.
It works by writing data to a magnetic disk.
# How It Works
![[Hard Drive-20240730204210863.webp|540]]
Data is stored on the spinning discs.
There is are read/write heads which are [[Transducers]] controlled by [[Servo Motors]], that operate on these discs.
There is a read/write head on the very topmost disc, and another read/write head on the bottom of the disc.
- Usually reaches 200 IOPS
- 15000 RPM
- Originally used [[Cylinder Head Sector|CHS]] addressing, now uses [[Logical Block Addressing|LBA]]
