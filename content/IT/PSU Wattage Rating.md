Every device in your PC requires a certain wattage to function. A [[Hard Drive]] usually takes 15W of power. A [[NVIDIA]] GPU can take ~400W.
When buying a PSU, it must be able to support the cumulative wattage of all your devices.
# Efficiency Rating
The consumption:return rate of your PSU. From the watts that your PC draws from AC and to how much of it is converted into DC current for your components.
**80+:** 80% Efficiency
**80+ Bronze:** 82% Efficiency
**80+ Silver:** 85% Efficiency
**80+ Gold:** 87% Efficiency
**80+ Platinum:** 90% Efficiency
**80+ Titanium:** 92% Efficiency
It is impossible to have 100% Efficiency as energy will always be lost to [[Thermal Energy]]
It is all about how much money you save in the long run.
# Recommended PSU Rating
Always get atleast 550W power supply, and purchase power supplies that have make 50% more power than you need.
All power supplies produce less wattage over time, so if you want a long lasting power supply, buy the higher wattage ones.
