Compounds that light up when given charge through current.
These compounds emit their own light, so there is no need for a backlight.
They can turn off, enabling pure black color.
Commonly used in ultra-thin notebooks.
- Expensive 
- Lower output light compared to [[Light Emitting Diode|LED]]
- Better contrast ratio than [[Liquid Crystal Display|LCD]]
- Better color than [[Liquid Crystal Display|LCD]]
# Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED)
OLED has an added TFT layer over the screen which produces better color and uses less electricity.
