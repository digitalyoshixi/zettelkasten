![[Multifunction Printer-20240725001500234.webp|363]]
A device that is a combination of:
- [[Printer]]
- [[Scanner]]
- [[Fax Machine]]
- [[Automatic Document Feeders]]
All MFD devices will have to spend 3-4 hours acclimating to the environment when first setup.
# Connection
- [[USB Types|USB-A]] to [[USB Types|USB-B]] cable
- [[RJ-45|Ethernet Cable]]
- [[WI-FI|WIFI]]
- [[Bluetooth]]
# Network Connectivity
Printers may come included with:
- [[WI-FI|WIFI]]
- [[RJ-45]]
- [[Bluetooth]]
that can be configured in the menu.
[[Print Server|Print Servers]] can also be purchased to provide network connectivity if a printer does not have one.
