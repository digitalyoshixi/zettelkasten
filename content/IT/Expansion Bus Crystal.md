Similar to the [[Crystal Oscillator|System Crystal]], the EBC provides a periodic signal only to [[Expansion Bus]].
This is to provide a standard speed regardless of system hardware.
![[Expansion Bus Crystal-20240526012847604.webp]]
You would connect these directly to the [[Expansion Bus]] 
This means **No Matter how fast the motherboard runs, the expansion slots run at a standard speed**
