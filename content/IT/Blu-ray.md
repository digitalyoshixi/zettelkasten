# Blu-ray
Stores up to 25GB on single layer disks and 50GB on double sided disks.
### BD-ROM
Blu-ray equivalent of CD-ROM. Fully backwards compatable with DVD-video players.
### BD-R
Writable blue ray disks.
### BD-RE
Blu-ray rewritable. You can write and erase data on a blu-ray disc several times.
