A [[Digital Rights Management|DRM]] solution created by [[Intel]] to prevent copying of digital audio and video as it travels through connectors like:
- [[Digital Visual Interface|DVI]]
- [[High-Definition Multimedia Interface|HDMI]]
- [[DisplayPort|DP]]
