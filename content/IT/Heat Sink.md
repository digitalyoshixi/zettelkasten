A copper or metal device designed to dissipate heat from whatever it touches.

# Product Types
### Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) CPU Coolers
These are CPU manufacturer-made heatsinks specifically designed to be compatible with your CPU.
![[Heat Sink-20240520200503975.webp|290]]
Buying some of these ensure that your CPU is 100% compatible
### Specialized CPU Coolers
3rd party heatsinks that usually exceed OEM coolers in the ammount of heat they dissipate.
![[Heat Sink-20240520201003631.webp|363]]
# Installation
Many heat sinks will have a thermal pad at the bottom. If there is tape, remove that tape and then apply the heat sink over the CPU after [[Thermal Paste]] is applied.
