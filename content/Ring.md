A [[Set]] $R$ with [[Addition]] and [[Multiplication]] operators such that:
1. [[Closed Under Addition]] ($\forall a,b \in R, a +b \in R$)
2. [[Closed Under Multiplication]] $\forall a,b \in R, a \times b \in R$
And:
3. [[Additive Identity]] ($\exists 0 \in R \text{ s.t } \forall a \in R, 0 +_{R}a = a$)
4. [[Additive Inverse]] exists ($\forall a \in R, \exists -a \in R \text{ s.t } a + (-a) = 0$)
5. [[Associative]] with [[Addition]] ($\forall a,b, c\in R, (a +_{R}b)+_{R}c = a+_{R}(b+_{R}c)$)
6. [[Commutative]] under [[Addition]] $(\forall a,b \in R, (a+_{R}b) = (b+_{R}a))$
7. [[Multiplicative Identity]] exists
8. [[Associative]] with [[Multiplication]]
9. [[Distributive]] under [[Addition]]
