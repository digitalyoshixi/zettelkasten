Encrypting using matrixes.
# Example
With the message $ACT$ and the matrix $MYSUPERKE$
We create a matrix:
$$
M_{key} = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
6 & 24 & 1\\
13 & 16 & 10\\
7 & 21 & 13\\
\end{array}\right]
$$

$$
M_{message} = 
\left[\begin{array}{cc} 
5\\
8\\
14\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
