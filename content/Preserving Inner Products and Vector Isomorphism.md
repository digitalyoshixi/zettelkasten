# Definition
1. Let $V, W$ as [[Inner Product Space]] with $F_{1}$
2. $T \in \mathcal{L}(V,W)$ then, $T$ preserves inner products if
3. $\langle T \alpha | T \beta \rangle_{W} = \langle \alpha | \beta \rangle_{V}, \forall \alpha, \beta \in V$
