The rate at which one variable affects another.
- When [[Covariance]] $Cov(X,Y) > 0$, then on average $X \uparrow$ as $Y \uparrow$ and vice-versa
- When [[Covariance]] $Cov(X,Y) < 0$, then on average $X \downarrow$ as $Y \downarrow$ and vice-versa
