A bit used to determine if a clock communication should be [[Clock Edge|Rising Edge]] or [[Clock Edge|Falling Edge]].
![[Clock Polarity-20250624212559788.webp]]
- CPOL = 0 means the leading edge of a clock pulse is at 0
- CPOL = 1 means the leading edge of a clock pulse is at 1
