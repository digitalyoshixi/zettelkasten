A finite collection of rules which tell us whether certain strings or sentences are grammatical or not.
A grammar is context free if it is in the form of:
$$G \to (V \cup T)^{*}, G \in V$$
- $G$ is the grammar
- $V$ is a variable or [[Non-terminal]]
- $T$ is a [[Terminal]]
