A property of [[Qubit|Qubits]] is that they lose their [[Quantum Superposition|Superposition]] when measured, this means that if a qubit is between 0 or 1, it has a 50% chance of being measured as either.
![[Quantum Measurement-20250620152425479.webp|219]]
This also allows a [[Qubit]] to contain more information than a regular [[Binary|Bit]]
