Used to represent pieces of the structure of a language. 'Left side of [[Programming/Grammar|Grammar]]'
They are more-so labels for parts of the program, and not values within the program.
# Programming
In programming, these are:
- Statement
- Condition
- Subroutine
# English
In english, these are:
- Nouns
- Verbs
- Noun Phrase
- Verb Phrase
- Sentence
