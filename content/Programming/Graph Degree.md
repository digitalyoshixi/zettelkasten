$deg(u) = |\text{neighbourhood(u)}|$
The # of elements in the [[Neighbourhood]] a node.
# Undirected Definition
A [[Graph Node|Vertex]]'s degree is the number of [[Graph Edge|Edges]] that are [[Vertex Incident|Incident]] on it.

# Directed Definition
A [[Graph Node|Vertex]]'s degree is [[Out Degree]] + [[In Degree]]
