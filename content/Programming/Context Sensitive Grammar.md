Grammars that have rules of the form:
$$
\alpha A\beta \to \alpha \gamma \beta
$$
With:
- $A$ as [[Non-terminal]]
- $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$ [[String]] of [[Terminal|Terminals]] or [[Non-terminal]] with $\gamma$ non-empty
Grammars can be recognizes with  [[Linear Bounded Automata]]
