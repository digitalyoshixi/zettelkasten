A collection of arguments that are bad in terms of going against disability screening.
# Arguments
### Argument 1
- Screening and selection are discriminatory and prevent births of racialized or unwanted traits
### Argument 2
- Reinforces forms of discrimination against existing disabled people, and reduces number of disabled people making them isolated and reducing political power
### Argument 3
- Reduction of human diversity and eliminates a unique contribution to the world - that being the lesson of respect for difference and nobility in achievement in face of great obstacles
### Argument 4
- Screening and selection says disabled people should not exist - 'The rest of us are trying to prevent the existence of people like you'
