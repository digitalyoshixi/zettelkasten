# Rational
Faith can be rational if:
- [[No Bad Faith]]
And further, permissible or required if:
### Permissiblity
Faith is rationally permissible if:
1. You are already confident that $P$
2. You know countervailing evidence wont undermine $P$
3. It is costly to gather more evidence
### [[Required]]
Faith is required if:
- There are costs to postponing your decision
# Examples for Rational Faith
You wonder whether to do a [[Doctor of Philosophy|PhD]]
- Its a risky choice
- Academic job markey terrible
- Dissertation is daunting
But:
1. Youre confident you want to do a PhD
2. You know that countervailing evidence wont tell conclusively against a PhD
3. Costly to gather more evidence, as the only way to do so is to spend more years in a masters program
Then, by this you are rationally permitted to tale a risk on doing a PhD
