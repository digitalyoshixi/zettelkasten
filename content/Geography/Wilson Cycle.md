The process of creating and seperation of the [[Supercontinent]].
Tectonic plates could only bend at their margins.
New crust is generated when the seafloor spreads and is destroyed at [[Subduction Zones]].
If all the crust between two continents is destroyed, the continents collide to form mountain ranges.
