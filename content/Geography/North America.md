# Creation
### Step 0 - Creation of cratons from [[Magmatic Arc]]
Small [[Province|Provinces]] and [[Craton|Cratons]] form at [[Magmatic Arc]]
### Step 1 - Cratons merge to form [[Arctica]]
The smaller cratons merge at the [[Trans-Hudson Orogeny]] to form [[Arctica]]
### Step 2 - [[Arctica]] merges with [[Province|Terranes]] to create [[Nena]]
Merging occurs at the [[Penokean Orogeny]]. Creates [[Southern Province]]
### Step 3 - [[Nena|Nena]] merges with [[Province|Terranes]] to create [[Rodina]]
Merging occurs at [[Grenville Orogeny]]. 
### Step 4 - [[Rodina]] merges with [[Province|Terranes]] to create [[Supercontinent|Pangea]]
Merging occurs at [[Appalachian Basin]]
