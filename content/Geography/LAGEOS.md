2 satellites launched to provide accurate [[Geodetic]] measurements.

They are brass balls with 426 reflective prisms that can bounce back lasers emitted from land-based stations.
Satellites have stable orbits at 6000km.

Used to enable accurate measurement of earths crust to detect shape of geoid.
