A portion of the [[Huronian Supergroup]] where there is evidence of a meteorite impact.
Contains lots of high-quality minerals due to the [[Hydrothermal Alteration]] caused by [[Bolides]]
Includes:
- Nickel
- Copper
- Gold
- Silver
- Platinum
- Palladium
- Rhodium
- Irridium
- Ruthenium
