The earliest part of earths history including the periods of:
- [[Hadean Era]]
- [[Archean Era]]
- [[Proterozoic Era]]
- [[Phanerozoic Era]]
No oceans, no granites, no continents.
# Formation History
1. Hadean era, meteors create magma
2. Magma cools and undergoes [[Crystal Differentiation]]
3. [[Volatile Gases]] escape and create [[Atmosphere]] and [[Water]]
4. [[Volcanic Arc]] forms creating lower-density rock (as water mixed with magma tends to make lower-density rocks)
