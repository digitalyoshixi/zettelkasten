A phenomenon that occurs at the seafloor where you have sea mountains in very close proximity, forming a track-like line.

![[Hotspot Tracks-20250121212738784.webp]]
Occurs because tectonic plates move over fixed 'hotspots' in the [[Mantle]].
![[Hotspot Tracks-20250219022104246.webp]]
Explains why volcanoes occur away from plate boundaries
