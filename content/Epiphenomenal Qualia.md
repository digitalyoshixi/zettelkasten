A thought experiment in which Mary knows every physical fact about colors, but has never seen any colors as she lives in a black-and-white room.

If [[Physicalism]] is true, then mary knows everything about color experiences.
Though, it is apparent that something fundamental is missing in her understanding of colors.
This is because she lacks the experience of [[Qualia]].
