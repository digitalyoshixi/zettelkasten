# Definition
- With $R = [a,b] \times [c,d]$ as a rectangle
- With $f$ integrable over [[Domain|Region]] $R$
A cut perpendicular to $x$ axis is:
$$
\int \int_{R} f(x,y)dA = \int_{a}^{b} [\int_{c}^{d}f(x,y)dy] dx
$$
A cut perpendicular to $y$ axis is:
$$
\int \int_{R} f(x,y)dA = \int_{c}^{d} [\int_{a}^{b}f(x,y)dx] dy
$$
