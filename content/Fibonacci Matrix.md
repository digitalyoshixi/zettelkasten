The fibonachi matrix is:
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 1\\
1 & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
As 
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 1\\
1 & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
\left[\begin{array}{c}
f_{n-1}\\
f_{n-2}\\
\end{array}\right]
= 
\left[\begin{array}{c}
f_{n}\\
f_{n-1}\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
