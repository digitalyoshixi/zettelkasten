# Definition
Suppose $f : D \subset \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}^{k}$ where $D$ is an [[Open Set]]
Suppose $x_{0} \in D$
Then, we say $\lim_{ x \to x_{0} }f(x) = b$ if: $\forall$ [[Open Ball]] $N$ of $b$, $\exists$ [[Open Ball]] $U$ of $x_{0}$ s.t $(x \in U \implies f(x) \in N)$
