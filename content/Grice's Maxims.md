The rules set in place to allow [[Language]] to be structured [[Logic|Logically]].
For this to hold, all language must follow the [[Cooperative Principle]].
# Maxims
### Maxim of Quantity
Give as much information as required, and no more.
- Dont say that a tomato is asbestos free
### Maxim of Quality
Tell the truth
### Maxim of Relation
Be relevant to the conversation, as the other person will try and fit together what you say within what has already happened.
### Maxim of Manner
Be clear in what youre saying
- Avoid obscurity of expressions
- Avoid ambiguity
- Be brief
- Be orderly
