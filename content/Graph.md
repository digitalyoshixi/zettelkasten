The graph of a function $f: X \to Y$ is the subset $f = \{ x, f(x) : x \in X \} \subset X \times Y$
# Examples
- The graph of $f(x) = x^{2}$ is a subset of $\mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R} = \mathbb{R}^{2}$
- The graph of $g(x,y)= x^{2}+y^{2}+1$ is a subset of $\mathbb{R}^{2} \times \mathbb{R} = \mathbb{R}^{3}$
