A quantum computer must be able to have [[Qubit]] with [[Quantum Superposition|Superposition]].
Properties required include:
- Initialization : Prepare qubits in **known** states
- Control: Perform operations to change [[Quantum Superposition|Superposition]] of [[Qubit]]
- Coupling : [[Qubit]] should interact
- Readout : Measurable Qubits
