The level of uncertainty/information associated with a random variable $X$'s possible states.
It tells us how much information we are missing about random variable $X$. Or in other words, how much information we gain by finding the exact value of $X$
# Formula
$$
H(X) = - \sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(x) \log_b P(x)
$$
