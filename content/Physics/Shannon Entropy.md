Describes how much information we need on average to encode an outcome of a given distribution formed from [[Random Variable]] $X$
- Allows calculating uncertainty of distribution of [[Random Variable|RV]] $X$
# Formula
$$
H(X) = - \sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(x) \log_b P(x)
$$
