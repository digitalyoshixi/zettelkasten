A type of [[Quantum Computing|Quantum Computer]] that is very good at networked communication.

Uses atoms as [[Qubit]], shoots lazers to modify the [[Quantum Superposition|Superposition]] of qubits.
# Trapping Techniques
[[Ion|Ions]] are trapped with:
- [[Paul Trap]]
- [[Surface Electrode Trap]]
- [[Penning Trap]]
