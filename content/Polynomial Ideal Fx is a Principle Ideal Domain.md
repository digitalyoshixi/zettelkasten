# Theorem
- Let $M$ be a non-zero [[Polynomial Ideal]] in $\mathbb{F}[x]$
- Then there exists a unique [[Monic Polynomial]] $d \in \mathbb{F}[x]$ such that $M$ is the [[Principle Polynomial Ideal]] generated by $d$.

This monic found by finding the [[Greatest Common Divisor of Polynomials]] in the set $M$
