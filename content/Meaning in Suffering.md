Life is a challenge, and each challenge provides the resolution of that challenge as a meaning.
Acquired by [[Viktor Frankl]] as a means to help him survive [[Holocaust]].
*Suffering ceases to be suffering at the moment it finds a meaning*

Tied to [[Stoicism]]
# Example
- Mans wife dies, man's grief means the suffering of her death is passed onto him. There is meaning to his grief
