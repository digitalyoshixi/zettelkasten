An object that is qualified during its introduction.
Appropriate use where it can be substituted with the phrase "With The Condition".
# Example
- Let $x$ be a $\mathbb{R}$-number, such that $x^{2}+1>5$
- $\exists p \in \mathbb{N}$ such that $p, p+2$ are [[Prime Number]]
