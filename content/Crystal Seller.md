A character written in [[The Alchemist]].
He is a follower of the [[Quaran]] that yearns to one day reach [[Mecca]], though, he keeps this dream in his head as he believes actually reaching the place will be a disapointment.
His dream is what keeps him waking up everyday and provides him purpose. He does not want to achieve his dream, he just wants to keep dreaming.
