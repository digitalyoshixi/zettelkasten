# Region
For [[Domain|Region]] $R = \cup_{i=1}^{n}R_{i}$ Then:
$$
\int\int_{_{R}} f(x,y)dA = \sum_{i=1}^{n}\int\int_{_{R_{i}}}f(x,y)dA
$$
# 2D Region Specific
For a 2D [[Domain|Region]] $R = R_{1} \times R_{2}$
$$
\int \int_{R}  f(x,y)dA = \int \int_{R_{1}}  f(x,y)dA + \int \int_{R_{2}}  f(x,y)dA 
$$
