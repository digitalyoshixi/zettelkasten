Assessments and reports given to regulatory bodies on a organizations efforts to remain compliant.
# Types
### Internal Compliance Report
A internal audit of policies, standards and procedures
Non-professional, and used to determine gaps in progress.
- Check for policy adherence
- Keep regular auditing
- Consult [[Incident Response|IR]] evaluations
- Conduct [[Risk Assessment]]
- Log progress on [[Security Awareness|Security Training]]
### External Compliance Report
A demonstrating of adherence of industry requirements to regulatory bodies.
- Adherence to [[General Data Protection Regulation|GDPR]], [[Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act|HIPPA]], etc
- Often requires third party auditors
- Emphasis on data privacy and protections
- Organizations should remain transparent
- External audits can be shown to give clients or partners assurance
