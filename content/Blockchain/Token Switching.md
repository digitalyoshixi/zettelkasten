A utilization of [[Smart Contract|Smart Contracts]] for simulating [[Foreign Exchange|Currencies]]
- Fixed pool of money
- Several different tokens
- When one token goes up, adjust all other token values to reflect their value in the pool
# Use Cases
- Good for traders who want to try out a new [[Cryptocurrency]] not listed on a traditional market. They link the value of one currency on the market to that new currency
