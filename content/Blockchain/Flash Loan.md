A money-making technique that involves:
- Using [[Ethereum]]'s borrowing [[Smart Contract]] to borrow a select amount
- Buying A [[Cryptocurrency]] on one market and selling it back on another market at a higher value
- Returning the amount listed on the smart contract, leaving extra profit for you
