A generalized [[Pumping Lemma]].
# Lemma
- If Language $L$ is generated by a [[Context Free Grammar|CFG]]
- Then $\exists p \geq 1$ s.t $\forall w \in L$ with length $> p$, a way of marking $\geq p$ positions, there exist [[Non-terminal]] $A$ and a way to split $w$ into 5 segments $uxzyv$ s.t:
	- $S \Rightarrow^{*} uAv \Rightarrow^{*} uxAyv \Rightarrow^{*} uxzyv$
- $x$ contains atleast one marked position
- $xyz$ contains at most $p$ marked positions
- $u,z$ contains marked positions or $y,v$ both contain marked positions.
