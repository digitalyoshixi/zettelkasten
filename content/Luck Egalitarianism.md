The belief that fairness should be determined only by the choices people make. Excludes external factors like [[Luck]].
- Person born into poverty given same consideration as person outside of poverty if both make the same choices in life
# Formal Definition
With:
- $F(\cdot|c, \pi)$ as the cumulative distribution of utility at circumstance $c$ and effort $\pi$
- $c$ is the circumstances beyond ones control ([[Luck]])
- $0 \leq \pi \leq 1$ is the $\pi$-th quartile of the distribution of effort of individuals under circumstance $\pi$
$$F(\cdot|c, \pi ) = F(\cdot|c', \pi)$$
In other words, luck does not matter
