The level of rigor applied to the [[Imitation Game]].
# Levels
Low-bar indistinguishability can be:
- Ordinary users as judges
- Short sessions such as 5 minutes
- Relaxed threshold of 30% pass for the machine
High-bar indistinguishability can be:
- Expert panel of judges
- Long sessions over 1 hour
- High threshold of 51% pass for the machine
