Process:
1. Find the midpoint $m = \frac{a+d}{2}$ which is the [[Matrix Trace]]/2
2. Find the product $\det(A) = ad - bc = \lambda_{1}\lambda_{2}$ which is the [[Determinant]]
3. Then, $\lambda_{1} = m- \sqrt{ m^{2} - p } , \lambda_{2} = m+ \sqrt{ m^{2} - p }$
