Humans will not be useful to AI, because a [[Artificial General Intelligence|AGI]] is able to do anything a human can.
Maintaining humans is expensive and can be replaced.
Either:
- We replace humans - similar to how humans have no need for horses, as we have automobiles that are cheaper to maintain.
- We remove humans of their 'irrelevant' portions - similar to factory farmed chickens
