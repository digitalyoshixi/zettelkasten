# Left Unique Relation
A [[Binary Relation]] $\{ (x,y) | xRy \}, x \in X, y \in Y$ where $\forall y \in Y,$ there is at most one $x \in X$ s.t $xRy$
# Right Unique Relation
A [[Binary Relation]] $\{ (x,y) | xRy \}, x \in X, y \in Y$ where $\forall x \in X,$ there is at most one $y \in Y$ s.t $xRy$
