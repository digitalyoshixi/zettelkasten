A [[Random Variable]] distributed from [[Binomial Distribution]].
$X \sim \text{Binomial}(n,p)$
# [[Probability Mass Function|PMF]]
$$p_{X}(x) = P(X = x) = \binom{n}{x}p^{x}q^{n-x}, \forall x \in \{ 0,\dots,n \}$$
- With $q = 1-p$
