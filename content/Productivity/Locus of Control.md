How much a person believes they can control the events that surround them
# Internal Locus
Person believes their events result from their own actions.
They assume hard work, effort and planning will allow them to succeed.
# External Locus
People who believe that:
- Others
- [[Fate]]
- [[Luck]]
determine events.
Less likely to be an [[Entrepreneur]].
Also see [[Stoicism]]
