A close representation of [[Jordan Form]] via conversion from a [[Rational Canonical Form|Rational Form]].
# Process
1. Get the [[Rational Canonical Form|Rational Form]] of a matrix, with the [[Cyclic Decomposition Theorem]]
2. For each polynomial in your cyclic decomposition, apply [[Primary Decomposition Theorem]]
3. For each primary factor, construct a [[Jordan Form|Jordan Block]] for each, thereby creating a primary jordan matrix
