A mentality that prioritizes work that is:
- Distraction-free
- Cognitively challenging
- High-concentration

# Deep Work Philosophy
### Monasitc
- Keep yourself isolated for as long as possible
- Reduce all [[Shallow Work]] by all means necessary
### Bimodal
- Reserve and allocate only few consecutive days to the monastic deep work state
### Rhythmic
- Transform deepwork into a routine
- Specific 3-4 hour time periods each day for deep work
### Journalistic
- Divide day into blocks of deep work, shallow work time
