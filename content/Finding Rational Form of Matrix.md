# Process
1. Find the [[Characteristic Polynomial]] of the matrix
2. Find [[Minimal Polynomial]] of the characteristic polynomial.
3. Expand the minimal polynomial
4. Write the [[Companion Matrix]] of the expanded version. This is your rational canonical form.
