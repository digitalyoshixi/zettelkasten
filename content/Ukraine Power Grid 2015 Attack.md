A cyberattack causing power outages all across ukraine by [[Sandworm Group]]

Launched through a excel macro attack.
- Stole [[Virtual Private Network|VPN]] and [[Windows Active Directory|AD]] creds, controlled [[Human Machine Interface|HMI]] and shut down every circuit breaker
- Used [[Killdisk]] to wipe operator PCs
# Takeaway
- Monitor and restrict remote [[Human Machine Interface|HMI]] access
- IT breaches can cascade into OT outages
- [[Network Segmentation]] is required
