# Method
- Suppose $f$ and $g$ are $C^{1}$ [[Class Functions]] from $U \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$ to $\mathbb{R}$.
- Let $x_{0} \in U$ be a point satisfying $g(x_{0}) = c$ and $\nabla g(x_{0}) \neq 0$
- Let $S = L_{c} = \{ x : g(x) = c \}$ be [[Level Sets|Level Set]] 
- If $f|S$ has a [[Local Extrema]] at $x_{0}$ then, $\nabla f(x_{0}) = \lambda \nabla g(x_{0})$ "The gradients are parallel"
