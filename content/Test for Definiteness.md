Given $n \times n$ matrix $A = [a_{ij}]$, the submatrices $A_{k}$ where $i, j \leq k$
- $A$ is [[Positive Definite Matrix]] if all determinants $\det(A_{k})$ are positive
- $A$ is [[Positive Definite Matrix|Negative Definite Matrices]] if all determinants $\det(A_{k})$ alternate sign with $\det(A_{1}) < 0$ , $\det(A_{2}) > 0$, $\det(A_{3}) < 0$, $\det(A_{4}) > 0$, etc
