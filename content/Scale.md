A series of [[Note|Notes]] that sound good together.
# Notation
From a starting note:
- `W+H` : Move 3 [[Semitone]] up
- `W` : Move 2 [[Semitone]] up
- `H` : Move 1 [[Semitone]] up
For example, starting at `C` with pattern `W+H-W-H` would result in:
`C->D#->E#(F)->F#`
