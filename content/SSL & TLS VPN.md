A [[Virtual Private Network|VPN]] developed with [[Secure Sockets Layer|SSL]] and [[Transport Layer Security|TLS]]
- Does not require much configuration. Any [[Hypertext Markup Language|HTML5]] browser can run these
- Requires an access portal to do [[Local Area Network|LAN]] activities
