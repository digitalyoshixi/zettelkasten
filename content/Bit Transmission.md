This is the process of communication through wire or air that network devices can decode.
# Encoding Scheme
- 0 is represented as the transition from high voltage to low voltage within a 1/10,000,000th-of-a-second interval
- 1 is represented as the transition from low voltage to high voltage within a 1/10,000,000th-of-a-second interval
