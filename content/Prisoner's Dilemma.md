A game in [[Game Theory]] that results in a [[Nash Equilibrium]].
![[Prisoner's Dilemma-20260327005335692.webp]]
- If parties cooperate, both gain reduced sentences
- If one party betrays, they gain no sentence
- If both parties betray, they gain the worst sentence
