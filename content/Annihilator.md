The set of [[Linear Transform|Linear Map]] that map to 0
# Definition
- Let $S$ be a [[Subset]] of [[Vector Space]] $V$ over [[Field]] $\mathbb{F}$
- The annihilator of $S$ is the set $S^{o} = \{  T \in \mathcal{L}(V,\mathbb{F}) | T(v) = 0 ,\forall v \in S\}$
