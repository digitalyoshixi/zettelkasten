# Theorem
1. Let $U \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ be [[Unitary Operator|Unitary]]
2. Then, a [[Orthonormal Set|Orthonormal Basis]] $\beta$ of unit vectors $V$ exists such that $[U]_{\beta} = diag(e^{i \theta},\dots, e^{i \theta n})$, where $e^{i \theta} ,\dots, e^{i \theta n}$ are the [[Eigenvector|Eigenvalues]] of $U$
