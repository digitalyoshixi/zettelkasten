# Definition
For sequence of [[Random Variable|RV]] $X_{1},X_{2},\dots$ and [[Random Variable|RV]] $Y$
$X_{n}$ converges in probability to $Y$ if:
$$\lim_{ n \to \infty } P(X_{n} \leq x) = P(Y \leq x), \forall x \in R$$
(Denoted $X_{n} \to^{D} Y$)
