In a [[Lattice]] $L$ with finite height, every [[Monotone Function]] $f$ has a unique least-fixed-point defined as:
$$
fix(f) = \bigsqcup_{i \geq 0}f^{i}(\bot)
$$
For which $f(fix(f)) = fix(f)$
