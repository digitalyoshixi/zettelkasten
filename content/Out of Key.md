The practice of setting notes outside a given [[Scale]]. Makes unnatural sounds.
Most songs are a little dissonant due to [[Melody]] and [[Chord Progression]] disconnect, but its ok.
# Reducing Dissonance
- Space out [[Melody]], [[Chord Progression]], [[Harmony]], [[Bass]] onto different [[Octave]]
- Reduce [[Note Velocity]] of notes causing dissonance
