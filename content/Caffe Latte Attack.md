An attack against [[Windows]] [[Wireless Equivalent Privacy|WEP]] where the attacker exploits windows' wireless stack to obtain WEP key from remote clients.
- Send flood of encrypted [[Address Resolution Protocol|ARP]] requests to take advantage of shared key auth and message modification flaws
- Uses [[Address Resolution Protocol|ARP]] responses to recover [[Wireless Equivalent Privacy|WEP]] key
