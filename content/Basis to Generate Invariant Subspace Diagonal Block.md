# Theorem
- Let $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$
- Let $c_{1},\dots,c_{n}$ be distinct [[Eigenvector|Eigenvalues]] of $T$
- Then, there exists a basis $\beta$ such that:
  $$
 [T]_{\beta} = \left[\begin{array}{c|c} 
diag(c_{1}I_{d_{1}},\dots,c_{n}I_{d_{n}}) & C\\ 
\hline
0 & D
\end{array}\right]
  $$
