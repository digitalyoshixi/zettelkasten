- Let $a,b \in R$
- Let $a < b$
- If $f$ is cont on $[a,b]$
 Then $\exists c \in (a,b)$ s.t $f(c) = \frac{1}{b-a}\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx$
 
 Katherine likes this form: $\exists c \in [a,b]$ s.t $\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = f(c)(b-a)$
