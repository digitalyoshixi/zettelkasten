# Chain Rule
Oftentimes, you may need chain rule.
$\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x)) = f'(g(x)) * g'(x)*$
For example, with the function f(x) = sin(6x), the derivative is: cos(6x) * 6

Another!
$7cos(\frac{\pi}{3}x+2)-6$
f'(g(x)) = $-7sin(\frac{\pi}{3}x+2)$
g'(x) = $\frac{\pi}{3}$
$-\frac{7\pi}{3}(\frac{\pi}{3}x+2)$
