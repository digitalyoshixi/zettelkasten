The trace for matrix
$$
tr(\left[\begin{array}{cc}
a & b\\
c & d\\
\end{array}\right]) = a+d
$$
# Properties
1. $tr(A^{T}) = tr(A)$
2. $tr(AB) = tr(BA)$
3. $tr(A + B) = tr(A) + tr(B)$
