This is a [[Polynomial Time]] lattice reduction algorithm.
# Algorithm
- Given a [[Basis]] $B = \{ b_{1},\dots,b_{d} \}$ with n-dim integer coordinates
- With lattice $L$ ([[Subgroup]] of $R^{n}$) with $d \leq n$
- LLL calculates reduced nearly orthogonal lattice basis
