Decimals are base-10 numbers. They are like [[Fractions]] where you can have parts of a whole number, but they are represented differently.

- Decimal numbers can go on to infinite digits
# Fraction -> Decimal
oftentimes you would just use the calculator for this
# Decimal places
since decimals can have infinite digits, we often shorthand them to some certain digit place.
for example. 1.3333 -> 1.3
