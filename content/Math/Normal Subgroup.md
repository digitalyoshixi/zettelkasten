# Definition
A [[Subgroup]] $N$ of $G$ that is [[Invariant]] under [[Conjugation]] by members of the same [[Group]] $G$.
# Formal definition
A subgroup $N$ is a normal subgroup $\Longleftrightarrow gng^{-1} \in N, \forall g \in G, n \in N$
# Subgroup Notation
$N \unlhd G$
