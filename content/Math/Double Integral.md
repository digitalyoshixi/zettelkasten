A [[Multiple Integral]] with two variables in $\mathbb{R}^{2}$.
# Example
$$\int_{0}^{1} \int_{0}^{y}(x^{2}-2xy+y^{2})dxdy$$
$$=\int_{0}^{1}(\frac{x^{3}}{3} + x^{2}y + xy^{2})|_{0}^{y}dy$$
$$=\int_{0}^{1}(\frac{y^{3}}{3} -y^{3} + y^{3})dy$$
$$=\int_{0}^{1}(\frac{y^{3}}{3})dy$$
$$=(\frac{y^{4}}{12})|_{0}^{1}$$
$$=\frac{1}{12}$$
