# Theorem
- Suppose that $f: \mathbb{R}^{n}\to \mathbb{R}$ is [[Differentiability|Differentiable]] at $x_{0}$
- Suppose $\nabla f(x_{0}) \neq 0$
- Let $c= f(x_{0})$
- Consider [[Level Sets|Level Surface]] $L_{c}= \{ x : c = f(x) \} \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$
- Then, the [[Gradient]] $\nabla f(x_{0})$ is perpendicular to the tangent plane $L_{c}$ at $x_{0}$
