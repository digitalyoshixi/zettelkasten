Any set $F$ that has the properties of
1. [[Associative|Associativity]] of [[Addition]], [[Multiplication]]
2. [[Commutative|Commutivity]] of [[Addition]], [[Multiplication]] 
3. [[Additive Identity]], [[Multiplicative Identity]]
4. [[Additive Inverse]]
5. [[Multiplicative Inverse]]
6. [[Distributive|Distributivity]] of [[Multiplication]] under [[Addition]]
# Examples
- [[Real Number]]
- [[Rational Number]]
- [[Complex Number]] (As its a superset of [[Real Number]])
# Non-Examples
- [[Natural Number]] (No additive inverses)
- [[Integers]] (No multiplicative inverses)
