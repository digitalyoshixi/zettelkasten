The sum of 2 or more functions can be found by adding the ordinates(y-coordinates) of the function at each abscissa(x-coordinate)

$x=y_{functionA}+y_{functionB}$

applies for differences too
$x=y_{functionA}-y_{functionB}$

The domain of the sum of the difference of functions is the domain common to the component functions
