Statistics is the gathering, organization, analysis and presentation of numerical information.
It is pretty much Experimental [[Probability]]

**raw data** - unprocessed information collected for study
**variable** - quantity being measured
- **continuous** - any quantity that has a value between a given range
- **discrete** - separate concrete values. has a hard cap. Not always integer. eg. shoe sizes(7, 7.5, 8)
[[Parameters vs Statistics]]
# Displaying
[[Frequency Table]] - convenient overview of the distribution of values of the variables
[[Histogram]] - bar graph with no gaps but the area of the bars are proportional to frequencies of the values of the variable
[[Relative Frequency]] - same information as histogram/bar graph but as a line graph.
[[Cumulative Frequency]] - cumulative version of frequency. used for niche situations
