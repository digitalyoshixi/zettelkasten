# pH Scale
the term pH represents the expression "the power of Hydrogen"
pH

Going bottom, more basic(alkaline), going up, more acidic

| pH  | scale |
| --- | ----- |
| 0   | 1     |
| 1   | 0.1   |
| 2   | 0.01  |
| 3   | 0.001 |

##### pH Formula
$pH=-\log(H^+)$
##### pH Example
1. eggs with pH of 8 is _ times as alkaline as milk of magnesia pH of 10.5
eggs is more acidic
$10^{-(10.5-8)}$

2. A patient with urine pH 5.7. what was hydrogen concetration? 
pH 5.7 = -log(-5.7)
$H^+$ = 10^-5.7
