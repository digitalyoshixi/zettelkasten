# Definition
A linear transform $T: V\to V$ is nilpotent $\Longleftrightarrow$ $\exists n > 1$ s.t $\forall v \in V, T^n(v) = 0$
# Example
With matrix
$$
A = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
2 & 1\\
0 & 2\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
The nilpotent
$$
N = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
0 & 1\\
0 & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
