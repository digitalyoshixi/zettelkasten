# Theorem
1. With $V$ as a [[Inner Product Space]]
2. With $W$ as a finite [[Subspace]] $W \subset V$
3. With $E$ as the [[Orthogonal Projection]] of $V$
4. With [[Orthogonal Set|Orthogonal Basis]] $\beta \subset W$
5. $(I-E) : \beta \to \beta - E(\beta)$ is the orthogonal projection of $V$ onto $W^{\perp}$
# Intuition
$(I-E)$ is the orthogonal projection of $V$ onto $W^{\perp}$
$range(I-E) = W^{\perp}$
