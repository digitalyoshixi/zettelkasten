This is a matrix $E^{i,j}$ where a $1$ is placed on every $(i,j)$ cell starting with $i=0$.
# Example
$$E^{i+1,i} = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
0 & 0 & 0& 0& 0& 0& \dots\\
1 & 0 & 0& 0& 0& 0& \dots\\
0 & 1 & 0& 0& 0& 0& \dots\\
0 & 0 & 1& 0& 0& 0& \dots\\
0 & 0 & 0& 1& 0& 0& \dots\\
\vdots
\end{array}\right]$$
