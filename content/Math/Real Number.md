All numbers that lie on the numberline.
Real Numbers are [[Completeness|Complete]]
# Definition
Let $a,b \in R$
Then $ab=0$ if and only if $a=0$ or $b=0$
Then $ab \neq 0$ if and only if $a \neq 0$ and $b \neq 0$
### Alternate Definition
$\forall x, x^2 \geq 0$
# Properties of Real Numbers
$$\frac{a}{b}+\frac{c}{d}=\frac{ad+bc}{bd}$$
$$\frac{a}{b}-\frac{c}{d}=\frac{ad-bc}{bd}$$
$$(\frac{a}{b})(\frac{c}{d})=\frac{ac}{bd}$$
$$\frac{\frac{a}{b}}{\frac{c}{d}} = (\frac{a}{b})(\frac{d}{c})=\frac{ad}{bc}$$
