A [[Multiple Integral]] with 3 variables over $\mathbb{R}^{3}$.
# Triple Integral as Riemann Sum
For bounded function $f :\mathbb{R}^{3}\to \mathbb{R}$, we define:
$$
\int\int\int_{R} f dV = \lim_{ n \to \infty } \sum_{i,j,k=0}^{n-1}f(c_{ijk}) \triangle x \triangle y \triangle z
$$
