Where $$
A = \left[\begin{array}{cc}
a & b\\
c & d
\end{array}\right]
$$
$$A^{T} = 
\left[\begin{array}{cc} 
a & c\\ 
b & d\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
# Properties
1. $(AB)^{T} = B^{T}A^{T}$
2. $(A+B)^{T} = A^{T} + B^{T}$
3. $(A)^{T^{T}} = A$
4. $rank(A) = rank(A^{T})$
