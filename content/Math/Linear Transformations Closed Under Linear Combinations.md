# Theorem
- $V,W$ be vector spaces
- $S: V\to W$ be a [[Linear Transform|Linear Map]]
- $T : V \to W$ be a [[Linear Transform|Linear Map]]
- Any linear combination of $S$, $T$ is also a [[Linear Transform|Linear Map]] from $V \to W$ ($\forall a,b \in \mathbb{F}, aS+bT : V\to W$)
