With $\langle \cdot | \cdot \rangle$ as an [[Standard Inner Product for Vector Spaces|Inner Product]] of $V$, we can define a [[Norm]]
$| | v | | = \sqrt{ \langle v | v \rangle }, \forall v \in V$.
Note that this is only possible if $|| \cdot ||$ is a [[Nice Inner Product|Nice Norm]]
