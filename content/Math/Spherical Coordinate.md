# Definition
A point $(p, \theta, \phi)$ where [[Cartesian Coordinate]] $(x,y,z) = (p \sin \phi \cos \theta, p \sin \phi \sin \theta, p \cos \phi)$
Where:
- $0 \leq p$ - defined as distance from origin or 3D radius
- $0 \leq \theta \leq 2\pi$ - defined as distance from positive x-axis to y-axis
- $0 \leq \phi \leq \pi$ - defined as distance measured from positive z-axis to p
