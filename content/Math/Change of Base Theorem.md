This is COB - Change Of Base
Any logarithm can be expressed in terms of common logarithm
# Theorem
$\frac{\log t}{\log b} = \log_{b}t=x$
# Proof
$x=\log_{b}m$
1. Rewrite as an exponential
$b^x=m$
2. log both sides
$\log b^x=\log m$
3. Power law. bring the moon down to earth
$x\log b=\log m$
4. solve for x
x = $\frac{\log_{anybase} m}{\log_{anybase} b}$
# Example
$\log_{2}(9)=x$
$x=\frac{\log_{9}}{\log_{2}}$
you can leave it in that form, or turn it into decimal
$x=3.16992500144$
