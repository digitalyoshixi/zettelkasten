An expectation of value $E(X)$ means the expectation or average of a given [[Random Variable]]
Probability has everything to do with expectations.
Thought of as the weighted center of a [[Probability Distribution]].
# Properties
- [[Linearity]] $E(aX+bY+c) = aE(X)+bE(Y)+c$
- Expectation of constant is constant $E(c) = c$
- Expectation once calculated is a constant $E(E(X))=E(X)$
- [[Independent Events|Independent]] $X \bot Y \implies E(XY)=E(X)E(Y)$
- $E(X - E(X)) = 0, \forall X$
