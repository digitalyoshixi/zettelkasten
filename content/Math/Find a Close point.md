A Method of finding the instant rate of change through looking at close points to your tangent point.

![[Pasted image 20230914111730.png]]
We find 2 points and get the [[Average Rate of Change|AROC]] of the 2 points.
![[Pasted image 20230914112004.png]]
our points are: (4.99,0.01) , (5.01,-0.01)
'IROC' = (5.01 - 4.99) / (-0.01 - 0.01)
		  = (0.2)/(-0.2)
		  = -1
Works in this case, but this method will **Never** be accurate
