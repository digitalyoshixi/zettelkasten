***THIS IS NOT VERY USEFUL IF YOU ARE COMPETENT AT ALL WITH RATIOS***

CAST rule is a method for determining the sign of each trigonometric ratio very quickly.
It is arranged like such:
![[CAST Rule-20231025032600317.webp|369]]
C: only cosine is positive
A: all are positive
S: only sine is positive
T: only tan is positive
