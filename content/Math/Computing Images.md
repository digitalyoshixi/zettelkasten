Note that the image is the span of all [[Pivot Columns]].
1. Convert the [[Matrix Transformation]] into a [[Augmented Matrix]]
2. Solve to [[Echelon Form|REF]] form
3. Find the [[Pivot Columns]]
4. Match those pivot columns to the corresponding column in the original matrix transformation. Those [[Column Vector]]s will be the basis columns that span the image
