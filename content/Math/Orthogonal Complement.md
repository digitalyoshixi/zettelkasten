A subspace perpendicular to another subspace.
It is a [[Perpendicular Set]], but for [[Subspace|Subspaces]]
# Definition
The orthogonal complement $W^{\perp} $of a subspace $W \subset V$ is the subspace $W^{\perp} = \{ v \in V | \langle v | w \rangle \forall w \in W \}$
