# Defn
- Let $a,r \in \mathbb{R}$, $a \neq 0$
- A series of the form $a + ar + ar^{2} + \dots = \sum_{n=i}^{\infty}ar^{n}$ is a geometric series where:
	- $r$ is the ratio
	- $ar^{i}$ is the base term
