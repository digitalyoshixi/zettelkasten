The standard basis set when given $n \in N$ is:
$\{ (1,0,\dots,0), (0,1,\dots,0), \dots (0,0,\dots_{1}) \} \subset R^{n}$
Where:
- $o$ is the vector in $R^{n}$ with $1$ in position $i$ and 0 everywhere else
# Example
$n=3$ standard basis is:
$\{ (1,0,0), (0,1,0), (0,0,1) \}$
