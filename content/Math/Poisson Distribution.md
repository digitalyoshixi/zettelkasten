- [[Random Variable|RV]] being $\text{\# of succcesses}$ in a continuous space

# PMF
$$P(X=x)=\frac{\lambda^{x}}{x!}e^{-\lambda}, x = 0,1,2,\dots$$
- $\lambda>0$ represents the average $\text{\# of success}$ over a given interval
# Expectation
$E(X) = \lambda$
# Variance
$\sigma^{2} = \lambda$
