# Definition
A [[Polynomial]] $p \in \mathbb{F}[x]$ is a prime polynomial if:
- $p \neq 0$
- $\not \exists f \in \mathbb{F}[x]$ s.t $fp = 1$
- if $p | ab$, then $p | a$ or $p | b$
Otherwise, it is reducible
