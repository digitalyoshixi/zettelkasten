# Theorem
- With $W$ as a vector space
- With $W^{\perp}$ as the [[Perpendicular Set|Perpendicular Set]]
- With a basis $\{ x_{1},\dots,x_{n} \}$ as a [[Basis]] for $W^{\perp}$
- Then, [[Annihilator]] $W^{o} = \{ f_\alpha \in \mathcal{L}(\mathbb{R}^{n}, \mathbb{R}) | \alpha \in span \{ x_{1},\dots,x_{n} \}\}$
