-For:
- [[Quantifier]] $Q$ 
- [[Logical Predicate]] $E$
Then:
1. $E \wedge Qx F\equiv Qx(E \wedge F)$ if $x$ is not [[Free Predicate Variable]] in $E$
2. $E \vee Qx F\equiv Qx(E \vee F)$ if $x$ is not [[Free Predicate Variable]] in $E$
3. $Qx E \wedge F\equiv Qx(E \wedge F)$ if $x$ is not [[Free Predicate Variable]] in $F$
4. $Qx E \vee F\equiv Qx(E \vee F)$ if $x$ is not [[Free Predicate Variable]] in $F$
5. $Qx E \to F\equiv \overline Qx(E \to F)$ if $x$ is not [[Free Predicate Variable]] in $F$
6. $E \to QxF\equiv Qx(E \to F)$ if $x$ is not [[Free Predicate Variable]] in $E$
