A shorthand notation for defining [[Geometric Series]] of numbers.
$0.999\dots$
Every decimal expansion will converge to the corresponding [[Real Number]]. by [[Geometric Series Convergence|GS Test]]
# All Converge Formula
For $0.769413765913\dots$
This will converge to $= \frac{765913}{999999}$
