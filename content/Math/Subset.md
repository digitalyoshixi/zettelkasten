# [[Linear Algebra]]
A subset $S$ of $R^n$ is a collection of vectors in $R^n$
### Example
$S$ is a subset of $R^4$ where $S = \{(x,y,z,w) \in R^4;x-w=3\}$
Examples of vectors in $S$ include:
- $(1,2,3,-2)$
- $(6,6,6,6)$
