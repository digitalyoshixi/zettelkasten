# Density Of Rational in Rational Numbers
Given two rational numbers, you can always find a rational number between the two.
This is easy to prove, just say $x,y$ are two different rational numbers then the rational number $\frac{x+y}{2}$ is between them
# Density Of Rational In the Real Numbers
$Q$ (A [[Rational Number]]) is dense in $R$ for every 2 real numbers $a,b$ with $a < b$. There exists a rational number $r$ satisfying $a < r < b$

# Density Of Irrational in Real Numbers
