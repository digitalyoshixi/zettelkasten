A function $f(x)$ is [[Definite Integral Riemann Sum Definition|Integrable]] on a closed interval $[a,b]$ if:
- $f(x)$ is continuous on $[a,b]$
- $f(x)$ has a finite # of [[Jump Discontinuities]] on $[a,b]$ (Derived from [[Definite Integral]])
