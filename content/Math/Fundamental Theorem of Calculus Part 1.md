# Theorem
- Let $a,b \in R$, $a<b$
- If $f$ is cont on $[a,b]$
- $F$ is any [[Antiderivative]] of $f$ on $[a,b]$
Then,
$$\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(x) |_{b}^{a}=F(b)-F(a)$$
