# Tangent Line To Secant Formula
$$y=mx + b$$
$$y= \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}(x-a)+f(a)$$
### Derivation
1. $m= \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$
2. Remember that also $m = \frac{y-y_{2}}{x-x_{2}}$
	1. $y-y_{2}=m(x-x_{2})$
	2. $y=m(x-x_{2})+y_{2}$
	3. $y=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}(x-a)+f(a)$
