A common notation used in [[Abstract Algebra]].
# Permutation of 3 Entries
- $(ijk)$ means:
	- $i$-th entry moves to $j$-th entry
	- $j$-th entry moves to $k$-th entry
	- $k$-th entry moves to $i$-th entry
# Single Swap
- $(ij)$ := swap $i$ and $j$
# Chain of Swaps
- $(kl)(ij)$ := swap $i$ and $j$ and then swap $k$ and $l$
