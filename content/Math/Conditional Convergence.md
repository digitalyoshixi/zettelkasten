# Definition
- Let $\sum a_{n}$ be a [[Series]]
- $\sum a_{n}$ conditionally converges $\Longleftrightarrow \sum |a_{n}|$ diverges, but $\sum a_{n}$ converges
Or, in other words, our series [[Series Convergence|Converges]] but does not [[Absolute Convergence|Absolute Converge]]
# Properties
### Rearrangement Property
- If $\sum a_{n}$ conditionally converges
- Then, $\forall r \in \mathbb{R}$ there exists an arrangement of $\sum a_{n}$ s.t $\sum a_{n} = r$
