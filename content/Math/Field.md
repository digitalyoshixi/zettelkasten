A [[Principle Ideal Domain]] that has a [[Multiplicative Inverse]].
# Definition
A field is a set $F$ that has closure under:
- [[Addition]]
- [[Multiplication]]
With axioms of
1. [[Additive Identity]] ($\exists 0_{\mathbb{F}} \text{ s.t } \forall a \in \mathbb{F}, 0_{\mathbb{F}} + a = a$)
2. [[Additive Inverse]] ($\forall a \in \mathbb{F}, \exists -a \in\mathbb{F} \text{ s.t } (a) + (-a) = 0_{\mathbb{F}}$)
3. [[Associative|Associativity]] of [[Addition]] ($\forall a, b, c \in \mathbb{F}, (a+b) + c = a + (b+c)$)
4. [[Commutative|Commutivity]] of [[Addition]] $\forall a,b \in \mathbb{F}, a +b=b+a$
5. [[Multiplicative Identity]] ($\exists 1_{\mathbb{F}} \in \mathbb{F} \text{ s.t }  \forall a \in \mathbb{F}, 1_{\mathbb{F}} * a = a$)
6. [[Multiplicative Inverse]] ($\forall a \in \mathbb{F}, \exists \frac{1}{a}$ s.t $a * \frac{1}{a} = 1$)
7. [[Associative]] with [[Multiplication]] $\forall a,b,c \in \mathbb{F}, ax_{\mathbb{F}}(bx_{\mathbb{F}}c) = (ax_{\mathbb{F}}b)x_{\mathbb{F}}c$
8. [[Commutative|Commutivity]] of [[Multiplication]] $\forall a,b \in \mathbb{F}, a x_{\mathbb{F}} = b x_{\mathbb{F}}a$
9. [[Distributive|Distributivity]] over [[Addition]] $\forall a,b,c \in \mathbb{F}, ax_{\mathbb{F}}(b +_{\mathbb{R}} c) + (ax_{\mathbb{F}}b) +_{\mathbb{F}} (ax_{\mathbb{F}}c)$
