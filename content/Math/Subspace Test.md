When given a set $W$ that is a [[Subset]] of [[Vector Space]] $S$
1. $\vec{0} \in W$
2. S is [[Closed Under Addition]] ($\vec{x},\vec{y} \in W \implies \vec{x}+\vec{y} \in W$ )
3. S is [[Closed Under Scalar Multiplication]] ($\vec{x} \in S,k \in R \implies k \vec{x} \in W$)
Or, you could group it to be:
1. $\overrightarrow{0} \in W$
2. $\overrightarrow{x}, \overrightarrow{y} \in W \implies \overrightarrow{x} + \overrightarrow{y} \in W$
