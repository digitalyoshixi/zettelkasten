# Definition
A finite [[Sequence]] of listed numbers.
# Types
### N-Tuple
A tuple $\{ 1,\dots,n \}$ with size $n$
### Singleton (1-Tuple)
A tuple $\{ a \}$ with a single item
### Pair (2-Tuple)
A tuple $\{ a,b \}$ with two items
### Triple (3-Tuple)
A tuple $\{ a,b,c \}$ with three items
