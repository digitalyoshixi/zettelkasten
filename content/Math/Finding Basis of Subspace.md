# Procedure
1. Prove that the [[Subspace]] $S$ is a subspace with the [[Subspace Test]]
2. Sub in for the required amount of free variables to solve the equation, continue until you cannot find any more vectors that are linearly independent.
# Alternate Procedure
1. For a given vector space $V$
2. Pick arbitrary $x \in V$, and express it as a combination of basic vectors
3. Create that set $S$ of those basic vectors
