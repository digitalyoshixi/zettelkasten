Is an event with a probability that adjusts based off previous events.
For example, it is more likely to storm right after windy day.

### Multiplicative rule
For [[Compound Events]], the multiplicative rule is:
P(A and B) = P(A) \* P(B|A)
P(B|A) is probability of B given A.

[[Conditional Probability]] is based off dependent events
