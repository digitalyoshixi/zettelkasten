$\forall x,y \in R, x,y>0 \Rightarrow \exists n \in N$ s.t $nx > y$
In other words, is a natural number $n$ when multiplied by $x$ is greater than $y$.
# Other Thoughts
Given a large body of water, and you have a small cup of water. If you drop $n$ cups of water, you will eventually match that large body of water.
# Reversed Archimedian Property
$\forall y \in R, y> 0 \Rightarrow \exists k \in N$ s.t $\frac{1}{k} \leq y$
