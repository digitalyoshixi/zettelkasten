Suppose $(V,\boxplus,\boxdot)$ and $(W,\oplus, \odot)$
are vector spaces.
A linear transformation / map / function of $T : V\to W$ s.t
$$T(v \boxplus v) = T(u)\oplus T(v) $$
$$T(a \boxdot v) = a \odot T(v)$$
