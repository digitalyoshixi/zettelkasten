Angular velocity of an object is given as the amount of rotation around a central point per unit of time. The amount of ration may be a expression of degrees or radians
# Revolution In degrees / radians
**To degrees:** revolutions \* 360
**To radians:** revolutions \* 2pi
Does this make sense?
# Example
A fan makes 60 rotations in 5 seconds. what is its angular velocity?
##### Solution
12 rotations a second.
To radians, a rotation is 2pi.
so 12\*2pi = 24pi
