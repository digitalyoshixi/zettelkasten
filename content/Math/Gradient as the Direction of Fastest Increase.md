# Theorem
- Suppose that $f : \mathbb{R}^{n}\to \mathbb{R}$ is [[Differentiability|Differentiable]] at $x_{0}$
- Suppose $\nabla f(x_{0}) \neq 0$
- The unit vector $n = \frac{\nabla f(x_{0})}{|| \nabla f(x_{0})||}$ maximizes $Df(x_{0})$
### Intuition
$Df(x_{0})n = \nabla f(x_{0})*n=||\nabla f(x_{0})|| ||n||\cos \theta = ||\nabla f(x_{0}) ||\cos \theta$
And, $\cos(\theta)$ maximized at $\theta = 0$
