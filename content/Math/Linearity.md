The properties of [[Sigma Notation]]
### Distributive
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}(a_{k} + b_{k}) = \sum_{k=1}^{n}(a_{k}) + \sum_{k=1}^{n}(b_{k})$$
### Constant Multiple
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}c(a_{k}) = c\sum_{k=1}^{n}(a_{k})$$
### Splitting Sum
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}(a_{k}) = \sum_{k=1}^{l-1}(a_{k}) + \sum_{k=l}^{n}(a_{k})$$
Where $1<l\leq n$
