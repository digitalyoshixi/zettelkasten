# Definition
- Suppose that $S : V \to W$ is a function
- A inverse of $S$ is $T : W \to V$ s.t
	- $\forall v \in V, TS(v) = v$
	- $\forall w \in W, ST(w) = w$
### Uniqueness Definition
$T$ invertible $\Longleftrightarrow \forall b, Tv = b$ has a [[Unique Existence|Unique Solution]]
