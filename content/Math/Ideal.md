An [[Additive Subgroup]] ($I,+$) of a [[Ring]] where:
- $\forall r \in R, \forall x \in I, rx \in I$
# Showing Ideals
1. Show non-empty
2. Show that $\forall r \in R, \forall x \in I, rx \in I$
# Finding Singleton
For a given ideal generated by: $\langle a_{1}, a_{2},\dots \rangle$, we get that the singleton is: $gcd(a_{1},a_{2},\dots)$
# Examples
### Showing Even Integers are a Ideal
- Let $x \in \{  \text{even numbers}\}, x = 2k, k \in \mathbb{Z}$
- Let $r \in \mathbb{Z}, \text{ then }, rx = r(2k) = 2(rk)$
- Thus, $rx \in \{  \text{even numbers} \}$
