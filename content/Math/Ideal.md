An [[Additive Subgroup]] ($I,+$) of a [[Ring]] where:
- $\forall r \in R, \exists x \in I, rx \in I$
# Showing Ideals
1. Show non-empty
2. Show that $\forall r \in R, \exists x \in I, rx \in I$
# Concepts
- [[Generator for Ideal]]
- [[Trivial Ideal]]
- [[Generator for Ideal Intersection]]
- [[Generator for Ideal Sum]]
- [[Principle Ideal]]
# Examples
### Showing Even Integers are a Ideal
- Let $x \in \{  \text{even numbers}\}, x = 2k, k \in \mathbb{Z}$
- Let $r \in \mathbb{Z}, \text{ then }, rx = r(2k) = 2(rk)$
- Thus, $rx \in \{  \text{even numbers} \}$
