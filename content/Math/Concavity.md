# Concavity
The polarity of the second interval.
### Concave
- $f''(x) > 0$
- All secant lines on the graph are above the graph
- Slopes increasing from left to right

### Convex
- $f''(x)<0$
- All secant lines on the graph are below the graph
- Slopes decreasing from left to right

# Concavity Test
Suppose both f and $f'$ are differentiable on interval I.
- If $f''$ is positive on I, then $f$ is concave up
- If $f''$ is negative on I, then $f$ is concave down
### Proof
$f''$ positive $\implies$ $f'$ increasing by (inc/dec test) $\Longleftrightarrow$ f concave up
