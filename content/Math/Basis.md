The basis $X$ for $U$ is a set such that:
- $X$ is [[Linear Independence|Linearly Independent]]
- $X$ is a [[Spanning Set]] of $U$. ($U = span(X)$)

# Basis Vector Notation
$e_{n}$ where $n$ is the basis vector in question.
$e_{1} = (1,0,\dots ,0)$
