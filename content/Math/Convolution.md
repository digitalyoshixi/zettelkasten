A operation $f * g$ between two [[Function|Functions]] $f$ and $g$ that produces a [[List]] of elements.
It is the integral of the product of the two functions after one is reflected about $y$-axis and shifted.
# Definition
$$(a * b)_{n} = \sum_{^{+}i,j,i+j=n}a_{i}\cdot b_{j}$$
