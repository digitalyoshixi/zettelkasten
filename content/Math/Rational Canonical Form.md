# Definition
1. With $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$
2. Then. $[T]_{\beta}$ is the rational canonical form if:
   $$
  [T] _{\beta} = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
C(p_{1}) & 0 & \dots & 0\\
0 & C(p_{2}) & \ddots & \vdots\\ 
\vdots & \ddots & \ddots & 0\\
0 & \dots & 0 & C(p_{j})
\end{array}\right]
   $$
   Where $C(p_{i})$ are [[Matrix Definition of Cyclic Subspace|Companion Matrix]] of polynomial $p_{i}$
