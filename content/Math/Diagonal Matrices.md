A matrix $D= [d_{ij}] \in M_{n \times n}(\mathbb{R})$ is diagonal if:
- $d_{ij} = 0$ when $i \neq j$
- The determinant of a diagonal matrix is $\det(D) = d_{11}d_{22}\dots d_{nn}$
# Niceness of Diagonal Matrices
![[Diagonal Matrices-20250312133531910.webp]]
The formula for a matrix that is all diagonal will be $d_{11} * d_{22} * \dots *d_{nn}$ always.
