# Example 1
8 pumpkins with weight: `11kg, 13kg, 15kg, 18kg, 12kg, 14kg, 10kg, 16kg`
masses are normally distributed with a mean of `14.2kg` and a standard deviation of `2.5`. determine a 90% confidence interval for the sample mean

### Solution
$\mu = 13.625$
$z=1.645$
$\sigma_{\overline x}= \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{ n }}$
$\sigma_{\overline x}=0.88$
$E=z\sigma_{\overline x}$
	= $1.4476$
interval is: $13.625 \pm 1.4476$
				$12.1774 < p < 15.0726$
