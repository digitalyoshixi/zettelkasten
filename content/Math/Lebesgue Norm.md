# Definition
- With $p$ as a positive [[Real Number]]
- With $V = \mathbb{F}^{n}$
- With $\mathbb{F} = \mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$
- Let $x = (x_{1}, \dots, x_{n})$
- Then, $||x||_{p} = \sqrt[p]{ \sum_{i=1}^{n}|x_{i}|^{p} }$
