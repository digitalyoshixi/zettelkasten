A finite [[Discrete Sample Space]] with outcomes of equal probability.
# Definition
- $S = \{ s_{1},\dots,s_{n} \}$ where $n=|S|$
- $P(s_{i}) = \frac{1}{|S|} > 0, \forall i\in[1,n]$
# Finite Probability Formula
- For [[Event]] $A$
- $P(A)= \frac{|A|}{|S|}$
