# Example 1
The driving time between Toronto and North Bay is found to be between 195 and 240 minutes. what is the probability that the drive will take **less** than 210 minutes?
### Diagram
![[Continuous Probability Problems-20231127130215512.webp|357]]
we need to find out that ratio of the rectangle from 195 to 210 in relation to the entire rectangle
that is 1/45.
so, each of these times have a value of 1/45
![[Continuous Probability Problems-20231127130312168.webp|471]]
so, to find the probability it is the probability of the area from 195 to 210
$area_{195\to210}=\frac{15}{45}$
so the probability is 1/3
