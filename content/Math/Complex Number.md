The algebraically closed set that follows:
$z=a+bi$ where $i$ is the [[Imaginary Number]]
- $a$ is the [[Real Number]] part of the complex number ($a = Re(z)$ where $z$ is the complex number)
- $bi$ is the imaginary segment
# Identities
### Additive Identity
$\hat{0}=0+0i$
### Multiplicative Identity
$1=1+0i$
