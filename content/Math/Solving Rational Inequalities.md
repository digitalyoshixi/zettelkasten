Since you cannot cross multiply, the only option to finish an inequality when there are variables on both sides is by getting common denominators.

### Example
$\frac{2}{x-5} < -10$
$\frac{2}{x-5} - 10 < 0$
$\frac{2 - 10(x-5)}{x-5} < 0$
$\frac{2 - 10x + 50}{x - 5} < 0$
$\frac{-10x + 52}{x-5} < 0$
Get the Critical Values(When numerator is 0, when denominator is 0)
Critical values are: x=5, x = 26/5
