These are 3 matrixes used in [[Quantum Mechanics]] that are [[Self Adjoint|Hermitian Matrixes]]
# Matrixes
### $I = \sigma_{0}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 0\\
0 & 1\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
### $X = \sigma_{1}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
0 & 1\\
1 & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
### $Y = \sigma_{2}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
0 & -i\\
i & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$

### $Z = \sigma_{3}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 0\\
0 & -1\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
