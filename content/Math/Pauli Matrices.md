These are 3 matrixes used in [[Quantum Mechanics]].
# $\sigma_{1}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
0 & 1\\
1 & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
# $\sigma_{2}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
0 & -i\\
i & 0\\
\end{array}\right]
$$

# $\sigma_{3}$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 0\\
0 & -1\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
