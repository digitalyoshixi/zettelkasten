# Method
- For [[Discrete Random Variable|Discrete RV]] $X,Y$
- For [[Joint Probability Mass Function|Joint PMF]] $p_{X,Y}(x,y)$
- To calculate [[Joint Probability Mass Function|Joint PMF]] $Z = h_{1}(X,Y), W = h_{2}(X,Y)$
$$p_{Z,W} = P(Z=z, W=w) = \sum_{(x,y) \in h^{-1}}p_{X,Y}(x,y)$$
