Test points are a method of determining how to graph an oblique asymptote.
They are used during the stage where you already have the V.A and O.A, and you are looking to how to graph the lines out.
You feed test values that are smaller or bigger than your V.A, then plot them into your formula, then test to see which O.A it fits inside.

$\frac{2x^2-x+1}{x-2}$
