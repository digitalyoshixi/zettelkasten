A measure for the rate of change for a [[Subroutine|Function]] with one input and one output.
Derived from the [[Difference Quotient]].
# Notations
### Leibniz Notation
$\frac{dy}{dx}f(x)$
OR
$\frac{d^ny}{dx^n}f(x)$
OR
$\frac{d^n}{dx^n}f(x)$
OR
$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\triangle y}{\triangle x}f(x)$
OR
$\frac{dy}{dx}|_{x=a,y=b}$
### Prime
$f'(x)$
