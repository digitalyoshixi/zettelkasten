Subcases are [[Cases]] within cases, that may occur oftentimes.
# Example Question
How many 4 letter 'words' can you make from the letters of the word URANIUM that begins with a vowel.

You must divide it into cases. Starts with a vowel, and also pay attention to the fact that there are 2 U's. 

Case 1: Starts with I
Case 1.1: Starts with I AND No U
Case 1.2: Starts with I AND One U
Case 1.3: Starts with I AND Two U
Case 2: Starts with A
Case 2.1: Starts with A AND No U
Case 2.2: Starts with A AND One U
Case 2.3: Starts with A AND Two U
Case 3: Starts with U
Case 3.1: Starts with U AND One U
Case 3.2: Starts with U AND Two U
