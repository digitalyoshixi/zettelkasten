The [[Math/Set]] containing all possible [[Outcome|Outcomes]].
Denoted as $S$ or $\Omega$ 
# Example
Sample space of rolling a dice is: $S = \{ 1,2,3,4,5,6 \}$
# Example Question
### Question
Describe the sample space of an insect that can lay 1-3 eggs with each egg being either male or female.
### Soln
$$
S = \{  \{ M,M,M \}, \{ M,M,F \}, \{ M,F,F \}, \{ F,F,F \}, \{ M,M \}, \{ M,F \}, \{ F, F \}, \{  M \}, \{  F \} \}
$$
