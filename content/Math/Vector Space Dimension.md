The dimension of a given [[Vector Space]] $U$, $dim(U)$ is the size of its [[Basis]]. This will always remain consistent by [[All Bases are Same Size Theorem|Invariance Theorem]].
# Dimension and Span-ability
If you have a subspace $S$ of $m$ vectors and $k = dim(U)$
- If $m > k$, $S$ is linearly dependent
- If $m < k$, then $S$ does not span $U$
