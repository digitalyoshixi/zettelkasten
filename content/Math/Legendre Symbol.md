A [[Multiplicative Function]] comprised of $a, p \in \mathbb{Z}$
$$
(\frac{a}{p}) = \begin{cases}
1 & \text{ if a is a quadratic residue modulo p and a} \not \equiv \mod p \\
-1 & \text{ if a is a quadratic nonresidue modulo p}\\ 
0 & \text{ if a } \equiv 0 \mod p
\end{cases}
$$
