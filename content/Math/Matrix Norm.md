This is a [[Norm]] for [[Matrix]]
# Definition
1. With $A$ as a [[Real Number]] or [[Complex Number]] $n \times n$ [[Matrix]]
2. Let $|| \cdot ||_{v}$ as a norm for $\mathbb{F}_{m}$
3. With $|| \cdot||_{w}$ be a norm for $\mathbb{F}_{n}$
4. Then, the matrix norm $||A||_{op} = sup \{ || Av||_{w} : ||v||v \leq 1 \}$
