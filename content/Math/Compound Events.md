A general concept. Primarily used in [[Conditional Probability]]
Combining probabilities of 2 or more events can be done very simply but done differently between [[Independent Events|Independent]] and [[Dependent Events|Dependent]] events

Consult [[Independent Events|Independent]] and [[Dependent Events|Dependent]] for more info on how to find the probability of compound events.
