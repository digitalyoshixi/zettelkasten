The expected value of an indicator is the probability of its defining event
# Theorem
- Given [[Indicator Random Variable|Indicator RV]], for some $A \subset S$
$$
I_{A} = \begin{cases}
1 & s \in A\\
0 & s \not\in A\\
\end{cases}
$$
- $E(I_{A})=P(A)$
