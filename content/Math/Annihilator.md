The set of [[Math/Conductor|Conductors]] that will map all vectors in a vector space to 0
# Definition
- Let $S$ be a [[Subset]] of [[Vector Space]] $V$ over [[Math/Field]] $\mathbb{F}$
- The annihilator of $S$ is the set $S_{T}(\alpha; \{ 0 \}) = S^{o} = \{  T \in \mathcal{L}(V,\mathbb{F}) | T(v) = 0 ,\forall v \in S\}$
