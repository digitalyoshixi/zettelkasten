# Definition
With [[Domain|Region]] $D^{*} \subset \mathbb{R}^{2}$,
A transformation $T : D^{*} \to \mathbb{R}^{2}$ a $C^{1}$ [[Class Functions|Class Function]] of the region.
$T(x^{*}, y^{*}) = (x,y)$
- $(x^{*}, y^{*})$ input
- $(x,y)$ output
