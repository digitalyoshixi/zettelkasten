# Definition
- Suppose $T$ is [[Upper Triangular Matrices|Triangularizable]]
- Let $E_{i}$ be the [[Projection]] onto $W_{j} = ker(p_{j}(T)^{r_{j}}) = range(E_{j})$
- Then, $D = c_{1}E_{1} + \dots + c_{k}E_{k}$ is a [[Diagonal Matrices|Diagonal Matrix]], and the diagonalizable part of $T$
# Example
$$A = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
2 & 1 \\
0 & 2 \\
\end{array}\right]$$
Diagonal part is
$$
D = \left[\begin{array}{cc} 
2 & 0\\
0 & 2\\
\end{array}\right]
$$
