The property for a mathematical property to remain unchanged after operations.
For example, the area of a triangle remains the same regardless of rotations or flips.
# Definition
1. With $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$
2. With $W \subset V$ be a [[Subspace]] of $V$
3. $T$ is invariant if $T(v) \in W, \forall v \in W$
# Intuition
Anything that goes through this linear map will remain fenced in the same domain.
