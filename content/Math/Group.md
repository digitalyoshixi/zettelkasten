Similar algebraic structures that follow the axioms:
1. Associative multiplication $G \times G \to G$
2. Identity element $e \in G$ s.t $eg = ge = g$
3. For any $g \in G$, an inverse $g_{-1} \in G$ s.t $g g^{-1} = g^{-1}g = e$
