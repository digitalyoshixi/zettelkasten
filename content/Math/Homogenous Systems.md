A [[System Of Equations]] is homogeneous if all its constants are zero $(\hat{0} \in \mathbb{F})$.

Example:

$$
\[ f(x) = \begin{cases} 
          0 & x\leq 0 \\
          \frac{100-x}{100} & 0\leq x\leq 100 \\
          0 & 100\leq x 
       \end{cases}
\]
$$
$$
\lbrace
\begin{align} \\
 & \text{a+b+c=0}\\
 & \text{a-b=0}\\
 & \text{a+c=0}\\
\end{align}
$$
There is always 1 or infinitely many solutions to this
