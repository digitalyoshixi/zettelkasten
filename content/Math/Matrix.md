A 2-d representation of rows and columns to represent mathematical objects.
# Notation
$$
M_{n \times k}(R) = \begin{bmatrix} 
x_{11} & x_{21} \dots x_{kn}\\ \\
\vdots & \\

x_{1k} & x_{2k} \dots x_{nk}\\
\end{bmatrix}

$$
