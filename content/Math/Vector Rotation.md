Given a linear map $R_{e} : \mathbb{R}^{2} \to \mathbb{R}^{2}$
We can express $R_{e}(x,y)$ for a given $(x,y)$ vector as:
$$R_{e}(x,y) = (x\cos(\theta) - y\sin(\theta) , y\cos(\theta) + x\sin(\theta))$$
