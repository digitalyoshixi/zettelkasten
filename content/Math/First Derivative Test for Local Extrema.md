# Test
Suppose $U \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$ is an [[Open Set]]
Suppose $f : U \subset \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}$ is [[Differentiability|Differentiable]]
If $x_{0} \in U$ is a [[Local Extrema]], then $Df(x_{0}) = 0$
# Intuition
Local extrema and differentiable $\implies$ critical point
