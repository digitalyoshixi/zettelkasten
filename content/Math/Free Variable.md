In contrast to a [[Bound Variable]].
It can be any value, and is used directly in the statement.
Example:
The statement $y \in \{ x | is\ divisible\ by\ w \}$
- Has $y$ and $w$ as the free variables
- Has $x$ as the [[Bound Variable]]
# Matrix
In a matrix, the free variable is one that does not have a [[Pivot Columns|Pivot Column]]
In [[Echelon Form]]:
- All the variables that appear to be leading terms are basic variables. There are [[Rank]] amount of basic variables
- All variables in columns where there is no leading one are free variables
