This is a [[Norm]] for a [[Linear Transform|Linear Map]].
# Definition
1. With $V ,W$ be [[Real Number]] or [[Complex Number]] [[Vector Space|Vector Spaces]]
2. With $T : V\to W$ as a [[Linear Transform|Linear Map]]
3. With $|| \cdot ||_{v}$ as a [[Norm]] for $V$
4. With $|| \cdot || _{w}$ as a [[Norm]] for $W$
5. Then, operator norm $||T||_{op} = sup \{ ||T(v)||_{w}  : ||v||_{v} \leq 1\}$
