If $f$ is [[Continuity|Continuous]] on $[a,b]$ then $f$ is [[Bounded]] on $[a,b]$.
# Formal Definition
Using the definitions of cont and bounded,
Cont $\implies$ Bounded
($\forall \epsilon >0, \exists \delta > 0$ s.t $|x-c| < \delta \implies |f(x) - f(c)| < \epsilon$) $\implies$ ($\exists M > 0$ s.t $\forall x \in [a,b], |f(x)| \leq M$)
