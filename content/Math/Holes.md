When you have a rational function where factors of the numerator and denominator cancel out, you are given a hole. at the factor of x put into the new cancelled formula.

## Example
$f(x)=\frac{(x-3)(x+4)}{(x-7)(x+4)}$
Hole at x = -4
$f(x) = \frac{x-3}{x-7}$
$f(-4) = \frac{-4-3}{-4-7} = \frac{7}{11}$
There is a hole at $\left( -4,\frac{7}{11} \right)$
