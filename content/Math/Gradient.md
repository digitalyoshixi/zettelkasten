A [[Vector Space]] representing the rate of change for a function with multiple inputs and a single output.
Will always point to the direction of maximum increase.
# Definition
The gradient of $f : \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}$ is defined as:
$$\nabla f = Df = [\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_{1}}, \dots, \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_{n}}]$$
