3 Ms
- Mean. average = sum/num entries
- Median. middle value of an ordered set. if there is an even number of data points, the median is the average between the 2 middle values. its possible for median to not be included in the original dataset.
- Mode. most frequent value. possible to have no mode, one mode, two mode, etc...

# Outliers
Data entire that really skew the central tendencies. This would be the case where you may have a mean that is entirely offset of where the true centre lies.

In a symmetric distribution, such as a uniform distribution, you should see a bell curve. the mean, median and mode will be equal.

In a non-symmetric or skewed distribution, the mean, median and mode will differ.
