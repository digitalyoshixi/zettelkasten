The property of [[Inequality|Inequalities]] to hold for other inequalities.
# Definition
$A \equiv B \wedge B \equiv C \implies A \cong C$
# Specific Transitivity Operations
### Transitivity of $=$
- If $a = b, b= c$
- Then, $a = c$
### Transitivity of $<$
- If $a < b, b < c$
- Then, $a < c$
