# Definition
1. Let $a,b \in R$
2. Suppose $a<b$
3. Suppose $f(x)$ is bounded on $[a,b]$
4. $\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx$ exists and is equal to the lower/upper darboux sum if and only if the lower darboux sum is equal to the upper darboux sum
    $\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = sup \{ L(f,p) | \text{P is any partiton of [a,b]} \} = inf \{ U(f,p) | \text{P is any partiton of [a,b]} \}$
