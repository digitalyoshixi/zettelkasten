- 1 Means guaranteed to happen

# Probability
The probability is the [[Set Cardinality]] of an [[Event|Event]] divided by the [[Set Cardinality]] of the sample space.
$$\frac{|E|}{|S|}$$

$P(A) = E(I_{A})$
