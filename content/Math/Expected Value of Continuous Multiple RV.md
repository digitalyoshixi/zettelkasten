For [[Continuous Random Variable|Continuous RVs]] with [[Joint Probability Density Function|Joint PDF]] $f_{X,Y}(x,y)$
$$E[g(X,Y)] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}  g(x,y)f_{X,Y}(x,y)dxdy$$
Provided the integral converges
