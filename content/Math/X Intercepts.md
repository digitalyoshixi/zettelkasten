Number of times the graph passes/sits/curls on the x axis.
The minimum number of X intercepts varies.
The maximum number of X intercepts will always be up the the [[Degree]]
## Even degree polynomial
An even degree polynomial can have at least **ZERO** X Intercepts Because, not all graphs touch the x axis. it can be 0.

Additionally, it could cross the x axis all possible times. [[Degree]] times.
## Odd degree polynomials
An odd degree polynomial has at least **ONE** X intercept. Cuz domain and range are all infinite.

And again, max is the [[Degree]] number of intercepts.
