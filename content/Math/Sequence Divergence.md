A series that reaches positive or negative [[Infinity]].
# Proving Divergence
### Prove M-N for $\infty$
$\forall M > 0, \exists N > 0$ s.t $\forall n \in \mathbb{N}, n > N \implies a_{n} > M$
### Prove M-N for $- \infty$
$\forall M < 0, \exists N > 0$ s.t $\forall n \in \mathbb{N}, n > N \implies a_{n} < M$
