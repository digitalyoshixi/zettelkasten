# Counting and Probability 
n(A) = number of A
P(A) = probability of A
P(A') = Probability of A not happening
P(~A) = Probability of A not happening
P(A|B) = Probability of B given A
P(A∪B) = Probability of A and B
P(A∩B) = Probability of Intersection of A and B

A could mean anything, it is a variable so it could mean: Aphids, Apples, Player A, etc.
Its very open-ended.
