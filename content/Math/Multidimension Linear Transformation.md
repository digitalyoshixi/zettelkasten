[[Linear Transform|Linear Maps]] can have multidimensional inputs and output vectors, so long as they satisfy:
- Additivity
- Homogeneity
In their respective dimension
# 2D Linear Transformation
Where $T(x,y) : V^{2} \to V^{2}$
### Proving 2D Linear Transformation
1. Show Additivity ($\forall c \in \mathbb{F}, cT(x,y) = T(cx,cy)$)
2. Show Homogeneity ($\forall x_{1},y_{1},x_{2},y_{2} \in V, T(x_{1}+x_{2},y_{1}+y_{2}) = T(x_{1},y_{1})+T(x_{2},y_{2})$)
