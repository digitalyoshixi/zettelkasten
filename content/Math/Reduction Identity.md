Formulas to reduce the power of a expression involving cos and sin
# Formula
$\sin^2(x)=\frac{1-\cos(2x)}{2}$
$\cos^2(x)=\frac{1+\cos(2x)}{2}$
$\tan^2(x)=\frac{1-\cos(2x)}{1+\cos(2x)}$
$$\sin x \cos x = \frac{\sin(2x)}{2}$$
