Describes the probability of two [[Discrete Random Variable|Discrete RV]] being exactly equal to two values.
# Definition
$p_{X,Y}(x,y) = P(X = x, Y=y) = P(\{ {X =x} \} \cap \{ Y = y \})$
# Properties
- $p_{X,Y}(x,y) \geq 0, \forall x,y$
- $\sum_{x,y}p_{X,Y}(x,y) = 1$
