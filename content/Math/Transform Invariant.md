A subspace that is sent to itself by the linear transform.
# Definition
1. For [[Linear Transform]] $T : V \to W$
2. For a [[Subspace]] $U \subset V$
3. $U$ is $T$ invariant $\Longleftrightarrow \forall v \in U, T(v) \in U$
Or:
4. $U$ is $T$ invariant $\Longleftrightarrow Img(T) \subset U$
Or:
5. $\beta = \{ b_{1}, \dots, b_{n} \}$ is a basis of $U$
6. $T(b_{1}), \dots, T(b_{k}) \in U \implies U$ is $T$ invariant
