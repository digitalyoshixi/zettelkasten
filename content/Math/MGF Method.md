# Method
[[Moment Generating Function|MGF]] uniquely characterizes the distribution of a [[Random Variable|RV]].
For [[Random Variable|RV]] $X,Y$ with [[Moment Generating Function|MGF]] $m_{X}(t), m_{Y}(t)$
$$
m_{X}(t) =m_{Y}(t) \Longleftrightarrow X \sim Y
$$
# Intuition
If $m_{Y}(t)$ follows the [[Moment Generating Function|MGF]] of some known distribution $\implies$ $Y$ follows that distribution
