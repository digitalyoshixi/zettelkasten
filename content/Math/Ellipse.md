A mathematical structure created from two points called Foci and a distance
![[Ellipse-20250623182823440.webp|241]]
A [[Circles]] is an ellipse with two foci at the same point
# Defintion
- With Foci $A,B$
- With distance $2a$
The ellipse is the selection of points where the sum of the distances from each foci $A,B$ adds up to the distance $2a$
![[Ellipse-20250623183057765.webp]]
### Center
This is the point equidistant from both points
### Major Axis
This is the axis that crosses the two points
![[Ellipse-20250623183557715.webp|229]]
##### Semimajor Axis
Half of the major axis. Denoted as $a$
![[Ellipse-20250623183702962.webp|317]]
### Minor Axis
The axis that is perpendicular to the major axis that crosses the center
![[Ellipse-20250623183615042.webp|249]]
##### Semiminor Axis
Half of the minor axis. Denoted as $b$
![[Ellipse-20250623183717335.webp|343]]
