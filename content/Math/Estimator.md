A [[Random Variable|RV]] $T(s)$ of a sample.
The calculated numerical value of the estimator (after the samples are found) is called the estimate.
# Intuition
| Parameter $(\theta)$ | Estimator $(T)$ | Estimate $(t)$ |
| -------------------- | --------------- | -------------- |
| $\mu$                | $\overline X$   | $\overline x$  |
| $\sigma$             | $S$             | $s$            |
| Unknown constant     | Random variable | Known constant |
