On the calculator, there is the stat mode for certain statistical functions.

The mode is called `SD`

# Standard Deviation Plotting
Good thing is, you don't need to follow any order.

1. Enter a number
2. M+ button
3. It should show something like `n=1` these are the # of data points entered in.
4. Keep entering and M+ing numbers. 
	1. S-VAR(Xrepeat or mean), then enter for Mean Deviation
	2. S-VAR(sigma), then enter for SD Population
	3. S-VAR(s), then enter for SD Sample
