These are methods to find errors in message transmission and attempt to fix them.
Commonly used in [[CD Players]], [[Hard Drive|Hard Drives]] to recover lost data.
# Methods
- [[Majority Vote ECC]]
- [[Hamming Codes]]
- [[Reed Solomon Codes]]
- [[Checksum]]
- [[Cyclic Redundancy Check|CRC]]
- [[Longitudinal Redundancy Check|LRC]]
