A [[Binary Expression]] that is true.
- Should be proven true, they are not assumed to be true.
It has assumptions and a conclusion, where the true assumptions [[Conditional Statement|Imply]] the conclusion.
Very often, the hypothesis or conclusion contains free variables.
Assigning values to these free variables is called **instantiating** and **instance** of the theorem
