# Expectation Single RV Formula
$$E(X) = \sum_{X}^{}xp_{x}(x)$$
- $X$ is the given random variable
- $x$ is the given trial
- $p_{x}(x)$ is the [[Probability Mass Function|PMF]]
### Alternate Formula
$$E(X) = \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}P(X > k)$$
