# Theorem
- Let $U \in \mathbb{F}^{n \times n}$ be a [[Unitary Operator|Unitary Matrix]]
- Then, there exists a unitary matrix $V \in \mathbb{F}^{n \times n}$ such that: $V U V^{*} = diag(e^{i\theta}, \dots, e^{i \theta n})$
$U$ is unitarily similar to the diagonal matrix
