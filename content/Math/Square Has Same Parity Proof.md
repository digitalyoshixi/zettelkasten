# Proof By Contradiction
Assume $a=2k+1$ and $a^2$ is an even integer $2j$ where $k,j \in Z$
Thus, $a^2 = (2k+1)^2 = 4k^2+4k+1 = 2(2k^2+2k)+1$
Since, $a^2$ cannot be represented as an even number, $a^2$ must be odd for an odd $a$.
Thus, for $a^2$ to be even, $a$ must be even aswell.
$\square$
