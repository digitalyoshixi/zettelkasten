A matrix in the form $M_{a \times a}(R)$.
- $a_{ij} = a_{ji}$
$$Symm_{a \times a}(R) = \{ 

\left[\begin{array}{cc}
a & b \\
c & d \\
\end{array}\right]


: a,b,c,d \in R, b = c\}$$
