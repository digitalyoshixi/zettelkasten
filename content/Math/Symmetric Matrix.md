A matrix in the form $M_{a \times a}(R)$.
- They are always squares.
- The diagonals from top-right to bottom-left are equivalent
$$Symm_{a \times a}(R) = \{ 

\left[\begin{array}{cc}
a & b \\
c & d \\
\end{array}\right]


: a,b,c,d \in R, b = c\}$$
