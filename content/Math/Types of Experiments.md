# Observational Studies
Researchers look at situations that are already occurring and trying to make inferences.
[[Surveys]] are a type of observational study
# Experimental Studies
Researchers control what is going on and make inferences based on those controls
### Types of groups
**Treatment group**: participants in an experiment who receive a specific treatment being measured
**Control group:** participants in an experiment who do not receive the treatment being measured

Prerequisites are: [[Experimental Study Prerequisites]]
