Operation between two [[Subspace|Subspaces]] that only exists if they are [[Independent Subspaces]].
# Direct Sum Theorem
$W_{1} + W_{2}= W_{1} \oplus W_{2} \Longleftrightarrow W_{1} \cap W_{2} = \{ 0 \}$
# Propositions
- $dim(V) = dim(V_{1}) + \dots + dim(V_{n})$
- $\forall i,j, i \neq j, V_{i} \cap V_{j} = \{ 0 \}$
