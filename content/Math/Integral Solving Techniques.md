# Basic
### Integration by Geometry
Convert the function into several geometric separate functions that are easily evalutable like:
- Semicircle $y = \sqrt{ 1-x^2 }$
- Triangle $y=x-5$
and then, you can easily compute the integral by summing each integral of each component
### Integration by Computation
Solve the limit of integral riemann sum definition.
##### Example
$\int _{0}^1 f(x) \, dx$
$f(x) = \sqrt{ 1-x^2 }$
$\implies y^2 = 1 - x^2$
$\implies y^2 + x^2 = 1$
We know how to graph this.
This is a circle with a radius of 1 and centered at (0,0).
