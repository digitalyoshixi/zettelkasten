# Corrolary
- Let $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$
- With $\sigma = \{ c_{1},\dots,c_{n} \}$ as the [[Eigenvector|Eigenvalues]] of $T$
- With $W_{i} = ker(T - c_{i}I)$
Then, the following are equivalent:
1. $T$ is [[Diagonalizable]]
2. $W_{1} + \dots + W_n = V$
3. $W_{1} \oplus \dots \oplus  W_n = V$
