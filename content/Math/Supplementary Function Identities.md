A trig identity based on $\pi$ transpositions
### Prerequisites
In order for this identity to be valid, you must be able to discern a transposition of an angle that is of a whole # multiple of $\pi$
##### Examples
$\pi$ + $\theta$
$2\pi + \theta$
$-1\pi + \theta$
$5\pi - \theta$
$\theta -2\pi$

Theta is any angle at all
