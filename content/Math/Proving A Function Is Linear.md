# Example
Consider $T:\mathbb{R}\to \mathbb{R},T(x)= 7x$
Prove $T$ is a [[Linear Transform|Linear Transformation]]
### T Preserves addition
Pick $x,y \in \mathbb{R}$
$T(x+y) = 7(x+y)$ defn of $T$
$= 7x+7y$ as $\mathbb{R}$ is a field
$=T(x)+T(y)$ by defn of $T$
### T preserves Scaling
$T(cx) = 7(cx)$ by defn of $T$
$= c(7x)$ as $\mathbb{R}$ is a field
$=cT(x)$ by defn of $T$
