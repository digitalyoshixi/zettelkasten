Let $L_{1}, L_{2},c \in R$. Suppose that $f$ is defined on an open interval around $c$ (except possibly at $c$) if $\lim_{ x \to c} = L_{1}$ and $\lim_{ x \to c}f(x)=L_{2}$ then $L_{1}=L_{2}$.
