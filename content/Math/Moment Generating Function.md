# Definition
The MGF of [[Random Variable|RV]] $X$ is $m(t) = E(e^{tX})$
- [[Well Defined]] when $m(t)$ is finite $\forall t < \epsilon, \epsilon > 0$
# Calculating Moments
$$E(X^{k}) = m^{k}(0) = \frac{d^{k}}{dt^{k}}m(t)|_{t=0}$$
