$$F_{X,Y}(x,y) = \int_{-\infty}^{x} \int_{-\infty}^{y} f_{X,Y}(s,t)dtds, \forall x,y \in \mathbb{R}$$
Or,
$$f_{X,Y} (x,y) = \frac{\partial ^{2} }{\partial x \partial y}F_{X,Y}(x,y), \forall x,y$$
