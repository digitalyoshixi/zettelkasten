# Writing $\sqrt{ i  }$ in standard form
$\sqrt{ i } = \sqrt{ e^{i \frac{\pi}{2}} }$
= $\left( e^{i \frac{\pi}{2}} \right)^{\frac{1}{2}}$
= $( e^{i \frac{\pi}{4}} )$
= $\cos(\frac{\pi}{4}) + i\sin(\frac{\pi}{4})$
= $\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }} + i \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}$
