# CDF to PDF
$$f_{X}(x) = \frac{d}{dx}F_{X}(x) = F_{X}'(x)$$
- $f_{X}(x)$ being the [[Probability Density Function|PDF]]
- $F_{X}(x)$ being the [[Cumulative Distribution Function|CDF]]
The probability at a given point on the [[Probability Density Function|PDF]] the gradient of the [[Cumulative Distribution Function|CDF]]
