The number $n$ that represents the number of solutions for of $z$ for equation $z^n = 1$ where:
- $z$ is a [[Complex Number]]
- $n$ is an arbitrary [[Natural Number]]

The $n$ solutions would be: $\{ z_{0} , z_{1} \dots z_{n-1}\}$ given by $k \in[0,n-1], z_{k} = e^{i \frac{2k\pi}{n}}$
