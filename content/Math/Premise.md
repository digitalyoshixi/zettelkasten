A [[Proposition]] used as a starting assumption for an argument.
In validating the [[Validity]] of an argument, the premises must follow the conclusion.
In validating the [[Soundness]], the premises must be true.
Example:
- It will either rain or snow tomorrow
- It will not snow
# Premise Strength
### Weak Premise
- One that assumes at a minimum
### Strong Premise
- One premise that assumes a lot
