These are representations of expressions in the form:
$x_{1}^{2} + \dots + x_{n}^{2} + \sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_{i}x_{j}| i \neq j)$

They can represent [[Matrix|Matrixes]] obtained from [[Polarization Identity|Polarization Identity]]
# Definition
- Let $x \in \mathbb{R}^{n}$, A function $x^{T}Ax = f(x_{1},\dots,x_{n}) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} x_{i} x_{j}$ creates quadratic form
- Let $x \in \mathbb{C}^{n}$, A function $x^{T}Ax = f(x_{1},\dots,x_{n}) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} \overline{x_{i}} x_{j}$ creates quadratic form
