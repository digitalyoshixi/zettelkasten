These are representations of vectors obtained from specific functions.
# Definition
- Let $x \in \mathbb{R}^{n}$, A function $f(x_{1},\dots,x_{n}) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} x_{i} x_{j}$ creates quadratic form
- Let $x \in \mathbb{C}^{n}$, A function $f(x_{1},\dots,x_{n}) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} \overline{x_{i}} x_{j}$ creates quadratic form
