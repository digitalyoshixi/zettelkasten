1. Write **Proof** or **pf** at the start of the proof.
2. Write **WTS (Want to Show)** the mathematical statement of the proof
3. If the option of disproving is allowed, test a few cases.
4. Write in complete, grammatically correct sentences.
5. Begin sentences with words and end with punctuation
6. Use **hence, therefore, thus, then, it follows that**
7. Use **since** and **by** for justifications
8. Be concise
9. Conclude with $\square$ or $QED$.
