For $C^{1}$ [[Class Functions]],
Transformation $T(u,v) = (x(u,v), y(u,v))$ has [[Jacobian Matrix|Jacobian Determinant]]
$$\frac{\partial(x,y)}{\partial(u,v)} = \det(DT) = \det\left[\begin{array}{cc} 
\frac{\partial x}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial x}{\partial v}\\
\frac{\partial y}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial v}\\
\end{array}\right]$$
