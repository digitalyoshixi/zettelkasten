# Theorem
Let $M \in \mathbb{F}[x]$, the following are equivalent:
1. $M$ is an [[Ideal]]
2. $M$ is non-empty subset such that:
	1. $f \in \mathbb{F}[x]$, $g \in M$, then $fg = gf \in M$
	2. $f,g \in M$, then $f+g \in M$
Very similar to the definition of a [[Subspace]]
