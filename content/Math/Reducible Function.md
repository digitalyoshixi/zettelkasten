# Definition
A [[Polynomial Function]] $f \in \mathbb{F}[x]$ is reducible over $\mathbb{F}$ if two polynomial $g, h \in \mathbb{F}[x]$ exist such that:
1. $deg(h) \geq 1, deg(h) \geq 1$
2. $f = gh$
Otherwise, $f$ is Prime Function over $\mathbb{F}$
