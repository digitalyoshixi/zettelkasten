For [[Continuous Random Variable]] $X,Y$, describes the area of probability within a [[Domain|Region]] $B =[a,b] \times [c,d]\subset \mathbb{R}^{2}$
$$P((X,Y) \in B) = \int \int_{R} f_{X,Y}(x,y)dxdy$$
# Properties
- $f_{X,Y} (x,y) \geq 0, \forall x,y \in \mathbb{R}$
- $\int \int_{\mathbb{R}^{2}} f_{X,Y}(x,y)dxdy = 1$
