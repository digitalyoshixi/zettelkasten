For [[Propositional Formula|Formulas]] $\mathcal{F}_{1}, \mathcal{F}_{2}$
$\mathcal{F}_{1} \text{ LIMP } \mathcal{F}_{2} = \mathcal{F}_{1} \implies \mathcal{F}_{2}$ means every [[Truth Assignment]] in $\mathcal{F}_{1}$ is [[Satisfies|Satisfied]] in $\mathcal{F}_{2}$
