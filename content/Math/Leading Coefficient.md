The leading coefficient is the coefficient constant belonging to the variable with the highest [[Degree]]

so, if we have polynomial 7x^2 + 5, the leading coefficient is **7**.

### Finding leading coefficient quickly in factor format
(3x - 4)^2 * (5x^2 - 3) * (4x - 8)
we don't care about the constants by them selves. so take them out
(3x)^2 * (5x^2) * (4x)
and then we can simplify now
9x^2 * 5x^2 * 4x = 180x^5

and so x^5 is the highest degree, the Leading Coefficient is by proxy 180
