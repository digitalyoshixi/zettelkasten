For a [[Multiple Integral]] with input variables $x,y$ where $x=g(u,v)$, $y = h(u,v)$
$$\int \int f(x,y)dxdy = \int \int f(g(u,v), h(u,b))| \frac{d(x,y)}{d(u,v)}|dudv$$ where: $| \frac{d(x,y)}{d(u,v)}|$ is the [[Norm|Absolute Value]] of the determinant of the [[Jacobian Matrix]].
