Usually values like 95%, but sometimes 90% or 99%
Probability that a particular statistic is within the range indicated by the [[Margin of Error]]
For example, out of all [[Confidence Intervals]] computed at the 95% level, 95% of them should contain the parameter's true value.
### Confidence Level & Z-score
**Confidence Level**: The percentage you want to encompass. eg 95%
**Tail**: $\frac{1-ConfidenceLevel}{2}$
