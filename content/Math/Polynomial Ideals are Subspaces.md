# Corrolary
Let $M$ be an [[Ideal]] of $\mathbb{F}[x]$, Then $M$ is a subspace of $\mathbb{F}[x]$
# Proof
1. Let $c \in E$
2. Let $g_{1},g_{2}\in M$
3. Then, $c \in \mathbb{F}[x]$
4. Thus, $cg_{1} \in M$
5. Additionally, $cg_{1} +g_{2} \in M$
6. Thus, $M$ is a subspace by the [[Alternate Characterizations of Polynomial Ideals]]
