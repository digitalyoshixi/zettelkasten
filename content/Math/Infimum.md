# Definition (Greatest Lower Bound)
Let $S \subset R$ and $s \in R$, we say that $s$ is the infimum of $S$ if:
1. $s$ is a [[Lower Bound]] of S ($\forall x \in S, x\geq s$)
2. If $b$ is an lower bound of $S$ then, $s \geq b$
# Notation
- $A \wedge B$ picks the infimum of the two
- $\wedge_{P}I$ notes that $I$ is infimum of [[Set]] $P$
