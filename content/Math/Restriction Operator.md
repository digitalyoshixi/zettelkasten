Defining a linear map for a smaller domain.
# Definition
- With $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$
- With [[Subspace]] $W \subset V$
- With $W$ [[Invariant]] under $T$
- The restriction operator $T_{W} : W \to W$ is defined as $T_{W}(x) = Tx, \forall x \in W$
Note that $T_{W} \neq T$, as $domain(T_{W}) =W, domain(T)=V$
