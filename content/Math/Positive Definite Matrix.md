# Definition
With $n \times n$ matrix $M$ that has [[Quadratic Form]] $Q_{M}(h) = h^{T}Mh$
$M$ is positive definite if: 
- $Q_{M}(h) > 0, \forall h \neq 0$ and $Q_{M}(h) = 0$ equivalent to $h=0$
$M$ is negative definite if: 
- $Q_{M}(h) < 0, \forall h \neq 0$ and $Q_{M}(h) = 0$ equivalent to $h=0$
# Alternate Characterization
- All [[Principal Minor|Principal Minors]] of the matrix are positive $\implies$ positive definite matrix
- All [[Principal Minor|Principal Minors]] of the matrix are negative $\implies$ negative definite matrix
-  [[Principal Minor|Principal Minors]] are mixed in [[Polarity]] $\implies$ indefinite matrix
