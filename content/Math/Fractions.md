# Fraction
- A number to represent parts of numbers
- Numerator
- Denominator
# Equivalent Fractions
$$
\frac{1}{2}= \frac{2}{3} = \frac{3}{6}
$$
# Simplifying Fractions
Turn it into the smallest version of itself
$\frac{3}{6}=\frac{1}{2}$
# Adding Fractions
- Get a common base. This if the Greatest Common Factor of the denominators
- Convert each fraction to their equilvant form with the common denominator
- Add the numerators

# Subtracting Fractions
- Same deal as adding, but this time you subtract the fractions

# Multiplying Fractions
$$\frac{
a_{1}}{b_{1}}*\frac{a_{2}}{b_{2}} = \frac{a_{1}b_{1}}{b_{1}b_{2}} 
$$
# Dividing Fractions
- flip the bottom fraction, then multiply
$$
\frac{\frac{
a_{1}}{b_{1}}}{\frac{a_{2}}{b_{2}} }=\frac{a_{1}}{b_{1}}*\frac{b_{2}}{a_{2}}=\frac{a_{1}b_{2}}{b_{1}a_{2}} 
$$
# Mixed Fractions
$1 \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{2}$
# Fractions -> Mixed Fractions
$\frac{3}{2}=1 \frac{1}{2}$
