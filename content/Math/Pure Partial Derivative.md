# Definition
- With $f : \mathbb{R}^{2} \to \mathbb{R}$ being [[Differentiability|Differentiable]]
- With partial derivatives $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} , \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}$
- The pure partial derivatives include: $\frac{\partial ^{2}f}{\partial x^{2}}, \frac{\partial ^{2}f}{\partial y^{2}}$
