Ordered arrangements of $k$ objects chosen without replacement from $n$ possible objects.
Used when counting, when order matters.
[[Box Occupant Based Thinking]] helps to visualize permutations
# Notation
### $_n$P$_r$
n = # of occupants
r = # of boxes
### P(n,r)
### Permutation formula
$_{n}P_{r} = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$
