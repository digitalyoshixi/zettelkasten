# Definition
A [[Ideal]] set $\mathbb{F}[x]$ of [[Polynomial|Polynomials]] with coefficients in $\mathbb{F}$
A polynomial $f \in \mathbb{F}[x]$ takes the form $f(x) = c_{0} + c_{1}x^{1} + \dots + c_{k}x^{k}$
