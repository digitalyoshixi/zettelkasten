To find out if a sequence converges, you can perform this iterative process to find the next subsequence elements of the sequence.
# Process
![[Graphing The Recursive Definition of Sequences-20250320170639571.webp]]
- Graph out the function and find the first y-value of $x=1$
- Then, this y-value is your next x value, so find the y value at $x=1.73205$
![[Graphing The Recursive Definition of Sequences-20250320170843377.webp]]
- Repeat this process infinitely until you find that the sequence eventually converges to 2
