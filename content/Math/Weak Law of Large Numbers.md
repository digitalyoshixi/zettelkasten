# Theorem
The [[Average of Random Variable|Average]] of [[Independent Random Variable|Independent RVs]] converges to their common mean $\mu$
$$X_{n} \to^{P} \mu \Longleftrightarrow P(|\overline X_{n} - \mu| \geq \epsilon) \to 0 \text{ as } n\to 0, \forall \epsilon > 0$$
