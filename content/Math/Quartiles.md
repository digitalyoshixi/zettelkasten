- 3 points divide the data set into 4 equal groups. 
- Q (The first quartile) is the middle number between the smallest number and the median
- $Q_{2}$ (The second quartile) is the median of data set (known as the 50th percentile)
- $Q_{3}$ (The third quartile) is the middle number between the median and largest number

**Range** : the highest value - lowest value
### Interquartile Range
The difference between the first and third quartiles
$Q_3 -Q_1$
### Semi-Interquartile/Mid-Quartile
1/2 the difference between the first and third quartiles.
affected very little by the extreme scores. good skew if you have outliers.
$\frac{Q_3 -Q_1}{2}$
