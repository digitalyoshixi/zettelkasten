# For $\mathbb{R}$
$$\langle \alpha | \beta \rangle = \frac{1}{4} || \alpha + \beta|| ^{2 } - \frac{1}{4}|| \alpha - \beta|| ^{2}$$
# For $\mathbb{C}$
$$ \langle \alpha| \beta \rangle = \frac{1}{4} || \alpha - \beta ||^{2} - \frac{1}{4} || \alpha - \beta ||^{2} + i \frac{1}{4} || \alpha + i \beta || ^{2} - i \frac{1}{4}|| \alpha - i\beta||^{2}$$
