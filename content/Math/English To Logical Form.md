# [[Logical Connectives]]
- Neither A nor B: $\neg A \vee \neg B$
# [[Conditional Statement]]
- Q Necessary for P: $P \implies Q$
- Q if P: $P \implies Q$
- P only if Q: $P \implies Q$
- P Sufficient for Q: $P \implies Q$
- P unless Q: $P \Longleftrightarrow Q$
# [[Quantifier]]
$\forall$: Everyone, Everything 
$\exists$: Someone, something
