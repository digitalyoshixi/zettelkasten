# Question
Suppose $g : R \to R$ is continuous on $R$ s.t $g(1) = 4$ and $g(2)=1$.
Does there exist a $c \in R$ such that $g(c) = c^2$
# Proof
Yes, there is.
let $f(x) = x^2-g(x)$
First note $f$ is a continuous function, difference of 2 functions $x^2$ and $g(x)$
