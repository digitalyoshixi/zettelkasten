$\wedge$ - And. statements $P$ and $Q$ must be true
$\vee$ - Or. Either $P$ or $Q$ must be true
$\neg$ - Not. Flip true/false polarity
# Precedence Hierarchy
1. $\neg$ (negation). Not
2. $\wedge$ (Conjunction). And 
3. $\vee$ (Disjunction). Or / Nor
