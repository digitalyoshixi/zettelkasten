# Definition
- With $V = \mathbb{C}^{n}$
- Fixing $\alpha \in \mathbb{C}^{n}$
- Let the inner product $< \cdot | \cdot> : \mathbb{C}^{n} \times \mathbb{C}^{n} \to \mathbb{C}$ be the standard inner product
- $x = (x_{1},\dots,x_{n)}$
- $y = (y_{1},\dots,y_{n})$
- Then, $\langle x | y \rangle = \sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i} \overline y_{i}$ 
	- where $\overline y_{i}$ is the [[Complex Conjugate]] of $y_{i}$
