Understand that a polynomial in factored form with each factor being a root will also mean each factor can have a degree.

A factor like (x-3)^**2** is an order **2**

The degree of the X inside the **parenthesis must be one**
Because (x^2 -1) can be factored even more to (x+1)(x-1)
