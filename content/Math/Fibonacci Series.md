A [[Sequence]] of numbers described by:
$$
\begin{cases} 
n_{0} = 1\\
n_{1} = 1\\
n = n_{i-1}+n_{i-2}
\end{cases}
$$
Grows in proportion to [[Golden Ratio]].
# General Term (Binet's Formula)
$$f(n)=\frac{\phi^n- \psi^n}{\sqrt{ 5 }}$$
Where 
- $\phi=\frac{1+\sqrt{ 5 }}{2}$
- $\psi=\frac{1-\sqrt{ 5 }}{2}$
