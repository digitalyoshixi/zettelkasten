# Theorem
1. if $f(x)$ is [[Differentiability|Differentiable]]
2. then, $f(x)$ is [[Continuity|Continuous]]
### Open Set Version
Suppose that $f : U \subset \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}^{k}$ is differentiable at $x_{0} \in U$ where $U$ is an [[Open Set]].
Then, $f$ is continuous at $x_{0}$.
