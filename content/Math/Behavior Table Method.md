A little bit to the negative: $0^-$
A little bit to the Positive: $0^+$

These all attribute to points. We dont put asymptotes or anything like that because those are not coordinates.
![[Behavior Table Method-20231210035433781.webp|628]]
### Example
With $y = 1/x$

as $x \to 0^- , y \to -\infty$
as $x \to 0^+ , y \to \infty$
as $x\to \infty, y\to{0}^+$
as $x\to -\infty, y\to{0}^-$
