A reordering of a set.
# Definition
A permutation is a [[Bijective Functions|Bijection]] $\sigma : \{ 1,2,\dots,n \} \to \{ 1,2,\dots,n \}$.
A permutation exists in the set $S_{n}$ of all permutations.
### Intuition
A permutation acts on a [[Tuple]] $\{ 1,\dots,n \}$ giving us a new tuple $s\{ \sigma(1),\dots, \sigma(n) \}$.
