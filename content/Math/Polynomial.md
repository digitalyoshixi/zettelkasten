# Definition
An equation where terms are numbers multiplied by variable `x`.
number * x
it can be like $9x$ or $9x^2$ or $9x^0$ 
### Requirements 
the stipulations for what x are is:
- Leading [[Degree]] is not negative. It can be atleast x^0.
