# Definition
A form of numbers that takes from a field.
- Let $\mathbb{F}[x]$ be the set of polynomials with coefficients in $\mathbb{F}$
- Then, $f(x) = c_{0} + c_{1}x^{1} + \dots + c_{k}x^{k} \in \mathbb{F}[x]$
