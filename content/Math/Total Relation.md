# Left Total Relation
A [[Binary Relation]] $\{ (x,y) \}, x \in X, y \in Y$ is left total if $\forall x \in X, \exists y \in Y \text{ s.t } xRy$
# Right Total Relation
A [[Binary Relation]] $\{ (x,y) \}, x \in X, y \in Y$ is left total if $\forall y \in Y, \exists x \in X \text{ s.t } xRy$
