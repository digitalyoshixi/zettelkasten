Every non-empty subset of real numbers that is [[Bounded]] will have a [[Supremum]] or [[Infimum]] or both depending on the bounding
# Implications
The real numberline is the only complete one. Any other number-set would fail. 
For example, the [[Rational Number]] set is not complete, since there will be gaps in the number-line so a [[Upper Bound]] and [[Lower Bound]] would not be guaranteed.
