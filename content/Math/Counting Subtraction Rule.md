If a task can be done:
- $n_{1}$ ways
- or in $n_{2}$ ways
- ...
- or in $n_{m}$ ways
- Where there is an intersection between $n_{1},n_{2},n_{m}$
- Then there are $n_{1} + n_{2} + \dots n_{m} - n_{1}\cap n_{2}\cap \dots \cap n_{m}$
