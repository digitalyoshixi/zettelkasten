A method to analyze qualitative data.
Aims to derive theory from systematic analysis of data.
# Categorizations
Contains three levels of coding:
- Open: Identify categories (word, phrase, sentences, etc)
- Axial: Flesh out and link to subcategories
- Selective: Form theoretical scheme (backbone of theory)
