Methods to prove fragments of ones identity.
# Knowledge
Something you Know.
- [[Social Security Number|SSN]]
- Username
- Password
- Birthday
- Pin
# Possession
Something you have.
- Smart Card
- [[Key Fob]]
- [[Ticket Granting Ticket|TGT]]
- [[External Hardware Token]]
# Inherence
Something you are.
- [[Biometric Device]]
# Activity
Something you do.
- Behavioral analysis of typing
- Card swiping
- [[Gait Analysis]]
# Location
Somewhere you are.
- [[Global Positioning System|GPS]] to authenticate based off if user is at home.
# Temporal
Something you do at specific times.
- Requiring logins at certain times of day or only within a certain window of time like 5 seconds.
# Third Party Applications
Third party apps used for authentication into corporate services.
They can issue temporary codes and act like [[Authentication Token|Authentication Tokens]].
