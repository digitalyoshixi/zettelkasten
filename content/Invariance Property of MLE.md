# Property
Let $\hat{\theta}$ be the [[Maximum Likelihood Estimator|MLE]] of $\theta$ and
$\psi(\theta)$ be a [[Injective Functions|One-to-one Function]] of $\theta$ defined on $\Omega$,
Then, $\psi(\hat{\theta})$ is the [[Maximum Likelihood Estimator|MLE]] of $\psi(\theta)$.
