A theory of how to make optimal sequential decisions under uncertainty.
It outlines how an optimal [[Artificial Intelligence|AI]] will act.
# Formal Definition
- Assuming we are in a [[Reinforcement Learning]] setup
- An agent $\pi$ interacts with environment $\mu$ by taking actions $a_{t}$ and recieving observations $o_{t}$ and [[Reward]] $r_{t}$
- We do not assume the environment is [[Markov Chain|Markovian]]
