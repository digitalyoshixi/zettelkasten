These are AI systems that prioritize:
- Human values
- Needs
- Well-being

Metrics can be correct, but do people want this?
# Key Considerations
- Trust: Do people trust the algorithm?
- Dignity: Does system treat people as holistic beings?
- Transparency: Can people understand how algorithm makes decisions
- Accountability: Who is accountable?
