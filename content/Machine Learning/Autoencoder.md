Often used for denoising and data compression.
Can also be used as a [[Generative AI|Generative Model]] when adding [[Noise]]
# Process
![[Autoencoder-20251010214127442.webp|360]]
1. One neural network [[Feature Encoding|Encodes]] data to convert into [[Latent Space]]
2. One neural network to [[Decoder|Decode]] data.
