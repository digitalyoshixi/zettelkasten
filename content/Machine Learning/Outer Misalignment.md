A problem in [[AI Alignment]] wherein we provide [[Specification]] that is fuzzy and provides gaps for the AI to behave unexpectedly. Our objective we specify does not match what we want.
The misalignment behavior actually scores highly on the [[Reward Function]].
We as humans fail to specify a goal
