A operation to measure the similarity between two [[Vector]].

$$S_{C}(A,B) = \cos(\theta) = \frac{A \cdot B}{||A|| ||B||}$$
- Two proporitional vectors have a $S_{C}(A,B) = 1$
- Two [[Orthogonal]] vectors have a $S_{C}(A,B) = 0$
- Two Opposite vectors have a $S_{C}(A,B) = -1$
