An AI system that outsmarts every human in every task.
# For
- No reason to think that machines can treach human brain ([[Representationalism]])
- Current systems are already quite close to human level intellect
- Even if we dont have the capabilities right now, we are pouring so much funding in AI [[Research and Development|R&D]]
- AI is far better at non-human suited tasks (Can reason about [[Quantum Mechanics]])
- [[Recursive Self Improvement]] -> [[Singularity]]
- Even if we cant get [[Artificial Superintelligence|ASI]], [[Artificial General Intelligence|HLAI]] can still have qualitative advantage over humans:
	- LLMs are knowledge bases
	- AI can do cognitive tasks much faster than we can
	- AI can outnumber us and easy to make copies of itself
# Against
- LLM systems still miss something (No breakthroughs, continual learning)
- AGI requires fundamentally different ideas (not blind scaling)
- As science and tech improves, ideas are harder to find: [[No New Ideas]]
