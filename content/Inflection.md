A [[Morpheme]] used as a [[Grammatical Marker]] for encoding extra information such as:
- Gender
- Tense
- Number
Without changing the [[Morpheme]] structure or [[Syntactical Category]] of the word.
# Inflection Types
- [[Affixation Inflection]]
- [[Internal Change]]
- [[Suppletion]]
- [[Reduplication]]
