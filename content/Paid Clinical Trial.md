The idea of paying people to undertake clinical trials.
# For Paid Trials
1. It is a compensation for time and effort
2. Compensation for risk
3. [[Benjamin Sachs Clinical Trial Argument]]
4. Incentives people to do it
5. We can use [[Minimum Wage]] or other standards, or a very small amount of money
6. Allows for a broader amount of demographics available ([[White Participant Problem]])
# Against Paid Trials
1. Exploitation against vulnerable groups
2. Manipulating people to participate because of the payment
3. Payment will recruit more poor people
