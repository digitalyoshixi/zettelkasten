Runs on [[Transmission Control Protocol|TCP]] port `443`
Provides [[Network Messages|Frame]] encryption for the [[TCP & IP Application Layer|Application Layer]]. 
Most commonly used for securing websites in [[Hyper Text Transfer Protocol Secure|HTTPS]].
