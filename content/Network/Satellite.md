Data is beamed to a satellite dish and runs through a [[Coaxial Cable]] to your [[Modem]]
- 25-50 Mbps Download speed
- 3 Mbps Upload speed
- 250ms latency
- 2 GHz frequency
# Positioning
- Satellites communicate via line of site. If they are obstructed, your signal is lost
- Since [[Geostationary Objects|Geostationary]] satellites hover near the equator, point your satellite south if you are in the northern hemisphere and north if you are in the southern hemisphere.
