![[Patch Panel-20240716203144822.webp]]
A panel to ease the process of moving cables around.
![[Patch Panel-20240816181432730.webp]]
[[Straight Through Cable|Solid Cables]] are permanently secured to the back of these panels with a [[Punchdown Tool]], then [[Unshielded Twisted Pair|Stranded Cables]] with colored jackets for organization are used to pass on the data from solid cables to their destination.
Each patch panel also has space for labels so each cable can be labeled
# Block Types
### 66 Block
- Used in analog telephone systems
### 110 Block
- Used in computer networks
