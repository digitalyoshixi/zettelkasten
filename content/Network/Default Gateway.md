The local IP for your router. This is the address your devices send to when they are sending data across the internet or other [[Local Area Network|LAN]].
Your gateway is always on the same network as your computer.
So, for example if your computer had ip `192.168.7.88`, then you default gateway would be `192.168.7.1`
