### Multimode Fibre (LED Dispersal)
![[Fibre Optic Cable-20240730184935798.webp|629]]
[[Light Emitting Diode|LED]]s shoot down light that is bounced around in the reflective fiber.
Most fibre optic networks use 62.5/125 multimode fibre optic cables.
The light disperses after long distances so it is better to use this technology for shorter distances
# Benefits
- Can travel 2 km 
- Cheaper than [[Single-mode Fibre]]
