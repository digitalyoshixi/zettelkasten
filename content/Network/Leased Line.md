Specialized network cables purchased temporarily from an [[Internet Service Provider|ISP]] for connecting a [[Router]] to the [[Wide Area Network|WAN]].
Creates a direct connection between networks.
![[Leased LIne-20250605162501292.webp]]
They are modified [[RJ-45]] or [[Fibre Optic Cable]] designed to follow [[High-Level Data Link Control Protocol|HDLC]] and [[Point-to-Point Protocol|PPP]] protocol for [[Wide Area Network|WAN]] communication.
They act like [[Point-to-point Communication|Full Duplex]] [[Cross-Over Cable]].
- Expensive to install
- Slower compared to other methods of [[Wide Area Network|WAN]]
