# # xDSL
![[RJ-11 Cable-20240723001341260.webp]]
Cables used to establish internet connection when plugged into a [[Modem]]
### DSL
- Speed is around 1-3 Mbps
- 10,000 ft limitation for signal. Signal quality diminishes the farther you are from the modem
### Asymmetric DSL (ADSL)
- Upload speed of < 1 Mbps
- Download speed of 3-15 Mbps
### Symmetric DSL (SDSL)
- Upload speed of 1.5 Mbps
- Download speed of 1.5 Mbps
