A device used to connect a [[Local Area Network|LAN]] to [[The Internet]] by decoding analog signals that come from the ISP.
[[Dial-Up]] required Modems to connect to the internet by converting analog signals into digital sound.
Modems do not support wireless and require every device wired in.
# Modem Types
- [[Cable Modem]]
- [[Asymmetric DSL Modem]]
- [[Optical Network Terminal|ONT]]
