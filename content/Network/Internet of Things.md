Devices that manage equipment that can be configured through an internet connection.
Includes devices like:
- Vending machines
- Environment sensors
- Parking meters
- Security cameras
These devices should be isolated in their own [[Virtual Local Area Network|VLAN]], since they are not made to be secure.
These devices also commonly use [[ARM]] cpus.
