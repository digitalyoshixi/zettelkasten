# Connecting to The Internet
ISP gives a [[Modem]] or [[Router]] and a Cable. This cable could be:
- [[RJ-11|DSL]]
- [[Coaxial Cable]]
- [[Fibre Optic Cable]]
You can also connect via:
- [[Wireless ISP|WISP]]
- [[WI-FI|WIFI]]
- [[Cellular Data]]
- [[Satellite]]
# SOHO Communication
### Addressing
1. [[Local Area Network|LAN]] has a [[Router]] 
2. [[Router]] connects to an [[Internet Service Provider]]
3. [[Internet Service Provider]] establishes public [[IP Address]] for all devices
### Receiving
Data coming in will get sent to the router. The router routes it to the correct device
### Transmitting
