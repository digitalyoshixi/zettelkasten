# 900 MHz
- Unlicensed band
# 2.4 GHz
- Unlicensed [[Industrial, Scientific and Medical Band|ISM]] that anybody can use.
- Other devices tend to also use this frequency like baby monitors and smart phones
- There are 11 channels at 2.4 GHz (13 in england). Channels 1,6 and 11 are non-overlapping. Don't use overlapping channels.
- 2.4 GHz frequencies travel farther 
- 2.4 GHz frequencies aren't absorbed by devices
# 5 GHz

- Unlicensed band
- 25 non-overlapping channels.
- You can bond several channels together to read 40GHz or 80GHz, but these will limit performance
- Frequencies tend to get lost by other objects
