A [[Serial Communication|Asynchronous Serial Communication]] protocol for connecting peripheral devices. 
- Transmission speeds are toggleable
- Can be [[Point-to-point Communication|Simplex]], [[Point-to-point Communication|Half Duplex]] or [[Point-to-point Communication|Full Duplex]]
Used in many low-speed, low-throughput applications.
