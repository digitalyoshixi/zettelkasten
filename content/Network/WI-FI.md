A method of data transmission through spread spectrum [[Radio Frequency]]. 
The frequency band is Super high, meaning it may interfere with microwaves or baby monitors
Used in [[Access Point|WAP]] to establish [[Wireless Local Area Network|WLAN]].
# Auth Methods
- [[Pre-Shared Key|PSK]]
- [[Remote Authentication Dial-In User Service|RADIUS]] for enterprise
- Open: no credentials
