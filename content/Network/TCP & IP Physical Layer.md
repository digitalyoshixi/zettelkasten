One of the 2 Alternate layers of the [[TCP & IP Link Layer]] found in the [[TCP & IP]] 5 layered model along with [[TCP & IP Data Layer]].
They work around providing protocols for cables like with RJ45 cables and how to perform procedures like Bit Sending/Decoding between 2 computers.

The big task of this is [[Bit Transmission]]
