Options that make your [[Wireless Local Area Network|WLAN]] less likely to be targeted for cyber-attacks
Not to be confused with [[WAP Security]]
# SSID No Broadcast
The WAP will not broadcast the SSID, but devices can still connect if they know the SSID name.
# SSID Obscure Name
Do not keep the SSID name the default vendor name
# Signal Obscurity
You can change the radio power of your WAP, so that you only get reception within your house
