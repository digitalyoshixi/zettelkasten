A melody technique that splits a single melody into two parts:
- A [[Questions|Antecedent]] ([[Dominant Chord]] or [[Predominant Chord]])
- An [[Answer|Consequent]] ([[Tonic Chord]])
![[Antecedent Consequent Melody Technique-20251019030304280.webp|463]]
At the end of the antecedent, the music will sound unresolved.
