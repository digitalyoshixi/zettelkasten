# Theorem
1. Suppose $\{ a \},\{ b \}$ are [[Sequence|Sequences]]
2. Convergent case:
	1. If $\forall n , a_{n} \leq b_{n}$
	2. And, we also know that $b_{n}$ converges
	3. Then, $a_{n}$ converges
3. Divergent case:
	4. If $\forall n , b_{n} \leq a_{n}$
	5. And, we know $b_{n}$ diverges 
	6. Then, $a_{n}$ also diverges
