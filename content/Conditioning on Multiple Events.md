# Definition
For events, $A,B,C$ the probability of $A$ given $B$ and $C$ is denoted as:
$P(A|B,C)= P(A| B \cap C) = \frac{P(A \cap B \cap C)}{P(B \cap C)}, P(B \cap C) > 0$
### [[Disjoint]] Generalization
If $A,B,C$ are mutually independent, then:
- $P(A|B,C) = P(A)$
- $P(B|A,C) = P(B)$
