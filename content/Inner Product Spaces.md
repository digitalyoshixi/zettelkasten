A [[Vector Space]] with a specified [[Standard Inner Product for Vector Spaces|Inner Product]].
# Theorem
With $V$ as a inner product space, $\forall \alpha,\beta \in V$, $\forall c \in \mathbb{F}$
1. $||c\alpha|| = |c| || \alpha ||$
2. $|| \alpha || > 0, \forall \alpha \neq 0$
3. $| \langle \alpha | \beta \rangle| \leq || \alpha || ||\beta ||$ ([[Cauchy Schwarz Inequality]])
4. $|| \alpha + \beta || \leq || \alpha || + ||\beta||$
