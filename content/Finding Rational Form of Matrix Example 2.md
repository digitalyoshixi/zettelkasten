# Example
Find the rational form for the [[Linear Operator]] $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ with:
- [[Characteristic Polynomial]] $f(x) = (x-2)^{2}(x-3)^{3}(x^{2}+1)$
# Soln
Note that the [[Minimal Polynomial]] is $f(x) = (x-2)(x-3)(x^{2}+1)$
