The hypothetical list of [[Attack Vector|Attack Vectors]] and [[Security Threat|Threats]] your organization has.
# Biggest Entrants
- [[Endpoint]]
- Network services ([[Web Server]], [[Email Server]], [[Virtual Private Network|VPN]])
- Open ports and protocols
- Weak or compromised user accounts
- Third-party integrations
- [[Cloud Computing|Cloud]] services
- Human factor of [[Social Engineering]]
# Minimizing the Attack Surface
- [[Vulnerability Assessment]]
- Implement strict [[Access Control]]
- [[Network Segmentation]]
- Reducing [[Single Point of Failure]]
- Frequent endpoint security updates
- [[Multifactor Authentication|MFA]]
- Regular auditing
- [[Security Awareness]]
