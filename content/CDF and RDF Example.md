With 
$$
p_{X}(x) = \begin{cases}
\frac{1}{2}, & x = 1\\
\frac{1}{3}, & x = 2\\
\frac{1}{6}, & x = 3
\end{cases}
$$
$$F_{X}(x) = P(X \leq x) = \begin{cases}
0 & x < 1\\
\frac{1}{2} & 1 \leq x < 2\\
\frac{5}{6} & 2 \leq x \leq 3\\
1 & x \geq 3\\
\end{cases}$$
