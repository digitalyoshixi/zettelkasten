Two variables $X,Y$ are independent if: $\forall A,B \subset \mathbb{R}$
$P(X \in A, Y \in B) = P(X \in A)P(Y \in B)$
# CDF/PMF/PDF Factorization
$$
X \bot Y \Longleftrightarrow \begin{cases}
F_{X,Y}(x,y) = F_{X}(x)F_{Y}(y) & (any)\\
f_{X,Y}(x,y) = f_{X}(x)f_{Y}(y) & (cont)\\
p_{X,Y}(x,y) = p_{X}(x)p_{Y}(y) & (disc)\\
\end{cases}
$$
