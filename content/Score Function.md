A [[Logistic Regression Model]] that considers a linear relationship between:
- Predictor [[Random Variable|RV]] $X$
- Log-odds of event $Y=1$, where $Y$ is a binary [[Random Variable|RV]] $\{ 0,1 \}$
Score function measures probability that $Y=1$
$$
p(x) = \frac{1}{1+e^{-(\beta_{0}+\beta_{1})}}
$$
