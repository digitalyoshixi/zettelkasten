# Theorem
Suppose $R \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$
Let $f : R \to \mathbb{R}$ be an [[Definite Integral|Integrable]] function.
Then, the average of $f$ on $R$ is:
$$
A = \frac{1}{Vol_{n}(R)}\int_{R} f dV_{n}
$$
Where:
- $Vol_{n}$ is the $n$-dimensional volume of $R$
- $dV_{n}$ is the $n$-dimensional volume element
