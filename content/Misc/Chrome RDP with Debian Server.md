On the host machine, go to this url:  [https://remotedesktop.google.com/access](https://remotedesktop.google.com/access)
Then, Setup Via SSH.
Follow its steps, download the software.

And then, once completed, the computer should be listed in your google account.
Any computer logged into this google account will be able to enter into this machine.
