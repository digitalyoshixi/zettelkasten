# Proof
1. Suppose $f$ is cont on $[a,b]$
2. Suppose $F$ is any anti-derivative on $[a,b]$
3. Then, $\forall x \in [a,b], F'(x) = f(x)$ by defn of [[Antiderivative]]
4. Consider $\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx$
5. $= \lim_{ n \to \infty }\sum_{i=1}^{n}f(x_{i}^{*})\triangle x$
