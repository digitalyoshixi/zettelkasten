Population will always outpace food, so some manual limitations must be set on population growth.

# Observations
1. Population grows geometrically, doubling every 25 years
2. Food production grows arithmetically, increasing at a constant rate every 25 years
3. Population increases at a faster rate than food production
4. Thus, there must be checks to limit population growth
### Population Checks
##### Preventative Checks
Decreasing birth rate.
- [[Celibacy]]
- Late marriage
- Self control in married life
##### Positive Checks
Increasing death rate - mostly naturally caused
- Famines
- Epidemics
- Wars
