A [[Trade Agreement]] that aims to:
- Promote trade liberalization(easing trade barriers) across the world
- Focus on economic prosperity and social development
- 164 member countries
- Decision made by consensus
- 90% of countries are members. Countries at war or with human right crimes are not members
##### WTO Activities
**Trade Negotiations:** Countries discuss trade disputes and come to mutually agreeable solutions
**Implementation and Monitoring:** Monitors whether WTO agreements have been adopted and properly implemented
**Dispute Settlement:** Forum for countries to consult, mediate andarbitrate discrepancies of previously agreed on trade agreements
**Foreign Aid**: Supports developing countries through training or infrastructure aid
**Outreach:** Marketing to build awareness of WTO activities
