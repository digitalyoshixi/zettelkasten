A guaranteed payment that ensures minimum income necessary to survive regardless of technical skill or current employment level.

# Ontario Basic Income Pilot (OBIP)
This was a campaign started in 2017 as an experiment to see if UBI is a valid solution.
Each participant would recieve $16,989 per year and earned $50 less for every hour they worked.
It ended up being cancelled after the political party changed hands.
