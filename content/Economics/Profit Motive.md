What distinguishes a [[Non-Profit]] and a [[Business]].
It is the incentive to give up time for profit.
Profit - The change to create
Wealth - The incentive
[[Adam Smith]] endorsed this idea in [[Capitalism]].

A profit that does not prioritize money, means it is not a [[Business]]. For example:
- It can be a [[Philanthropy|Philanthropic]] endeavor for fame.
- It can be a moral action for [[Religion|Religious]] reasons
