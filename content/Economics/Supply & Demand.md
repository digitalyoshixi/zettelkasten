# Supply
![[Supply & Demand-20240526221733461.webp|408]]
Supply is the amount of product available
### Factors that shift supply
- raw material cost
- environment
- technology
- \# of suppliers
- ability to substitute/company change market
# Demand
![[Supply & Demand-20240526221754012.webp|353]]
Demand is the need for the product
### Factors that shift demand
- Complimentary goods
- substitute products
- population changes
- income changes
- future expected price
- consumer tastes
- season
# Market
![[Supply & Demand-20240526221822620.webp|398]]
The market is the relation formed between the supply and demand. You always want to reach for equilibrium.

### Market States
**Shortage:** Demand > Supply
**Surplus:** Supply > Demand
**Equilibrium:** Demand = Supply
