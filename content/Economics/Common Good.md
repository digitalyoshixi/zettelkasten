# Problems with Common Good
### Value Systems
People cannot agree on "the good life". Our relative values differ too much
### "Free-rider problem"
Certain groups of people will not do their part and exploit the common good for their own gain.
E.g. Tax evader still partakes in free healthcare
### Individualism
Self interest prevents people from willingly sacrificing their freedom for sake of the common good
### Unequal Burden Sharing
Certain groups must bear greater costs.
Rich will pay more taxes of course.
