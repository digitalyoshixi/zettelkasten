![[International Monetary Fund-20240527005803777.webp|265]]
Financial agency in the [[United Nations]] with 190 member countries.
Aims to: 
- Foster global monetary cooperation
- Financial stability
- [[International Trade]] 
- High employment
- Sustainable economic growth
- Reducing [[Poverty]]
# Activities
- Provides loans to member nations
- Fills deficit gaps
- counselling for a country's economic policy
# Failings
- Too much or too little intervention
- Certain countries become dependent on IMF loans
