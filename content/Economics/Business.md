A profit-driven organization that provides goods and services that customers want.
Traits:
- You can do whatever you want with the profit
- Intention is to generate profit
- Provides goods and services
# Power of Businesses
300 years ago the world was controlled by the state,
600 years ago, we liked in a feudalist world controlled by religion.
Today, businesses control the world. [[SNC-Lavalin Affair]] proves this.
# Business Characteristics
1. Organized Effort - 1 or more people put in time and effort into the business
2. Business Provides [[Needs]] and [[Wants]] - There is a key need/want that is being tackled and solved
3. Businesses Have Customers - There is a demographic and group of people who buy your goods/services
4. Business Must Generate [[Revenue]] - Generated from selling goods/services
5. Businesses Must Incur [[Expense]] - There must be incurred costs
6. Businesses Try to Make [[Profit]] - The [[Profit Motive]] influences business decisions
