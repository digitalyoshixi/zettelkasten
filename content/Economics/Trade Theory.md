# Trade Theory
It is **not cost-efficient to be self-sufficient**. A man should not be building their own house while sewing their clothes and contemplating physics.
Trade incentivizes **specialization** which:
- Increases productivity
- Improves standard of living
# Types of Trade
### Merchandise Trade (Visible Trade)
These are the physical, tangible goods:
- Grown goods,
- Extracted resources
- Manufactured resources
That are sold between countries
### Non-merchandise trade (Invisible Trade)
Intangible goods. Includes:
- Services
- Tourism
- Investment
- Transfers of funds
They are far more difficult to document and measure statistically
