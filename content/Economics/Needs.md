The concept of needs and wants changes continuously.

Needs are [[Felt Deprevation]] you can feel that something is missing.

# Normative Needs
Nowadays, many people see that needs are a [[Normative Facts|Normative]] fact rather than a set and concrete need of survival.
Many people feel deprived if they are just living with the bare necessity to survive.
