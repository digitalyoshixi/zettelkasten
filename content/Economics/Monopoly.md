Only one supplier. They have 100% of the market share.
# Causes
- Legislation. Government allows the business to be a monopoly
- Natural monopoly. A particular business owns 100% of the market
# Examples
- LCBO
# Formal Definition
$MR = MC < P$
- $MR$ is marginal revenue
- $MC$ is marginal cost
- $P$ is price
