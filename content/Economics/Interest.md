Increasing the prices of loans taken from the [[Bank of Canada]].
Calculated by using [[Consumer Price Index|CPI]].
# Formula
$$Inflation=\frac{CPI_{Year2} - CPI_{Year1}}{CPI_{Year1}}$$
# Healthy Inflation Rate
The rate that the [[Bank of Canada]] aims for is ~1%-3%. Preferably 2%
