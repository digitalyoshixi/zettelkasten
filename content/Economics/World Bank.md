International organization dedicated to providing:
- Financing
- Advice
- Research
specifically for **Middle to low income countries**
# Activities
- Supply government with low interest or zero interest credit
- Documents information of all countries
### Human Capital Project
A research effort into how much funding each country spends into their education and healthcare. They created an index of human capital

# Criticisms
- Disregard for environment 
- Disregard for indigenous populations
- Poor evidence base for policies
- Promoting private healthcare
- Creates an environment where lending is rewarded
