# Criticisms
### Not Indicative of Average Wealth
In countries where there is a wealth gap, the gdp per capita could not accurately represent the average wealth per person
### Not Representative of Country Citizens
Most people contributing to GDP are not citizens of the country.
In luxembourg, 44% of the workers don't live therep
