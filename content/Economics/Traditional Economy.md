# System
### What to produce
- What is needed by a family, group, community
- Produce what you consume
### How to produce
- Past methods of production, passed through generation to generation
### For whom to produce
- Used by immediate family, surpluses are traded
### Specialties
- Inheritance based. Very familial, If you are a farmer, your son inherits your farm. If you are a doctor, your daughter is a doctor, etc.
# Pros/Cons
### Advantages
- Predictable and there are set methods
### Disadvantages
- Lack of progress
- Lack of female role (see [[Patriarchy]])
