A factor of production responsible for equipment and money.
# Types of Capital
### Loan Capital
Capital that is given for free
### Debt Capital
Capital given and expected to be repaid
- Lent and must be repaid
- Lender has no say to where their money goes
- Lender gets interest
Obtain this from: [[Banks]]

Overuse of debt capital can lead to:
- Too much debt - assets seized
### Equity Capital
Capital given in exchange for [[Corporation Shares]]
- Money given and never repaid
- Investor gets a say in where the money goes
- Both investor and owner share the money
Obtained by: [[Investors]] through [[Investment Banks]]

Too much equity capital can lead to:
- Lack of business control
- [[Share Dilution]]
