A group of countries acting as one in foreign relations.
Trade barriers are heavily reduced or eliminated between member countries
# Types of Trading Blocs
Free Trade Area > Customs Union > Common Market
Each subsequent stage has less restrictions than the last
### Free Trade Area
- No tariffs between member countries
- Each country has freedom to impose own tariffs with non-member countries
##### Examples
- [[North American Free Trade Agreement|NAFTA]]
### Customs Union
- No tariffs between member countries
- Member countries agree upon shared trade restrictions with non-member countries
##### Examples
- [[Caribbean Community & Common Markey|CARICOM]]
### Common Market
- No tariffs between member countries
- Member countries agree upon shared trade restrictions with non-member countries
- Free movement of capital and labour between member countries
- Centralized body to make decisions for all members
##### Examples
- [[European Union|EU]]
