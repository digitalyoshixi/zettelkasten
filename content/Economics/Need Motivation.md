# Need for Power (N-Pow)
People who want to be influential and need to lead.
They want:
- Personal status
- Prestiege
# Need for Affiliation (N-Aff)
People who need friendly relationships. Motivated by interaction with others.
They want:
- To be liked
- Held in high regard
- Team players
# Need for Achievement (N-Ach)
People who want to achieve challenging goals.
They want to:
- Accomplish things
