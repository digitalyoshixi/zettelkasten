# Causes
### Generational Entrepreneurship
Most entrepreneurs are single children of other entrepreneur parents.
It can be inherited genetically, vocationally or economically.
### Immigrant Families
Immigrant families who are willing to take the risk to move to a different country are likely to start businesses of their own.
