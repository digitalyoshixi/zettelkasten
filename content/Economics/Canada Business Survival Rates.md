# Starting Businesses
Canada is a liberal market economy.
Anyone can start a business
- No fees
- Few regulations
- No government permission
It is the 3rd easiest place in the world to start a business.
- It is best to start your business in canada then move to the US when your business picks up
### Business Survival Rates
1. 85% of businesses survive first year
2. 70% of businesses survive 2nd year
3. 62% of businesses survive 3rd year
4. 53% of businesses survive 4th year
5. 51% of businesses survive 5th year
# Business Risk
Starting a business is risky because of:
1. Customer unpredictability
2. Competition
3. Complexity of managing a business
4. Fundamental need to spend before making a profit
5. Possibility of law suits
