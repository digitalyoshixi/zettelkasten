A phenomena that describes population growth in stages.
Every country is at a single stage.
Technological developments result in lower birth rates overall.
# Process
![[Demographic Transition-20260214202435839.webp]]
### Stage 1 - Early Death
A starting population will have poor living standards
### Stage 2 - Explosive Growth
A population explosion due to technology causing fewer deaths
### Stage 3 - Increase Slow Down
Fewer deaths causes fewer births later on
### Stage 4 - Stable
Low birth and death rates
### Stage 5 - Little Change
Yet to be seen yet
