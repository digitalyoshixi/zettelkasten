# Economic System
### What to produce
- Determined by central authority or big organizations
### How to produce
- Determined by central authority in state's best interest
### For whom to produce
- Determined by state authority
The state acts like a motherland. it provides for all its citizens.
### Specialties
- Individuals obliged to serve state
- Very low amount of consumer goods
- Everybody has a job
# Planned Economy
Countries tried the planned economy, then ditched it.
These include:
- [[Russia]]: 1917-1980. [[Lenin]]
- [[USA]]: 1932-1940. [[Franklin Roosevelt]]
- [[UK]]: 1939-1970. 
- [[China]]: 1948-1980. [[Mao Zedong]]
- [[Cuba]]: 1957-Present
All planned economies failed (except [[Cuba]]).
Most of them transitioned to [[Mixed Market Economy]]
