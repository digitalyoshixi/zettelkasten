- Lots of competitors
- No barriers to entry
- No barriers to exit
- All competitors have small [[Market Share]]
- All must sell more or less at the same price
- They sell similar products often-times they are undifferentiable (no logo, can't remember the business that sold it)
# Examples
- Barber shops
- Food trucks
- Key cutting
# Formal Definition
$MR = MC = P$
- $MR$ is marginal revenue
- $MC$ is marginal cost
- $P$ is price
