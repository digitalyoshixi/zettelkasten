A unbiased organization not tied to any political affiliation *or so they say*
Regulates credit and currency in the best interests of the country.
# Role of the Bank
### Directory of monetary policy
They control interest rates
### Bank of the chartered banks
BMO, CIBC, TD, RBC, Scotiabank. These banks borrow from the bank of canada
### Bank of the Federal Government
The government's budget is stored at the bank of canada
### Issuer of Currency
Where the money is printed. Also validates counterfeit currency

# Policies
### Tight Money Policy
Caused by a shortage of money supply
- High interest rates
- More difficult availability of credit
### Easy Money Policy
Caused by a surplus of money supply
- Low interest rate
- Easy availability of credit
