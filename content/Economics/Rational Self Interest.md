Whatever you think is best for you, you should do it.
Rational self interest does not distinguish between any value of the action. There is no right and wrong. The only right choice is the choice made by oneself
# Wallet Example
If there is a wallet with $1000 dollars and no ID, many of us will take it home.
Economists, will see this as the best action.
