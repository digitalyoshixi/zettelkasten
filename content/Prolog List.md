Lists are [[Linked List]]:
- `[]`: empty list
- `[Head|Tail]`: list with first element head, rest of elements for tail
# Formal Notation
$$
[e_{1} | [e_{2} | [\dots e_{n}]]] =_{\text{abbreviated}} [e_{1},e_{2}, \dots, e_{n}]
$$
